About *Killtime*

*Killtime* is a thrilling, genre-defying sci-fi fantasy novel that will take readers on an unforgettable journey through a universe where magic and science collide. Set in a world governed by quantum-level forces, *Killtime* blends elements of high-stakes adventure, deep philosophical exploration, and complex characters facing their own internal and external battles.

In *Killtime*, magic is not just a mystical force, but a fifth fundamental interaction of the universe—woven into the fabric of reality alongside gravity, electromagnetism, and the nuclear forces. At the heart of the story is Lucas, a young man who discovers that his own powers are linked to an ancient prophecy that could change the fate of his world.

As Lucas and his friends—Mina, Thomas, and a diverse cast of allies—uncover dark secrets, battle factions vying for control, and struggle with their own inner conflicts, they must navigate a reality where magic, technology, and power are in constant flux.
