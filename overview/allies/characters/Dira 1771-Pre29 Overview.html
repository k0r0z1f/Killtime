Birth Year

Dira was born in the year 1739. She is 42 years old in the current year of 1771.

Role/Background

Dira is a repentant member of the Cult of the Sent Ones. Formerly an infiltrator for the Unbound, she now serves as a researcher, aiming to understand the Relics and their significance.

Personality Traits

Dira is nervous and secretive but also earnest and determined. She acts as a steadying force amid uncertainty, displaying intellectual depth and caution in her actions.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

She is currently allied with the Cult of the Sent Ones, working to atone for her past affiliations with the Unbound.

Appearance

Dira is a female dwarf with a long dark braid, slightly tremulous hands, and anxious eyes. Her weary demeanor reflects the toll of her past and present challenges.

Character Arc/Goal

Dira’s goal is to prevent civil war within the Cult of the Sent Ones and protect the group’s cohesion. She is determined to uncover the mysteries of the prophecy and Relics while balancing her personal redemption and the group's mission.

Secret

Dira previously hid critical information about the true nature of the temple, leading to her betrayal of her allies. She is now repenting and using her knowledge to aid the group in understanding the prophecy.

Inventory

Dira carries a notebook that contains her studies on the prophecy and the temple of the Sent Ones, which aids her in contributing to the group’s mission.

Lucas

Dira considers Lucas a friend but struggles with feelings of unworthiness due to her past betrayal.

Mina

Dira admires Mina and hopes that she can forgive her someday for her betrayal.

Thomas

Dira finds Thomas intimidating but values him as a friend and hopes for his forgiveness.

Candice

Dira sees Candice as an acquaintance who is deeply connected to the prophecy. She believes Candice’s mission is crucial to their shared goals.
