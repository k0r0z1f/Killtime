Birth Year

Dira was born in 1739, making her 42 years old in the current year of 1771.

Role/Background

Dira is a former member of the Unbound cult who infiltrated the Cult of the Sent Ones as a spy. She now serves the Cult of the Sent Ones, repenting for her past actions. She was once a guide to the Cult of the Sent Ones but was deeply involved in the Unbound's machinations before spilling everything to the party.

Personality Traits

Dira is nervous and secretive, with a deep sense of guilt for her past involvement with the Unbound cult. She is earnest and determined, often acting as a steadying force within the group. However, she is anxious and conflicted about her role, which makes her cautious, observant, and intellectual. She is determined to prevent the collapse of the Cult of the Sent Ones, but her past weighs heavily on her actions.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Initially a spy for the Unbound cult, Dira has since repented and joined the Cult of the Sent Ones, where she serves as a member of the group. Her primary allegiance now lies with the party, as she works to protect and hold the Cult together.

Appearance

Dira is a female dwarf with long, dark hair braided in a traditional style. She carries herself with an air of weariness, and her hands often tremble, reflecting her inner turmoil. Her eyes show signs of anxiety, and her face is a mixture of exhaustion and determination. Despite the outward signs of wear, there is a quiet strength in her demeanor.

Character Arc/Goal

Dira is grappling with her past actions, particularly her betrayal of the party when she led them into the sewer’s beast lair. She actively works to prevent civil war and protect the Cult of the Sent Ones from collapse. As she seeks redemption, Dira also hopes to uncover the truth about the prophecy and relics while trying to keep the Cult together amidst growing internal strife.

Secret

Dira once hid critical information about the true nature of the temple and had her own motives tied to the Cult of the Unbound. However, she has since confessed everything, hoping that the party will forgive her for her past mistakes. With both cult leaders imprisoned, Dira is now using her knowledge of the prophecy to assist the party in navigating the unfolding events.

Combat Training

Combat training for Dira is not specified, though her background suggests she may have some defensive skills, particularly in knowledge of the Cult of the Sent Ones and the Temple.

Inventory

Dira carries a notebook with detailed notes on the prophecy and the Temple of the Sent Ones. This knowledge is crucial in understanding the larger events surrounding the Cult of the Sent Ones and their eventual fate.
