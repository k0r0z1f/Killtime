Birth Year

Candice was born in 1721, making her 49 years old as of the current timeline.

Role/Background

Candice is a member of the Nythari tribe and serves as a warrior, Seeker, and Messenger. She is an apprentice to Ceylan, tasked with guiding Lucas and Mina to the Watcher and playing an integral role in the prophecy. Her deep connection to her lineage adds complexity to her mission. Additionally, she is responsible for ensuring the group’s survival by securing resources and maintaining morale during challenging moments.

Personality Traits

She exhibits traits such as agility, confidence, determination, protectiveness, insightfulness, and strong will. Candice is also reflective and resourceful, showing leadership qualities that stabilize the group amidst challenges. Her reserved demeanor hides a strong emotional core, making her both compassionate and steady in times of crisis.

Abilities/Magic

Candice is an expert in survival, climbing, stealth, archery, and the use of short blades. She has a strange entity present in her mind, which is tied to her connection with Ceylan and the Watcher. Despite this presence, it does not interfere with her ability to lead or perform her duties effectively.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Candice is aligned with the Watcher, the Nythari Tribe, and Queen Aphyrosia. She also receives temporary protection from the Elven Council of Haliriel. She often consults trusted allies like Eryndor for updated information, relying on their wisdom and insights to navigate her mission effectively.

Appearance

She has an athletic build, bright amber eyes, and long brown-threaded hair. Her appearance reflects her pride in Nythari culture and her readiness for climbing and survival tasks. The flush of her cheeks from the cold and exertion emphasizes her connection to the harsh environments she often navigates.

Character Arc/Goal

Her primary goal is to fulfill her mission of guiding Lucas and Mina to the Watcher, navigating her role in the prophecy, and ensuring the group’s survival through dangerous terrain. She is driven by a deep belief in the prophecy and her role within it, despite her internal conflicts about returning home after years away. Additionally, she strives to uncover vital truths about the Children of Hybris and their connection to the Watcher, ensuring the group's safety through her leadership and strategic thinking.

Secret

Candice harbors a chaotic presence in her mind, possibly linked to Ceylan. This presence does not overtly affect her but is tied to her mission and role in the prophecy. Her dedication to understanding this presence fuels her determination to consult the Watcher for guidance.

Combat Training

She is highly skilled in survival techniques, climbing, stealth, archery, and blow darts. Her combat proficiency makes her a vital member of the group, capable of defending herself and others against threats. Her agility and precision in combat reflect her extensive training and natural aptitude for survival.

Inventory

Her inventory includes a pouch of iridescent stones, blow darts (4 poison darts and 1 normal dart), climbing gear, Nythari survival equipment, a blanket, and a special pass from the Elven Council. Additionally, she carries a small stone fragment from the Nythari ruins, symbolizing her deep connection to her tribe and heritage.

Lucas

Candice has known Lucas for 4 days. She regards him as an acquaintance but blindly trusts him as a Child of Hybris, recognizing the proof of his powers and his significance to the prophecy.

Mina

Candice has known Mina for 4 days. She considers Mina an acquaintance and places blind trust in her as a Child of Hybris, fully aware of Mina’s importance in fulfilling the prophecy.

Thomas

Candice has known Thomas for 4 days. She respects his imposing presence and recognizes his protective and strategic nature, appreciating his value to the group and their shared mission.

Dira

Candice has known Dira for 4 days. She is aware of Dira’s cautiousness and desire for redemption and regards her as intelligent, acknowledging her contributions to the group’s endeavors.
