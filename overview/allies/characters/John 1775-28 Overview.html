Birth Year

John was born in 1728, making him 48 years old in the current year of 1775.

Role/Background

John is the captain of the *Starlight Voyager*, serving as a mentor, transporter, and combat specialist. He is entrusted with the responsibility of guiding and protecting a 'messiah' figure through perilous situations, especially on the dangerous world of Vardis, while managing strategic leadership and survival tasks.

Personality Traits

John is wise, protective, and patient. He has a strategic and calculating mind, approaching tense situations with calm diplomacy. While he values survival and pragmatic alliances, John focuses on earning trust and leadership, never freely giving it.

Abilities/Magic

John is a skilled combatant, navigator, and survivalist. His tactical mind enables him to lead in complex situations, while his cultural understanding and negotiation skills help maintain key alliances and steer his group through dangerous encounters.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

As the leader of the group, John fosters strategic alliances to ensure the mission's success. He is deeply knowledgeable about the Empire’s increasing threat and works to build pragmatic alliances to protect those under his charge.

Appearance

John is typically seen wearing a leather hat pulled low over his face, giving him a mysterious and shadowed presence. His calm, steady expression complements his authoritative, combat-ready demeanor, fitting his role as both protector and strategist.

Character Arc/Goal

John’s goal is to ensure the safety of the 'messiah' figure, navigating dangerous missions while maintaining control under the ever-looming threat of Imperial forces. His arc revolves around survival, leadership, and forging necessary alliances to safeguard his group’s success and freedom.

Secret

John harbors deep knowledge and strategic alliances that remain hidden. While his public role focuses on survival and leadership, he quietly works on long-term strategies, aware of the Empire’s dangerous reach and prepared for any eventuality. His true objectives and depth of knowledge shape his calculated decisions.
