Role/Background

Dira is an observing and participating member of the group. She is a researcher, involved in experiments with Relics, seeking to understand their significance and connection to the greater world.

Personality Traits

Dira is thoughtful, observant, and somewhat cautious. She tends to be hesitant but also diplomatic and intelligent. Her anxiety is tempered by a reassuring and quiet demeanor, though she can be weary from the weight of her thoughts.
