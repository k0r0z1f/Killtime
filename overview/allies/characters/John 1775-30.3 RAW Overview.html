Birth Year

John was born in 1775. His current age is 50 years old.

Role/Background

John is part of a group traveling with Lucas, Erika, and Kael, and is possibly affiliated with the Resistance's mission. He plays a key role in leading the group through dangerous sewers and tunnels, using an old map. His primary task is to protect Lucas, coordinate actions during confrontations, and ensure the safe travel of the group to the Resistance base.

Personality Traits

John is calm and steady, always acting protectively towards Lucas and the group. While pragmatic and direct, he sometimes shows frustration under pressure. He is responsible, strategic, and deeply concerned for the safety of his companions. Despite the mounting pressure of the mission, John maintains focus, relying on finely-tuned instincts and a cautious, stoic demeanor.

Abilities/Magic

John is highly skilled with both a pistol and blade, and has extensive experience in firearms. He is also well-versed in navigating hazardous conditions, making him an invaluable asset during their travels.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

John is allied with the Resistance and his fellow travelers: Erika, Kael, and Lucas. Together, they are working towards a shared mission to resist the Empire's forces.

Appearance

John's appearance is rugged, wearing a leather hat that shields his face and gloves for protection. His weathered look speaks of years of survival, and he carries a blade and pistol, always prepared for any encounter.

Character Arc/Goal

John's ultimate goal is to protect Lucas and guide the group through perilous terrain. He seeks to avoid detection by the Empire's patrols while completing his mission, and ultimately hopes to aid the Resistance's cause and ensure the safety of his group.

Secret

John’s secret remains unknown, adding an element of mystery to his character.

Combat Training

John is highly proficient with firearms and blades. He also has demonstrated competence in navigating hazardous environments, showcasing his ability to survive dangerous situations.

Inventory

John carries an old, faded map of the area, a pistol, a blade, leather hat, gloves, and a hidden second pistol. These items are essential for his survival and protection during the journey.
