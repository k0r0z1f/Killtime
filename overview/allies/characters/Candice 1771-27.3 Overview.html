Birth Year

Candice was born in 1721 and is 49 years old as of the current year, 1771.

Role/Background

Candice is a member of the Nythari tribe, a skilled warrior and Seeker. She serves as an apprentice to Ceylan and has been sent on a mission to bring Lucas and Mina to the Watcher. Her role is part of a larger game involving the Elven Council, and she is integral to the unfolding prophecy.

Personality Traits

Candice is agile, confident, and curious. She is protective of nature, introspective, and loyal. Despite being determined and composed under pressure, she feels torn and internally conflicted about her mission, which burdens her. She remains insightful, quiet, and firm, yet sometimes frustrated at the dismissal of the Watcher.

Abilities/Magic

Candice is an expert in survival, climbing, stealth, archery, blow darts, and short blades. She possesses a chaotic presence she struggles to control, possibly tied to Ceylan. Her knowledge of the Watcher is vast, and she believes it plays a key role in the success of her mission.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Candice is aligned with the Nythari Tribe and temporarily under the protection of the Elven Council. She also shares an alliance with the Watcher, and is part of a faction within the Nythari Tribe that deeply values the guidance of the Watcher.

Appearance

Candice has an athletic build and striking amber eyes. Her long, brown treaded hair and elegant demeanor reflect the pride she feels for the Nythari culture. Her presence is firm and captivating, embodying her connection to her tribe and mission.

Character Arc/Goal

Candice’s goal is to fulfill her mission, ensuring Lucas and Mina’s safe return to the Watcher. As she navigates her role in the prophecy, she is dedicated to uncovering vital truths about it and helping the group. Her internal conflict about the prophecy and her role weighs heavily on her decisions.

Secret

Candice believes in the prophecy regarding Lucas and Mina, and her mission is closely tied to it. She also harbors a chaotic presence that lies dormant in her mind, possibly linked to Ceylan’s influence.

Combat Training

Candice is highly skilled in combat, particularly in survival, climbing, stealth, archery, blow darts, and short blades. These skills make her a formidable opponent in any environment.

Inventory

Candice carries a pouch of iridescent stones (currency from Derek), a small stone fragment from Nythari ruins, a sarbacane, four poison darts, one normal dart, Nythari survival gear, and a special pass granted by the Elven Council.
