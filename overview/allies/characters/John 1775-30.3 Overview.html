Birth Year

John was born in 1728, and as of the current date, he is 48 years old.

Role/Background

John serves as the captain of the *Starlight Voyager*, a mentor figure, and protector. He is responsible for guiding Lucas, a 'messiah' figure, through dangerous situations, including navigating Vardis and dealing with threats from the Empire. John also leads a group that includes Erika and Kael, aligning himself with the Resistance’s mission.

Personality Traits

John is wise, protective, patient, and strategic. He can be calculating and has a sharp sense of humor. Despite his calm, steady demeanor, he feels burdened by his mission and shows an edge of unease. He is responsible and deeply concerned for Lucas’ well-being.

Abilities/Magic

John is highly skilled in combat, navigation, survival, and strategic leadership. He is proficient with both a pistol and blade and can handle delicate negotiations while managing complex alliances.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

John is the leader of the group and has formed strategic alliances with the Resistance and his companions, Erika, Kael, and Lucas. He values pragmatic alliances that ensure the safety of his mission.

Appearance

John wears a leather hat pulled low over his face, gloves, and maintains a practical, combat-ready appearance. His weathered face and steady expression emphasize his strategic and authoritative nature.

Character Arc/Goal

John’s primary goal is to protect Lucas and ensure the group’s safety while navigating through dangerous terrains. His mission is focused on survival, guiding the group through perilous situations, avoiding detection from the Empire, and supporting the larger objectives of the Resistance.

Secret

John harbors a deep knowledge of survival and strategic alliances. While publicly serving as a protector and leader, he is always planning for both immediate survival and the long-term success of his group, all while keeping his true motives closely guarded.

Combat Training

John is proficient in firearms and blades and has received extensive training in navigating hazardous environments and handling himself in combat situations.

Inventory

John carries an old, faded map, a pistol, a blade, a leather hat, gloves, and a hidden pistol. These items support his role as a protector and strategist.
