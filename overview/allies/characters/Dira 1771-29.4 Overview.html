Birth Year

Dira was born in 1739, making her 42 years old.

Role/Background

Dira is a member of the adventuring group and assists with camp duties, preparing remedies, and documenting events. She is a former guide to the Cult of the Sent Ones, where she worked as an infiltrating Unbound spy but has since devoted herself to the Sent Ones in repentance. Her current research focuses on relics and their significance.

Personality Traits

She is nervous and anxious but serves as a steadying force during uncertainty. Dira is earnest, cautious, thoughtful, and diplomatic, always ready to respond calmly in a crisis.

Abilities/Magic

Dira is skilled in herbalism, particularly in preparing remedies to support her group.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

She is aligned with the Cult of the Sent Ones and is an integral part of her adventuring group.

Appearance

Dira is a small female dwarf with long, dark braided hair, anxious eyes, a pale face, and slightly tremulous hands. She often clutches her notebook tightly, her demeanor reflecting her cautious and weary nature.

Character Arc/Goal

Dira’s internal conflict centers around her fear of the Cult’s collapse. She works tirelessly to prevent civil war, protect the Sent Ones, and understand the prophecy and relics. She is determined to assist her group during crises and ensure their well-being while navigating the delicate balance between her mission and the Watcher’s insights.

Secret

Dira was once an Unbound spy who infiltrated the Cult of the Sent Ones. Now, she is repenting after revealing crucial information about the temple's true nature. She carries a lingering fear that the relics hold a dangerous power and harbors knowledge about them.

Combat Training

She has basic combat training with a small blade, allowing her to react under pressure.

Inventory

Dira carries a notebook documenting her studies of the prophecy and the temple, a satchel with herbs, a small blade, and a rope.

Lucas

Lucas is a friend Dira has known for less than a month. She harbors a fear that her betrayal makes her unworthy of his friendship.

Mina

Mina is a friend whom Dira admires deeply. Dira hopes Mina can forgive her someday for her past actions.

Thomas

Thomas is a friend Dira has known for less than a month. She finds him intimidating but hopes he will forgive her as well.

Candice

Candice is an acquaintance Dira has known for four days. Dira believes Candice is connected to the prophecy and plays an important role in their mission.
