Birth Year

Candice was born in the year 1721. Currently, the date is 03-06-1771 (dd/mm/yyyy), making her 49 years old. She has spent much of her life in the Nythari tribe and has been deeply involved in its traditions and missions since childhood.

Role & Background

Candice is a member of the Nythari tribe, serving as a Nythari warrior, Seeker & Messenger. She is an apprentice to Ceylan and was tasked with finding Lucas and Mina to bring them to the Watcher. Her role places her at the center of a larger plan devised by the Elven Council, with the prophecy guiding her steps. While she believes the Watcher’s guidance is essential, she remains cautious about the mission's inherent risks and the true motives of those involved. She also seeks to learn more about the Children of Hybris and the disciples tied to this prophecy. Candice has been on this mission away from her village for 33 days.

Personality Traits

Candice is agile, confident, and curious. She is deeply protective of nature, determined, introspective, and loyal. Yet she is also torn and quietly wary, as she grapples with internal conflict about her mission. Under pressure, she remains composed, firm, and sincere. Her curiosity and openness extend to her views on the Watcher, and she holds a reflective, almost awe-inspired reverence for its guidance. Occasionally, she grows frustrated with those who dismiss the Watcher’s importance, feeling the urgency of her duty to see Lucas and Mina’s involvement through. She can be anxious and emotional when she senses the mission is in jeopardy.

Abilities / Magic

She is an expert in survival, climbing, stealth, archery, and blow darts, as well as short blades. There is also a chaotic presence within her—something she struggles to control—connected to her mentor, Ceylan, which hints at deeper magical ties. This presence underscores her knowledge of the Watcher and the significance of its power.

Alliances / Factions

Candice operates under the temporary protection of the Elven Council, aligning herself with the Watcher, and remains a loyal member of the Nythari Tribe. The tribe’s traditions and reverence for natural harmony heavily influence her decisions. Despite the Elven Council's involvement, Candice's personal loyalty to the Nythari people and the Watcher stands above all else.

Appearance

Candice has an athletic build and bright amber eyes that reflect her unwavering determination. Her long, brown-threaded hair is emblematic of her Nythari roots, often woven in practical styles befitting her role as a warrior. She carries herself with a certain elegance and exudes a striking aura, influenced by her pride in Nythari culture and her accomplished skill set.

Character Arc / Goal

Candice’s primary goal is to fulfill her mission and ensure Lucas and Mina’s return to the Watcher. She believes that consulting the Watcher is crucial for the mission’s success. Simultaneously, she wrestles with the prophecy and her part in it—she fears what it could demand of her, yet remains committed to seeing it through. She intends to guide the group to the Watcher and uncover vital truths about the Children of Hybris and the disciples. Another source of internal conflict is the thought of returning home after many years away—she’s uncertain about how that reunion might unfold.

Secret

Candice holds a deep belief in the prophecy concerning Lucas and Mina. She is convinced her mission is inevitably tied to it. Within her mind, she carries a chaotic presence that lies dormant, possibly connected directly to Ceylan. This presence is both a source of power and a point of tension, fueling her drive while leaving her anxious about the cost.

Combat Training

She has honed her survival skills, including stealth, climbing, archery, and the use of blow darts and short blades. Though her training is formidable, she must remain mindful of the chaotic force within her, which can complicate her control and judgment in high-stress situations.

Inventory

Candice carries a pouch of iridescent stones, which serve as currency known as Derek’s currency. She has a small stone fragment from the Nythari ruins, a sarbacane (blowpipe), and multiple darts (four poison darts and one normal dart). Additionally, she is outfitted with Nythari survival gear and a special pass granted by the Elven Council, allowing her relative freedom of movement across the lands under their domain.

Relations (Lucas, Mina, Thomas, Dira)

Candice has known Lucas, Mina, Thomas, and Dira for only two days. As they appear to be the Children of Hybris, Candice trusts them—or at least feels compelled to place faith in them—given the prophecy. She believes Lucas and Mina’s powers confirm the importance of her mission. With Thomas and Dira, she remains mostly an acquaintance, but she does not underestimate their roles in the unfolding events. The fleeting nature of their shared journey is overshadowed by the significance of the prophecy, uniting them under a common cause.
