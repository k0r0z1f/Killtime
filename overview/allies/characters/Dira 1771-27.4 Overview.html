Birth Year

Dira was born in 1739, making her 42 years old in the current year of 1771.

Role/Background

Dira is a devoted member of the Cult of the Sent Ones, having once infiltrated the Unbound cult as a spy. Now, she repents for her past actions and plays a vital role in the Cult, focusing on research related to Relics and the group's broader mission.

Personality Traits

Dira is nervous and secretive but shows earnest determination in her actions. She is a steadying force in moments of uncertainty, intelligent, diplomatic, and cautious. She can be thoughtful and intellectual, although her anxiety often holds her back. Dira is a reserved but reliable presence, balancing her quiet nature with moments of assertiveness when necessary.

Abilities/Magic

No magical abilities are explicitly mentioned. Dira's strength lies in her knowledge of the prophecy and her deep understanding of the Relics' significance.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Dira is a former member of the Unbound cult and now seeks redemption by aligning herself with the Cult of the Sent Ones. She has become an active participant in their research and operations, offering her expertise while seeking forgiveness for her past actions.

Appearance

Dira is a female dwarf with long, dark braided hair. Her hands often tremble slightly, and her anxious eyes reflect both a youthful and weary soul. She appears somewhat younger than her years, worn down by the emotional and physical toll of her journey, yet there is a certain quiet strength about her.

Character Arc/Goal

Dira’s primary goal is to protect the Cult of the Sent Ones from internal collapse and to prevent civil war. Her arc revolves around reconciling her past as a member of the Unbound cult with her present role in the Cult of the Sent Ones. Dira is driven to uncover the mysteries of the prophecy and the Relics, hoping to understand their true significance and their connection to the party’s mission, especially concerning Lucas, Mina, and Thomas. She must also navigate her fears about the potential dangers of the Relics and make difficult choices as she balances the needs of the mission with her knowledge of the Watcher's insights.

Secret

Dira harbors the secret of the true nature of the temple she once guided the party to. She had been hiding crucial information tied to the Unbound cult, and although she revealed everything in a moment of repentance, her past actions remain a source of guilt. Dira fears the Relics are more than they appear and holds some knowledge about their dark potential.

Combat Training

No specific combat training is mentioned in the provided details for Dira.

Inventory

Dira keeps a notebook filled with her studies on the prophecy and the Temple of the Sent Ones. This notebook is a valuable tool in her ongoing research and may hold clues to the mysteries surrounding the Relics.
