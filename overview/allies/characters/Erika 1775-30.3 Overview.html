Birth Year

Erika was born in the year 1719.

Role/Background

Erika is the Commandant of Cleya's army and a key member of the Resistance opposing the Empire. She is part of Lucas's group and frequently navigates the streets of Atraxia, working with the Resistance to take down the Empire.

Personality Traits

Erika is a charismatic leader with a slightly aloof demeanor. She is driven, strong-willed, and commanding, yet deeply empathetic and protective of her people. Her fiery nature often contrasts with her sharp, patient, and steady attitude. Though brave and skilled, she is also wary, cautious, and occasionally frustrated or impatient.

Abilities/Magic

Erika has powerful fire magic and the ability to channel battle memories. Her tendril-like hair can emit light and bristle with energy. She is also highly skilled in blade combat.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Erika is aligned with Cleya, Lucas, and the Resistance, fighting to oppose the Empire. She is also part of Lucas’s group, which includes John and Kael.

Appearance

Erika has short, tendril-like hair that looks like tentacles in water, with faint glowing tips. Her reddish skin indicates her fiery magical heritage. She is tall for her kind but short for a human, with a roundish face and average features. Erika often wears cloaks and hoods to blend into crowds.

Character Arc/Goal

Erika's primary goal is to prove her strength and defend her people. She strives to protect Lucas and ensure the safety of her group while navigating Atraxia and fighting against the Empire. She is determined to complete her missions and survive the conflicts around her.

Secret

When Erika is emotionally affected, her fire magic flickers, which reveals her deep emotional connection to her powers.

Combat Training

Erika is highly trained in blade combat and handling explosives, making her proficient in melee combat.

Inventory

Erika carries a blade, a concealed pouch of explosives, lanterns, and standard Resistance gear as part of her arsenal in combat.
