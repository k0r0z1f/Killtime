Birth Year

1719

Role/Background

Erika serves as the Commandant of Cleya, a strategic leader with a deep connection to her people's survival and future.

Personality Traits

Erika is a charismatic leader, often maintaining a commanding presence. She can come off as slightly aloof but is deeply driven and protective of her people. Despite her strong-willed nature, she demonstrates empathy towards those she leads and fights for.

Abilities/Magic

Erika possesses fire magic, a gift tied to her emotional state. Her abilities also include battle memory channeling, allowing her to recall past tactics and strategies during combat. Her tendril-like hair is a sign of her magical heritage and often reflects her power.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Erika is closely aligned with Cleya and Lucas, sharing a bond of trust and shared goals.

Appearance

Erika’s most striking feature is her tendril-like hair, which signifies her magical lineage. Her hair reacts to her emotions, often flaring with fire magic when she is emotionally affected. Her presence is commanding, and she carries herself with confidence and strength.

Character Arc/Goal

Erika’s goal is to prove her strength and protect her people from the growing threat of the Empire. She strives to defend Cleya, stop the Empire’s encroachment, and find a way to balance her emotions with her magic.

Secret

Erika’s secret lies in her fire magic, which flickers and fluctuates when she is emotionally affected, betraying her inner turmoil. This connection between her emotions and power is something she hides from others, as it reveals a vulnerability she is unwilling to share.
