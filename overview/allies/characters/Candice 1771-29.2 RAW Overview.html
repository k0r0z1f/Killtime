Role/Background

Candice is the leader of the Children of Hybris. She is an experienced guide, intimately familiar with Nythari customs and mountain conditions. Her primary responsibility is to ensure safe passage to the Watcher’s cave. She is also tasked with aiding the Nythari village by responding to the Watcher's call and fulfilling the prophecy entrusted to the Children of Hybris.

Personality Traits

Candice is calm, composed, respectful, and knowledgeable. She remains humble and empathetic despite her leadership role. She is brave, confident, and responsible, determined to see her tasks through. Over time, she becomes even more humbled and steady, showing deep compassion for others. She does, however, exhibit moments of pride and apprehension as she grapples with the enormity of her responsibilities.

Abilities/Magic

While Candice does not wield overt magical powers, her strongest abilities lie in strategic leadership and a profound understanding of Nythari traditions, lore, and the intricate routes throughout the mountains. This knowledge is instrumental in guiding others safely and overcoming natural hazards.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Candice is a key member of the Children of Hybris and maintains an alliance with the Nythari village. She fosters strong cooperative ties, ensuring mutual protection and guiding collaborative efforts to uphold the prophecy.

Appearance

Candice carries herself with a graceful presence. She wears practical attire suited for the jungle environment—light, rugged materials designed for ease of movement. Natural elements, such as subtle leaf motifs or woven accessories, adorn her clothing to reflect her connection to the land and the traditions she represents.

Character Arc/Goal

Candice aims to fulfill the prophecy of the Children of Hybris and protect the Nythari village from emerging threats. Her goal includes leading her group safely to Ceylan’s cave, arranging visits to various villages, and responding to the mysterious Watcher's call. As she progresses, she grows both as a leader and a person, understanding the weight of her mission and finding balance between her sense of duty and compassion.

Combat Training

Candice possesses basic combat training suitable for a leader, giving her the capacity to defend herself and guide others in hostile environments. Though she relies more on strategy, knowledge, and negotiation, she can hold her own if necessary.

Inventory

Candice carries essential climbing gear for navigating mountain terrain. She also keeps a small pouch containing a charm given to her by a villager, a token of goodwill and protection.
