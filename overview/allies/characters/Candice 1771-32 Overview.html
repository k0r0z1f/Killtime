Birth Year

Candice was born in the year 1721. At 49 years old, her age reflects the era she comes from and the extensive experience she brings as a warrior and guide.

Role/Background

Candice is a dedicated member of the Nythari tribe. As a warrior, messenger, and seeker, she has been entrusted with crucial missions including guiding Lucas and Mina to the Watcher—a key figure in a far-reaching prophecy. An apprentice to Ceylan, she has played central roles in major expeditions, such as traversing treacherous mountain paths, exploring mysterious mansions and caverns, and being present during the aftermath of the Beast’s death.

Personality Traits

Candice is agile, confident, and determined. Her personality is marked by a protective instinct and a deep introspection that balances her urgency and occasional anxiety. Resourcefulness and resilience define her as she faces both physical challenges and internal conflicts with quiet strength and leadership.

Abilities/Magic

Skilled in survival techniques, Candice excels at climbing, stealth, and archery. She uses blow darts and short blades effectively, and wields a bow and dagger when needed. An enigmatic mental presence—possibly the influence of her mentor Ceylan—subtly accompanies her abilities without hindering her performance.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Candice is allied with several influential groups. She is a key member of the Nythari Tribe and is temporarily under the protection of the Elven Council of Haliriel. Her mission aligns with the interests of the Watcher and she collaborates with figures like Queen Aphyrosia as part of a larger expedition, ensuring she has the necessary support and resources.

Appearance

With an athletic build and striking bright amber eyes, Candice’s long, brown-threaded hair flows with an elegance that belies her warrior nature. Dressed in attire suited for both combat and climbing, she carries an aura of both cultural pride and readiness for the challenges ahead.

Character Arc/Goal

Candice’s journey is centered on her mission to ensure the safe return of Lucas and Mina to the Watcher. She is determined to uncover hidden truths—ranging from ancient relics and mysterious cave changes to the enigmatic door that guards long-forgotten secrets—while grappling with her own inner conflicts and the transformative impact of her mentor’s legacy.

Secret

Deep within, Candice harbors a secret belief in the prophecy surrounding Lucas and Mina. Additionally, she is subtly influenced by a chaotic presence linked to Ceylan—a secret that adds layers of internal conflict and mystery to her character.

Combat Training

Trained extensively in survival, climbing, stealth, and archery, Candice is adept in combat. Her proficiency extends to the use of blow darts and short blades, making her an expert in both offense and reconnaissance during dangerous expeditions.

Inventory

Candice carries an assortment of practical and symbolic items: a pouch of iridescent stones used as currency, a small stone fragment from ancient Nythari ruins, and an array of darts (including 4 poison darts and 1 normal dart). Her gear includes essential survival items such as climbing equipment, a blanket, and a special pass from the Elven Council. Additionally, she wields a bow with a final arrow, a dagger in a holster, and uniquely, a darkened metal plate etched with faded Nythari script alongside a crystal shard wrapped in leather.

Lucas

Candice has known Lucas for 5 days. Despite the brief acquaintance, she places a significant trust in him, recognizing him as a Child of Hybris whose latent powers align with the unfolding prophecy.

Mina

Also known for 5 days, Candice trusts Mina blindly as she is identified as a Child of Hybris. Their early connection is marked by an unspoken bond crucial to their shared destiny.

Thomas

Having known Thomas for 5 days, Candice respects his imposing presence and recognizes his strategic and protective qualities, which help balance the dynamics of the group.

Dira

Candice’s acquaintance with Dira, established over 5 days, is characterized by mutual awareness. Dira’s cautious nature and quest for redemption resonate with Candice’s own inner conflicts and aspirations for clarity.
