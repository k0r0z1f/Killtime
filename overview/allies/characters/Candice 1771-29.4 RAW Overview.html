Role/Background

Candice is the leader of the group climbing the Watcher. Her responsibilities include navigation, securing camp necessities like firewood and blankets, and maintaining the fire in shelters.

Personality Traits

Candice is resolute, cautious, courageous, determined, sharp-eyed, confident, focused, practical, resourceful, caring, and slightly anxious in the face of danger.

Abilities/Magic

Her primary skill lies in climbing, making her invaluable for the group’s survival and progression.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Candice leads the expedition group and is aligned with Queen Aphyrosia.

Appearance

She has amber eyes, an athletic build suited for climbing, and cheeks flushed from the cold and physical exertion.

Character Arc/Goal

Candice’s goal is to lead the group safely through the Watcher’s challenges and the Pass of the Hawk, ensuring their survival by managing essential tasks and maintaining group cohesion during crises.

Inventory

Her inventory includes climbing gear, a blanket, and basic survival gear to support the group’s needs.
