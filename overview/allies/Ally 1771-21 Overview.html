Erika

Born in 1719, Erika is the Commandant of Cleya, known for her fiery magical abilities and battle memory channeling. Charismatic but slightly aloof, she is driven by a strong desire to prove her strength, defend her people, and challenge powerful figures like Lucas. Despite her leadership, her past remains a mystery.

John

John, born in 1728, serves as a mentor figure and protector. A patient and wise guide, he specializes in teaching and nurturing young talent. John has vast experience with combat and magic, although his full abilities remain largely untapped. He seeks to protect those at the Institute from hidden dangers, ensuring the survival of the next generation.

Candice

Candice, a skilled martial artist and member of the Nythari tribe, possesses unparalleled jungle survival skills. Her duty to the tribe drives her inquisitive nature, and she seeks to uncover the truth behind Kingston’s disaster. Tall and slender, Candice wears dark braids and leather armor, her eyes glowing with determination. She carries the weight of her mentor’s request as she uncovers the mysteries of her world.
