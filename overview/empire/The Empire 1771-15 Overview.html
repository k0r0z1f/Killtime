Unknown

Role/Background

The Empire is an authoritarian regime that controls vast territories, including planets like Vardis. They enforce order through military might and advanced technology, striving to impose their rule across star systems.

Personality Traits

The Empire is ruthless, calculating, and authoritarian. Its leadership is strategic and expansionist, always seeking to increase its power and suppress any form of resistance.

Abilities/Magic

The Empire possesses technological superiority and relies heavily on military force. They have advanced weaponry and a vast military apparatus. They also suppress magical forces to maintain control and prevent potential threats to their dominance.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Empire's key alliances are with its military forces, various corporate entities, and authoritarian allies, all of whom support their expansionist goals.

Appearance

Imperial soldiers wear advanced, form-fitting armor with distinctive insignia. High-ranking officials wear sophisticated uniforms with powerful emblems. The aesthetic of the Empire emphasizes cold, utilitarian, and efficient designs, projecting an image of strength and control.

Character Arc/Goal

The Empire's goal is to expand its control over key planets and resources, suppress resistance, and maintain dominance through the strength of its military and technological capabilities.

Secret

The Empire is aware of the mystical forces on Vardis and seeks to harness or eliminate them. They are also focused on exploiting the powers of the "messiah" figure, believing that this figure could be a key tool in solidifying their dominance.
