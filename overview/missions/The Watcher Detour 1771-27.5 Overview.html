Mission Name

The Watcher Detour

Primary Objective

To consult the Watcher and uncover vital information about the prophecy, the Children of Hybris, and the relics.

Mission Background

The Watcher is an ancient, sacred site tied to the prophecy involving the Children of Hybris. It holds knowledge that could guide the group in their battle against the Disciple of Air and inform their next steps in the Eastern Continent mission.

Task Objective

The team will travel to the Watcher, spend two days investigating, and seek answers about the relics and their connection to Lucas and Mina.

Special Instructions

Candice will guide the group to the base near her village. The Watcher is a place of ancient power, and there may be others watching. Prepare for potential risks.

Mission Scope

The detour will last two days for investigation, followed by a three-day journey to the Eastern Continent.

Key Players

Lucas, Mina, Thomas, Candice, Dira, Altheris

Critical Details

The Watcher is sacred and dangerous; its knowledge could either help or hinder their mission. Time is limited.

Potential Risks

The group may face threats from unknown parties, including others seeking the Watcher’s knowledge. The Watcher itself may hold dangers tied to its power.

Final Objective

To gain crucial insights into the prophecy, the Children of Hybris, and the relics, and then proceed to the Eastern Continent mission.
