Mission Name

The Call to Kingston

Primary Objective

Find the two children of Hybris in Kingston and bring them back to The Watcher.

Mission Background

- A mysterious explosion in Kingston has been linked to arcane forces and a demonic presence.
- The children of Hybris are identified as key figures who may hold the power to protect their world.
- The Empire, an ancient and dangerous force, may be seeking these children for unknown purposes.

Task Objective

1. Travel to Kingston.
2. Locate the two children of Hybris.
3. Return them to The Watcher.

Special Instructions

- Do not bring back the *Empire’s Champion*, a figure tied to the Empire who poses an even greater danger.
- The children of Hybris are linked to Hybris's magic and are central to preventing an impending threat.

Mission Scope

- Kingston, a distant city known for its recent explosion.
- The journey will likely take Candice beyond her homeland and into unknown territories.
- The danger involves both external threats (The Empire) and internal dangers (the unknown nature of the children).

Key Players

- **Candice**: The protagonist tasked with the mission.
- **Ceylan**: The mentor, guiding Candice and providing cryptic warnings.
- **The Children of Hybris**: The two unknown figures whose presence caused the explosion in Kingston. They might not be children as they are referred to metaphorically.
- **The Empire's Champion**: A dangerous individual tied to the Empire that Candice must avoid.

Critical Details

- The explosion in Kingston has left a demonic presence, suggesting a powerful magical force at play.
- Candice is expected to find the children without specific descriptions but will recognize them through an innate feeling (which is untrue, but even Ceylan believes this to be true).
- The stakes are high: the safety of The Watcher, the jungle, and the wider world depend on the success of the mission.

Potential Risks

- The children may not be fully aware of their significance or powers.
- The Empire may be actively searching for them, complicating Candice's task.
- Candice is entering unfamiliar territory, where both magical and political dangers abound.

Final Objective

- Safely return the children to The Watcher, avoiding the Empire’s interference, and uncover the role they play in the greater conflict involving Hybris.
