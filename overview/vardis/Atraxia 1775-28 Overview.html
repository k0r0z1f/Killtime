Population

The population of Atraxia is over 15 million, mainly consisting of Imperial settlers, with a significant indigenous minority. The Empire has exerted control over the city, but there is still a strong resistance presence from the native tribes.

Appearance

Atraxia is a sprawling metropolis with towering obsidian and steel spires, alongside crumbling Vardisian ruins. The city’s skyline blends imperial technology with ancient mysticism, making it an awe-inspiring yet ominous sight. The suburbs stretch for hundreds of miles, merging with the jungle.

Energy & Atmosphere

The city is powered by the **Aurion Nexus**, an energy core that taps into the planet’s mystical forces, giving the atmosphere an ethereal glow. However, there is an underlying sense of looming oppression, as the Empire's presence weighs heavily on the city.

Architecture

The architecture in Atraxia is a fusion of high-tech Imperial design and ancient tribal stonework. The city features towering spires, monolithic walls, and massive citadels, with tribal carvings interwoven into the imperial structures, symbolizing the planet’s contested history.

Symbolism & Carvings

The Empire’s insignia is carved into many of the city’s public buildings, while tribal symbols intertwine in areas inhabited by the indigenous tribes. These symbols represent the merging and clashing of cultures on Vardis.

Defenses

Atraxia is heavily defended with automated sentries, energy barriers, and elite Imperial guards. The **Aurion Nexus** serves as a key defensive structure, controlling access to the planet’s mystical power and providing an additional layer of protection.

Legends & Lore

Local legends speak of the **First Collapse**, when an ancient empire on Vardis fell due to overuse of the Nexus. It is said that the remnants of this civilization’s power still linger beneath the city, waiting to be rediscovered.

Hidden Dangers

The **Aurion Nexus** is unstable, and manipulating its energy could trigger dangerous magical phenomena. Additionally, there are rumors of rogue factions operating in secret underground tunnels, smuggling weapons and magical artifacts.

Link to the Party

Atraxia plays a crucial role in the party's journey. The city serves as a focal point for political, military, and magical conflicts, either as a destination or as a source of critical information and resources.

Surrounding Terrain

Atraxia is surrounded by a mix of dense urban sprawl and untamed wilderness. The ancient Vardisian ruins are slowly being overtaken by the jungle, creating a contrast between nature and the technological empire.

Weather

The weather around Atraxia is erratic, marked by strange storms, strange lights, and magnetic disturbances. These phenomena are believed to be linked to the instability of the **Aurion Nexus** and the planet’s mystical forces.

Recent Developments

Recently, the Empire has expanded its technological and military influence over Atraxia, strengthening its control of the city. Resistance factions have become more active, creating tension as they work against the Imperial occupation.
