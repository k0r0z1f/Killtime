Role/Background

The Tribal Guards are a group of warriors sworn to defend their tribe from external threats, particularly the Empire, which has caused them great harm in the past. They rely on their deep cultural connection to nature and tradition in both their defensive tactics and everyday life.

Personality Traits

The Tribal Guards are initially distrustful and aggressive, due to their past experiences with the Empire. However, they are deeply protective of their people, resilient in the face of danger, and highly wary of outsiders. Once trust is earned, they become more cooperative.

Abilities/Magic

Skilled in combat using bone, sinew, and natural materials, the Tribal Guards are masters of guerrilla warfare and stealth. They are adept at using spears, jagged blades, and crossbows, often with unique tribal designs that carry cultural significance.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Tribal Guards are primarily loyal to their own tribe, with a profound disdain for the Empire, which has caused them immense suffering in the past. Their allegiance lies in the defense of their people and maintaining their cultural practices.

Appearance

The chief of the Tribal Guards is weathered, with a hardened appearance, and wears a cloak adorned with tribal symbols. The guards themselves wear tribal attire made from natural materials that are designed for combat. Their weapons and armor are crafted from materials found in their environment, creating a distinct, nature-based aesthetic.

Character Arc/Goal

The primary goal of the Tribal Guards is to protect their people and maintain their cultural integrity. Their experiences with the Empire have made them distrustful, but they are open to aiding those who prove themselves worthy. They may also secretly seek redemption or revenge for past traumas caused by the Empire's actions.

Secret

The chief may have a personal vendetta against the Empire, driven by a deep desire for revenge or redemption concerning the past trauma inflicted on the tribe.

Combat Training

The Tribal Guards are highly trained in guerrilla warfare, utilizing their knowledge of the land to remain hidden and strike when the time is right. They are skilled in using natural materials for weapons and are adept at stealthy tactics in combat.

Inventory

The Tribal Guards carry weapons crafted from natural materials, including spears, jagged blades, crossbows, and knives. Their weapons are often inlaid with tribal designs and hold cultural significance. Some guards may also carry ceremonial tools or tribal charms.
