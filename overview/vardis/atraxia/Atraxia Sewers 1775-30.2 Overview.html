Location Name

The location is known as the Atraxia Sewers, a critical point of operation for the Resistance.

Population

The Atraxia Sewers are home to Resistance members, Imperial soldiers, patrols, and drones traversing the area.

Appearance

The sewers are damp, labyrinthine tunnels featuring rusted grates and machinery, dark stone walls slick with grime, and an oppressive atmosphere. Glistening brickwork, bioluminescent fungi, and moss illumination contribute to the eerie surroundings. Forgotten Imperial equipment and flickering lanterns add to the bleak aesthetics.

Energy & Atmosphere

The energy of the Atraxia Sewers is oppressive, tense, and foreboding, filled with the stench of decay and damp earth. It is a suffocating, eerie environment that fills individuals with a sense of fear and unease.

Architecture

The architecture consists of narrow, ancient stone tunnels with rusted grates as entrances. It forms a maze-like network of junctions, chambers, corridors, and ladders, creating a disorienting and claustrophobic system.

Symbolism & Carvings

The Resistance has left markers and symbols etched into the walls of the sewers, often hidden beneath grime to remain unnoticed by Imperial forces.

Defenses

Imperial patrols, soldiers with rifles, maintenance drones, and hidden patrols represent significant threats in the sewers. There is a constant risk of ambushes.

Hidden Dangers

The Atraxia Sewers hold many latent dangers, including Imperial ambushes, traps, unstable tunnels, broadcasting drones, sensory overload, and dark forces linked to Lucas’s powers. A constant feeling of being watched and whispers in the dark heighten the sense of dread.

Link to the Party

The party is currently navigating the Atraxia Sewers as part of a Resistance mission. They are carrying the unconscious Lucas and using the sewers to avoid Imperial detection, heading toward the outer sectors.

Surrounding Terrain

The sewers are situated beneath the city of Atraxia, surrounded by narrow streets, alleys, and upper levels, forming a vast underground network.

Weather

The underground conditions are damp, decaying, and oppressive, while above ground, a cooler breeze provides a sharp contrast to the sewers’ stifling atmosphere.

Recent Developments

Due to outdated maps and increased Imperial patrols, the Resistance now uses the sewers as the safest route to the outer sectors. The party is engaged in an ongoing mission against the Empire, planning their escape through a ladder system.
