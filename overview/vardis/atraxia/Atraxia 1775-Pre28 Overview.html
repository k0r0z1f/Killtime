Population

Over 15 million inhabitants, predominantly Imperial settlers, with a significant indigenous minority who live in marginalized districts.

Appearance

A sprawling city of towering obsidian and steel spires that dominate the skyline, surrounded by crumbling ruins and suburbs stretching for hundreds of miles.

Energy & Atmosphere

The city draws power from the Aurion Nexus, giving the atmosphere an ethereal glow, but also instilling a sense of heavy oppression due to constant surveillance and control.

Architecture

The city blends high-tech Imperial designs with ancient tribal stonework. Gigantic spires and monolithic walls tower above districts where tribal carvings are integrated into Imperial structures.

Symbolism & Carvings

Buildings feature intertwined tribal symbols and Imperial insignias, reflecting the power struggle between the Empire and indigenous peoples.

Defenses

The city is heavily defended with a strong military presence, automated sentries, energy barriers, and elite guards. The Aurion Nexus itself serves as both a power source and a defensive asset.

Legends & Lore

Legends tell of the First Collapse, where the overuse of the Nexus destroyed an ancient empire. It is believed that remnants of this civilization’s power still lie beneath the city.

Hidden Dangers

The unstable Aurion Nexus poses a threat of dangerous magical phenomena. Rogue factions use hidden tunnels to smuggle weapons and artifacts, adding to the tension.

Link to the Party

Atraxia is central to the party’s journey, serving as a critical location for political intrigue, military operations, and pivotal events.

Surrounding Terrain

The area around Atraxia consists of dense urban sprawl transitioning into untamed wilderness. Ancient ruins are being overtaken by jungle, showing the clash of nature and technology.

Weather

The Aurion Nexus causes erratic weather, including storms, magnetic disturbances, and strange lights that add to the city's mysterious aura.

Recent Developments

The Empire has increased its military presence and fleet size, tightening its grip on the city. Resistance factions are organizing but remain under immense pressure.
