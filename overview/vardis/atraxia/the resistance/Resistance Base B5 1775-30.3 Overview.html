Location Name

The location is known as Resistance Base B5, a stronghold for the rebels.

Population

The base is home to multiple Resistance fighters, ready to defend against Imperial forces.

Appearance

The structure is a hulking mass of rusted metal, bearing faded insignias of the Resistance. Some remnants of Resistance markings can still be seen etched into the walls, though many have been scraped away over time.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere is tense, with fighters on high alert, their eyes sharp, and weapons always at the ready.

Architecture

The base features warehouse-style architecture with rusted metal walls and remnants of faded insignias from the Resistance’s past activities.

Symbolism & Carvings

Faint traces of Resistance markings and rebellion are etched into the walls, offering a glimpse of the struggles that have taken place within these confines.

Defenses

The base is heavily defended with prepared fighters, stationed at defensive positions, ready to hold off any Imperial forces that may approach.
