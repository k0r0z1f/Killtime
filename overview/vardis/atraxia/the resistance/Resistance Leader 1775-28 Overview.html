Role/Background

The Resistance Leader is a strategic mastermind and experienced tactician who coordinates operations and fights against the Empire's increasing control over Vardis. They are responsible for ensuring the survival of their group while devising methods to disrupt the Empire's hold on the planet.

Personality

The Resistance Leader is grim, serious, and highly pragmatic. They are driven by a deep sense of responsibility toward their group, often appearing disillusioned but unwavering in their mission to protect their people. They have little patience for indecision and focus on immediate threats, which sometimes makes them seem distant or cold.

Abilities/Magic

Highly skilled in leadership, covert operations, and resource management, the Resistance Leader is also proficient with technology, using communication systems to coordinate resistance efforts. They possess vast knowledge of Empire operations, which helps them anticipate and counter enemy movements.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Resistance Leader is allied with the resistance faction on Vardis, based in the city of Atraxis. This faction is engaged in an ongoing struggle against the Empire's encroaching control.

Appearance

The Resistance Leader is middle-aged, with an intense gaze and weathered features that suggest years of hardship. Their clothing is practical and worn, designed for the daily demands of resistance life. Their posture is authoritative, but there’s an underlying air of weariness from the constant pressure of their role.

Character Arc/Goal

The Resistance Leader is primarily focused on continuing the fight against the Empire. Their goal is to protect their group while exposing the Empire’s oppression. However, they are also driven by a personal need for redemption and the fear of failing those they lead. A significant part of their journey is the effort to rescue Lucas' friend, who has been captured by the Empire.

Secret

The Resistance Leader secretly struggles with a fear of losing control over the resistance group. They have a hidden past involving betrayal or regret, which they keep concealed to maintain their authority and command over the faction.

Combat Training

The Resistance Leader has received extensive combat training, including guerrilla tactics, self-defense, and survival skills. Their knowledge of Empire operations is invaluable in understanding the strengths and weaknesses of their enemies, making them a formidable leader in battle.

Inventory

The Resistance Leader carries concealed weapons, communication devices, emergency rations, and tools for covert operations. They also have a map of escape routes and a dossier containing crucial intelligence about the Empire’s movements and plans.
