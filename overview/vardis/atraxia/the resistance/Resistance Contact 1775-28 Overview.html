Role/Background

The Resistance Contact is a former Imperial soldier who defected and now works as a contact for the Resistance. He helps pass along information and supplies, all while trying to avoid detection by the Empire.

Personality Traits

The Resistance Contact is nervous, cautious, and fearful of being caught by the Empire. He is resourceful, but constantly lives under the pressure of detection. He is also greedy and manipulative, often doing whatever it takes to ensure his own survival, even if it means betraying others.

Abilities/Magic

His abilities are limited to basic combat training from his time in the Imperial military. His main strength lies in intelligence gathering, though his proficiency is lacking when it comes to direct combat.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

He is affiliated with the Resistance, though in an informal capacity. His relationship with the group is rooted in necessity, rather than a deep ideological commitment.

Appearance

The Resistance Contact wears tattered remnants of an Imperial uniform. His overall appearance is unkempt and weary, reflecting the stress of his covert operations and constant evasion of the Empire.

Character Arc/Goal

His main goal is to stay alive and continue helping the Resistance. However, as the Empire tightens its grip on the city, he becomes increasingly fearful and desperate. He is one of the first individuals targeted for questioning by Imperial guards, adding urgency to his efforts.

Secret

He once betrayed his comrades during an Imperial crackdown, narrowly escaping with his life. This secret weighs heavily on him and contributes to his paranoia and anxiety.

Combat Training

The Resistance Contact received basic combat training during his time in the Imperial military, but he is not proficient in hand-to-hand combat. His skills are better suited for covert operations and intelligence work.

Inventory

He carries a map leading to the Resistance base, along with a few basic supplies necessary for his survival and communication. These include a few tools, rations, and a communicator to maintain contact with the Resistance.
