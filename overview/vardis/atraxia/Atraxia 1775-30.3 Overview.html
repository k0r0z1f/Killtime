Location Name

Atraxia is the heart of the Empire on Vardis, a sprawling megalopolis with over 15 million people, predominantly Imperial settlers, with a significant indigenous minority.

Population

Atraxia is home to over 15 million people, mainly Imperial settlers, but there is also a significant indigenous minority. It is a crowded city filled with vendors, soldiers, and a mix of general populations, Imperial forces, and Resistance members.

Appearance

Atraxia has towering obsidian and steel spires, symbolizing its technological and militaristic nature, surrounded by crumbling Vardisian ruins. The streets are chaotic and grimy, with narrow alleys and towering spires casting shadows over the city.

Energy & Atmosphere

The city’s atmosphere is influenced by the Aurion Nexus, giving it an otherworldly, mystical glow. It has a tense, oppressive atmosphere filled with decay, conflict, and constant surveillance.

Architecture

Atraxia’s architecture blends high-tech Imperial designs with ancient tribal stonework, creating a labyrinthine urban structure with narrow alleyways and towering spires.

Symbolism & Carvings

Tribal symbols can be found in the indigenous districts, emphasizing the contested history between the Empire and the indigenous people. Resistance markings can be seen scraped away on warehouses.

Defenses

Atraxia is heavily defended with automated sentries, energy barriers, elite Imperial guards, surveillance drones, and patrols. The Aurion Nexus also controls access to the planet's mystical power.

Legends & Lore

The First Collapse, a catastrophic event where a previous empire colony fell due to overuse of the Nexus, remains an important piece of Atraxia's history. There are also rumors of a prophecy involving a messiah, Lucas, who is central to the Resistance.

Hidden Dangers

There are dangerous magical phenomena tied to the instability of the Aurion Nexus, along with rogue factions smuggling weapons and magical artifacts through hidden tunnels beneath the city.

Link to the Party

The Resistance operates from hidden underground tunnels where Lucas, central to the Prophecy, plays a pivotal role. The party is navigating through Atraxia while evading Imperial forces.

Surrounding Terrain

Atraxia is surrounded by dense jungles and barren, ruined clearings, reflecting the environmental damage caused by the Empire's expansion.

Weather

The weather in Atraxia is strange, with magnetic disturbances and odd lights caused by the Aurion Nexus. It’s typically gray, drizzly, and humid, with occasional storms.

Recent Developments

Recently, the Empire has strengthened its military and technological presence. Resistance factions are organizing, but they remain fragmented, and the Empire’s grip on the city is tightening.
