Role/Background

Altheris is a warrior and protector of the Queen, serving as the commandant of the expedition. He is a vigilant member who brings up the rear of the group, constantly scanning for potential threats. His past includes roles as a guard and scout responsible for patrolling and vigilance.

Personality Traits

Altheris is focused, steady, observant, vigilant, serious, concerned, protective, loyal, and quick to act. His personality is defined by his dedication to safeguarding the Queen and the group.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Altheris is loyal to Queen Aphyrosia and is an integral part of the expedition group.

Appearance

Altheris has a larger frame for an elf, with an imposing presence. His sharp eyes and muscular, battle-hardened physique contribute to his formidable appearance.

Character Arc/Goal

Altheris's primary goal is to protect the Queen and the group by monitoring and alerting to threats. He assists in ascending the mountain and defends against dangers like the Minulican and the Beast.

Secret

Altheris witnessed a mysterious, hairy figure watching the camp. He keeps this secret as instructed by the Queen.

Combat Training

Altheris has military training and is skilled in physical combat, fighting, and defense techniques.

Inventory

Altheris carries a fencing sword as part of his standard equipment.
