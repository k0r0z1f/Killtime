Birth Year

The exact birth year of The Relic is unknown. It is an ancient artifact tied to the fates of Lucas, Mina, and Thomas.

Role/Background

The Relic is an ancient and powerful artifact, once dormant, that is closely tied to Lucas and the two other artifacts tied to Mina and Thomas. These artifacts' origins are mysterious and connected to the Sent Ones prophecy, playing a crucial role in the resistance against the Empire.

Personality Traits

The Relic exhibits a chaotic nature, once dormant, it radiates alien and powerful energy. It has a commanding and authoritative presence but also remains steady and unyielding when activated.

Abilities/Magic

The Relic has once dormant energy that, when activated, has the potential to unlock unknown powers within Lucas. It radiates energy that instills resolve in those around it, influencing the resistance's fight against the Empire.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Relic is intricately tied to the Sent Ones prophecy and plays a vital role in supporting the Resistance against the Empire.

Appearance

When fused with Lucas, The Relic takes the form of a dark-blue metallic wristband. In humanoid form, it features a sleek, metallic platinum-blue coating, lacking any facial features, and pulses with an unnatural energy.

Character Arc/Goal

The Relic is crucial to the characters' fates and is activated by Lucas. It helps guide the Resistance in their ongoing fight against the Empire.

Secret

The Relic may be linked to an ancient intelligence or entity, which remains a mystery.
