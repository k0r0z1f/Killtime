Role/Background

Altheris serves as an injured soldier and a key member of a traveling group. He acts as a guide for the party, leveraging his extensive knowledge of the mission and the Queen's involvement. Once a guard and scout responsible for patrolling and vigilance, he remains ever watchful, positioned at the rear to monitor for potential threats. Additionally, his role extends to documenting mysterious events in a mansion, and he maintains a connection with the Elven realms through an advanced technological device.

Personality Traits

Altheris is characterized by his swift and brave nature, combined with a stoic and observant demeanor. His personality reflects composure, respect, and a strong sense of duty. Calm and pragmatic, he remains focused and vigilant, displaying protective loyalty and readiness to act quickly. His reserved nature often leaves his true feelings unreadable.

Abilities/Magic

Altheris is proficient in swordsmanship and is skilled in utilizing advanced hybrid technology. These abilities make him an effective combatant and a resourceful member of his group.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

As the commandant of Haliriel's Defense, Altheris holds a position of authority and maintains close ties with Queen Aphyrosia. His loyalty extends to the expedition group he serves with, and he is implicitly linked to the Elven realms. He is also associated with the Children of Hybris.

Appearance

With a larger frame typical of an elf, Altheris presents an imposing presence. His sharp eyes and muscular, battle-hardened build are complemented by fresh scars that testify to past conflicts. He dresses in practical attire suited for both combat and the rigors of travel.

Character Arc/Goal

Altheris’s journey centers on guiding his party through a complex mission. His goals include ensuring that Ceylan’s wisdom is consulted, protecting the group and the Queen from threats, and playing a crucial role in ascending a mountain. Additionally, he is tasked with confronting a dangerous Beast in combat, aiding in the search for Lucas and Mina, and meticulously documenting the mysterious events unfolding within the mansion.

Secret

In a closely guarded secret, Altheris once observed a mysterious, hairy figure—referred to as the Beast—watching the camp. He has kept this unsettling discovery confidential, adhering strictly to the Queen's instructions.

Combat Training

Altheris is well-versed in military training, having honed his skills in combat and physical defense. His background as a soldier ensures he is capable in a variety of battle scenarios.

Inventory

His gear includes a fencing sword and a long blade that is carried on his back. In addition, he utilizes a small, advanced camera device—hybrid technology sourced from the Elven realms—to assist him in his documentation and mission-related tasks.
