Birth Year

The exact birth year for The Resistance is not provided, as it refers to a group rather than an individual.

Role/Background

The Resistance is a group of underground fighters and rebels who oppose the Empire’s control over Vardis. They operate covertly out of a hidden base located in Atraxia, working tirelessly to overthrow the oppressive regime.

Personality Traits

The members of The Resistance are marked by skepticism and urgency in their actions, driven by a hopeful vision of a free Vardis. They are cautious but determined to achieve their goals, despite internal divisions and the challenges posed by the Empire’s surveillance.

Abilities/Magic

The Resistance is skilled in guerrilla tactics, underground operations, and resourcefulness, using their wits and the environment to fight back against a much stronger enemy.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Resistance opposes the Empire and is connected to Lucas, whose prophecy plays a significant role in their ongoing efforts.

Appearance

The members of The Resistance have varying appearances, often seen in ragged clothing or wearing remnants of Imperial uniforms to blend in while maintaining secrecy.

Character Arc/Goal

The primary goal of The Resistance is to overthrow the Empire’s oppressive control over Vardis, protect their people, and maintain secrecy in a heavily surveilled city. They are concerned about Lucas and the impact his role might have on their movement.

Secret

The Resistance is fragmented and distrustful, especially with the arrival of Lucas. These internal divisions may complicate their mission, but they also make the group more elusive and difficult for the Empire to track.
