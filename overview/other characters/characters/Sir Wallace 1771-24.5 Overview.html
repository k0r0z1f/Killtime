Birth Year

Sir Wallace was born in 1722, having lived through many years of service to the Mina family. His experience spans far beyond traditional household duties, extending into the realms of battle strategy and espionage.

Role/Background

Initially, Sir Wallace served as a loyal butler to Mina's family, but his true purpose was to be a protector. Trained in combat, espionage, and tactical strategy, he now takes an active role in the ongoing battle, particularly in strategizing and protecting Mina. His loyalty to the family is unwavering, and his involvement in the conflict has shifted from passive to active as he navigates the challenges of war.

Personality Traits

Sir Wallace’s character is shaped by his long years of service and his dedication to his duties:

  • He is duty-bound, always placing the well-being of Mina and her family above all else.
  • His discreet nature ensures that he handles sensitive matters with care and confidentiality.
  • He is highly observant, able to assess situations quickly and make decisive actions.
  • Sir Wallace is fiercely loyal to Mina and her family, never faltering in his commitment to their safety and interests.
  • He is an expert strategic thinker, constantly assessing the battlefield and planning accordingly.
  • Despite the challenges of war, Sir Wallace remains a battle-hardened individual who adapts to the changing tides of conflict.

Abilities/Magic

Though not a practitioner of magic, Sir Wallace is an accomplished fighter with a vast range of skills:

  • Advanced combat skills, particularly in close-quarter battles.
  • Expert in espionage, capable of gathering critical intelligence and executing covert missions.
  • A trained assassin with the ability to neutralize threats silently and efficiently.
  • Strategic battlefield leadership, able to coordinate forces and plan maneuvers that turn the tide of battle.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Sir Wallace’s primary allegiance is to Mina and her family. He is aligned with the family estate and has been sent as a protector, maintaining his loyalty to their well-being and strategic interests. His involvement with Mina’s father is key to understanding his deeper role in the larger conflict.

Appearance

Sir Wallace is a well-groomed man, always presentable in formal yet practical attire suitable for combat. He is known for his sharp eyes that miss nothing, and his commanding presence, which earns respect both in the household and on the battlefield. His appearance reflects his dual role as both a servant and protector.

Character Arc/Goal

Sir Wallace’s arc is one of transformation from a behind-the-scenes figure to an active participant in the battle against the Disciple. His primary goal is to protect Mina at all costs, ensuring her safety while also aligning with her growing independence. His tactical expertise is invaluable, and despite recent injuries, he continues to contribute to the battle plan, demonstrating unwavering commitment to the cause and his personal duty to the family.

Secret

While Sir Wallace appears to be just a butler, his true role is far deeper. He was specifically trained and sent as a protector by Mina’s father, with an intricate understanding of espionage, combat, and strategy. His true allegiance is not just to Mina, but to the larger family interests, and he played a crucial role in the strategic planning of the battle against the Disciple. His skills and knowledge have made him indispensable in the family’s survival.
