Birth Year

The Resistance consists of multiple members, and their birth years vary, as the coalition includes individuals from different tribes, backgrounds, and time periods.

Role/Background

The Resistance is a coalition of native tribes, outlaws, and defectors from the Empire who are working together to oppose Imperial control over the planet Vardis. They operate from a hidden base in Atraxia, focused on protecting their land, people, and the mystical forces of Vardis.

Personality Traits

Members of The Resistance are determined and resourceful, driven by a deep sense of urgency and hope to free their people. They are often skeptical of outsiders, particularly concerned about the role and arrival of Lucas. They are pragmatic and cautious, balancing the necessity of secrecy with their mission.

Abilities/Magic

The Resistance members use guerrilla tactics, underground operations, and possess survival skills. Some rare members also have elemental magic, enhancing their ability to disrupt Imperial control over Vardis.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Resistance is made up of the native tribes of Vardis, outlaw groups, and defectors from the Empire. They have formed a fragile alliance to fight back against the Empire's control of the planet.

Appearance

Members of The Resistance have varied appearances, often dressed in ragged clothing or remnants of Imperial uniforms. Many wear camouflage gear, tribal symbols, and elemental tattoos, signifying their connection to the mystical forces of Vardis.

Character Arc/Goal

The goal of The Resistance is to overthrow the Empire's control over Vardis, protect their people, harness the mystical powers of the planet, and prevent outside influences from taking hold. They must operate in secrecy, as the city is heavily surveilled by Imperial forces.

Secret

While outwardly united, The Resistance has fragmented leadership. Some factions secretly negotiate with the Empire or use mystical powers for their own agendas, complicating the group's unity and objectives.
