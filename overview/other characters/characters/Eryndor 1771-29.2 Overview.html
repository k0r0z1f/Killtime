Role/Background

Eryndor is a skilled elf hunter, dedicated to protecting the Nythari village and guiding his people through the treacherous paths of the Watcher mountain.

Personality Traits

Eryndor has a gruff and sharp-tongued demeanor but is deeply protective of his people. He shows a softer side when offering encouragement or in moments of reverence.

Abilities/Magic

As an expert tracker and marksman, Eryndor possesses exceptional survival skills. He can navigate dangerous terrain and utilize his knowledge of the wilderness to aid his tribe and allies.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Eryndor is a steadfast member of the Nythari Tribe, a group deeply connected to the Watcher’s wilderness.

Appearance

Eryndor is tall and lean with weathered features. His long dark hair is tied back, and his sharp brown eyes hint at his years of experience. He often wears a cloak of furs, blending with the forest surroundings.

Character Arc/Goal

Eryndor’s ultimate goal is to protect the Nythari people and safeguard the ancient secrets of the Watcher mountain.

Secret

No explicit secret is provided for Eryndor.

Combat Training

Highly skilled in archery, stealth, hand-to-hand combat, and setting traps, Eryndor is a formidable warrior in both offensive and defensive scenarios.

Inventory

Eryndor carries a longbow with a quiver of enchanted arrows, a hunting knife, essential survival tools, a charm tied to Nythari traditions, and a satchel of dried herbs for medical and ritual purposes.
