The Beast (Character Overview)

Attribute Details
Role/Background Guardian of the lair on the northern cliffs.
Appearance Enormous, bipedal creature with a thick, matted fur coat and imposing, saber-tooth-like fangs.
Character Arc/Goal To protect the lair and its artifacts.

Role/Background

The Beast serves as the steadfast guardian of its lair, strategically positioned on the northern cliffs. Its primary responsibility is to safeguard the lair and the valuable artifacts contained within. This role has defined The Beast's existence, making it a formidable presence in the region.

Appearance

The Beast is an imposing figure, standing on two legs with an enormous stature. Its body is covered in a thick, matted fur coat that provides both protection and camouflage against the rugged cliffs it guards. The creature's most striking features are its saber-tooth-like fangs, which add to its fearsome demeanor and serve as a warning to any who might threaten its domain.

Character Arc/Goal

The Beast's primary goal is to protect the lair and its artifacts from any intruders or threats. This unwavering dedication has made it a solitary figure, deeply connected to its role as a guardian. Throughout its existence, The Beast has faced numerous challenges that test its loyalty and strength, reinforcing its commitment to its duty.
