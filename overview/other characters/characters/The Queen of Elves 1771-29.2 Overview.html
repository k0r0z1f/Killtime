Character

Queen of Elves (Aphyrosia). She is the ruling monarch of Haliriel and acknowledged as the leader of the High Elves Council. Although publicly known by royal titles, her true name “Aphyrosia” remains obscure to most.

Birth Year

929 (842 years old, current date 03-06-1771). Despite her advanced age, she retains a regal grace and an ageless aura, reflecting both her longevity and elven heritage.

Role/Background

Ruler of Haliriel, Queen of Elves, Leader of the High Elves Council, Ruler of the Elven Realm. She presides over political and societal matters within the Elven world, guiding her people through both prosperity and hardship. She also maintains deep knowledge of ancient prophecies and fosters alliances to ensure the safety and flourishing of her realm.

Personality Traits

Commanding, regal, authoritative, wise, diplomatic, protective of her people, calm, warm, calculated, cryptic, serene, composed. Aphyrosia’s demeanor balances warmth and approachability with a mysterious aura, reflecting her centuries of experience, her devotion to her people, and her intrinsic connection to the arcane forces of nature.

Abilities/Magic

Mastery over Air Magic (Nature-based, healing, control over plant life, enhanced senses), arcane knowledge, highly skilled in diplomacy. Through her attunement to nature, she can heal, command the winds, and work closely with the essence of plant life. Her keen senses aid her in both battle and in reading the intentions of others, while her diplomatic prowess has preserved peace among the various elven factions for centuries.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Haliriel (City of Elves), elven royalty, High Elves, involved in ancient prophecies, Elven Realm; allied with Candice’s tribe, deep alliance with the Nythari Tribe. Aphyrosia works tirelessly to maintain unity within her city and the broader Elven Realm. Her alliances, especially with the Nythari Tribe, are founded on mutual respect and the shared goal of preserving elven heritage.

Appearance

Tall, regal and elegant, silver hair in intricate braids, piercing green eyes; crown of leaves and jewels, circlet of entwined leaves and gemstones, robe woven from the essence of the forest with patterns of trees, earth, and air, dignified. She embodies a timeless beauty and a formidable presence, enhanced by attire that symbolizes her deep bond with nature.

Character Arc/Goal

Leading the Elven people with wisdom and strength, navigating political tensions, seeking to protect her people, ensure the prosperity of the elves, and safeguard Haliriel. Guiding Lucas and Mina, establishing alliances for future challenges, guiding Candice, testing her resolve, revealing truths about the prophecy, and consulting Ceylan for wisdom. She upholds her duty of supporting the mission to the Watcher, guiding the group, and uniting diverse factions. Aphyrosia also engages deeply with the Nythari Tribe, earning their reverence through her mystical connection to the land and her ability to maintain harmony amidst looming threats. She visits Ceylan every five years for guidance, striving to hold the realm together against the rise of the Empire. Through her leadership, Aphyrosia aims to ensure the stability and prosperity of Haliriel and the Elven Realm at large, while carefully maneuvering through the complex tapestry of prophecies that shape the world’s future.

Secret

Potential hidden knowledge about the prophecy, secret alliances, deception of Candice, Jarek, and others for the greater good of the Elven cause. She possesses unknown, deeper knowledge concerning the prophecy and the Watcher, and keeps her true name, Aphyrosia, closely guarded. She is also privy to Ceylan’s mental decline, understanding it results from a profound sacrifice and that it will eventually lead to his demise.

Combat Training

High combat training in elven warfare, archery, defensive and offensive magic; highly skilled in magic and diplomacy. Although she strives for peaceful resolutions, Aphyrosia’s command of both martial and arcane arts allows her to defend her domain when needed.

Inventory

Elaborate ceremonial robes, a small pouch of rare healing herbs. She carries items reflective of her station and her role as a protector, ensuring she is always prepared to perform rituals, treat injuries, or demonstrate the historical significance of her lineage.
