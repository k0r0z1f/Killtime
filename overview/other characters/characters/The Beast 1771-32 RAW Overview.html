Birth Year

No birth year has been provided for The Beast.

Role/Background

The Beast is a powerful, intelligent creature known for capturing, testing, and hunting Lucas. Initially, it is perceived as a relentless predator; however, it is later revealed by Aphyrosia that its actions have been those of a guardian all along.

Personality Traits

The Beast exhibits a complex array of traits. It is described as calculating, patient, observant, strategic, dominant, intelligent, primal, relentless, adaptive, instinct-driven, furious, and cautious. This mixture of traits highlights a creature that is both methodical and instinctually driven, capable of adapting its approach as needed.

Abilities/Magic

It possesses formidable abilities, including great strength, speed, and intelligence, complemented by an impressive memory recall. Its physical power and agility allow it to adapt its attacks effectively in different combat situations.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

No alliances, clans, or factions have been provided for The Beast.

Appearance

The Beast is initially described as having a hulking form with thick fur, a massive muscular frame, lethal claws, fangs, and glowing eyes. In subsequent descriptions, its appearance shifts to one marked by burned fur, charred muscle, twisted sinew, and lifeless, ember-like eyes, suggesting a transformation in its physical state.

Character Arc/Goal

Its arc centers on testing Lucas by gauging his worth and possibly comparing him to an aspect of its own past. The narrative shifts from portraying The Beast as a relentless predator to that of a cornered prey in combat, before ultimately revealing its true role as a guardian.

Secret

The Beast harbors a secret: it holds the memory of a figure wreathed in flame. This memory is pivotal as it redefines its identity, exposing that it was never merely a mindless predator but a guardian with a deeper purpose.

Combat Training

Exemplifying the prowess of a natural predator, The Beast is highly skilled in combat. It studies its opponents carefully before launching calculated, instinct-driven strikes.
