Role/Background

The Resistance Leader is the head of the Resistance base A13, located on the planet Vardis. They lead the resistance group against the Empire’s growing control, especially in the city of Atraxis. With deep knowledge of the Empire's operations, the leader coordinates covert missions and leads the group with a strict, no-nonsense attitude.

Personality Traits

The Resistance Leader is grim, serious, and exhibits an intense authority. They prioritize survival and tactical advantage, displaying little patience for indecision. Their leadership is driven by a strong sense of responsibility and a desire to protect their people, even at the cost of personal doubt and emotional burden.

Abilities/Magic

The Resistance Leader excels in leadership, resource management, and covert operations. They are also highly skilled with communication systems and technology, which they use to coordinate the resistance’s efforts against the Empire.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Resistance Leader leads a faction on Vardis, specifically based in Atraxis, organizing efforts against the Empire.

Appearance

The Resistance Leader is middle-aged with weathered features and a grim expression. They have a commanding posture and dress in practical, worn-out clothing suited for resistance efforts. Their intense gaze is a reflection of their seriousness and weariness.

Character Arc/Goal

The Resistance Leader’s goal is to protect their people from the Empire’s oppression, to continue the fight against the Empire, and to find redemption for past regrets. Their journey involves managing the pressure of leadership and ensuring the survival of their group.

Secret

The Resistance Leader secretly fears losing control over their group and carries deep emotional regret from their past. They keep this burden hidden to maintain authority and focus on the mission.

Combat Training

The Resistance Leader has extensive training in guerrilla tactics, survival skills, and self-defense. They also possess in-depth knowledge of Empire operations, which is invaluable in the resistance’s fight for survival.

Inventory

The Resistance Leader carries concealed weapons, communication devices, emergency rations, a map of escape routes, and a dossier containing valuable intelligence about the Empire’s movements.
