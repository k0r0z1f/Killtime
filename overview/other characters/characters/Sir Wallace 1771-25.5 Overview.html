Birth Year

Sir Wallace was born in 1722, a veteran of many years who has served as a protector and strategist for Mina's family.

Role/Background

Once a Secret Intelligence agent, Sir Wallace is a loyal servant to Mina's family, serving as both protector and confidant. His role extends far beyond that of a mere servant, as he has a deeper involvement with the family's interests and has worked in espionage. He is now fully engaged in the battle against the Disciple and plays a crucial role in the group's survival.

Personality Traits

Sir Wallace is a composed and strategic individual who maintains a level of discretion in all matters. His personality traits include:

  • He is duty-bound and has a profound sense of loyalty to those he serves, especially to Mina.
  • He is discreet and observant, making him an ideal protector and spy.
  • His battle-hardened experience makes him composed and steady even in dire situations.
  • He is highly strategic and a tactical thinker, often aiding in battle plans.
  • Sir Wallace remains protective of Mina and her family, even when faced with great danger.

Abilities/Magic

Sir Wallace possesses no magical abilities but is highly skilled in espionage, advanced combat techniques, and battlefield strategy. His training as an assassin and tactical warfare expert makes him a formidable ally in any conflict.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Sir Wallace is closely aligned with the estate of Mina's family and, through his involvement with her father, holds a connection with the Cult of the Sent Ones. His loyalty to Mina and her family is his primary driving force, but he also carries the weight of greater allegiances that are yet to be fully understood.

Appearance

Sir Wallace is a well-groomed, male human with a sharp, commanding presence. His attire, though formal, is practical and suited for both protection and combat. His sharp eyes convey a man who is always alert, and his stature reflects his training and experience.

Character Arc/Goal

Sir Wallace's arc revolves around his duty to protect Mina, ensuring her safety and balance in the face of growing threats. As the battle intensifies, his loyalty to her family remains steadfast, but he becomes increasingly involved in the group’s larger goals. His tactical expertise becomes indispensable in their fight against the Disciple. Though he is injured, his resolve is unshaken. His fate, however, remains uncertain after he is sent back to Kingston.

Secret

Sir Wallace's true role goes far beyond that of a mere butler. He was trained as a protector and operative, skilled in both espionage and combat. His deep involvement in Mina's family's affairs, along with his strategic mind, makes him a focal point of suspicion. His tactical contributions during the battle against the Disciple remain critical to their success, but much about his personal motivations remains shrouded in mystery.

Combat Training

Sir Wallace is trained in advanced blade combat, espionage, and tactical warfare. His skills allow him to blend seamlessly into both the shadows and the battlefield, where his strategic insight can turn the tide of combat.
