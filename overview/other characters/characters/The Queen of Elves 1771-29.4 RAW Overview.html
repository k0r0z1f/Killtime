Role/Background

Aphyrosia is the Queen of Elves and leads her group in maintaining safety, overseeing camp management, and assisting in climbing the Watcher. She intervenes in crises like the Minulican confrontation and monitors Lucas and Mina's bond.

Personality Traits

She is calm, composed, and radiates quiet reverence. Her demeanor is thoughtful, observant, and protective, often acting as a resolute leader during critical moments.

Abilities/Magic

Aphyrosia has mastery over air magic and intermediate skills in fire, water, and earth. Her abilities include controlling wind, creating barriers, healing, and restraining powerful creatures.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

She is allied with the Nythari Tribe, reflecting her connection to her people and role as their leader.

Appearance

Aphyrosia is tall, with silver hair that catches the light of the setting sun. Her piercing green eyes and elegant frame convey her regal nature, while a faint aura of air magic surrounds her.

Character Arc/Goal

Her goals include supporting the group in proving their worth to the Watcher, managing the Minulican crisis, and ensuring the safety of her people. She recognizes the bond between Lucas and Mina as crucial for survival and strives to maintain order in chaotic situations.

Combat Training

Aphyrosia is skilled in magic, with a focus on combat-oriented magical techniques.

Inventory

She carries magical items designed for barrier creation and healing, essential tools for her role as a leader and protector.
