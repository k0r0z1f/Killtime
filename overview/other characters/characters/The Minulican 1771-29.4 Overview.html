Role/Background

The Minulican is a mysterious and hulking creature unleashed during an arcane storm. It is possibly linked to Lucas and Mina's powers, serving as a chaotic and monstrous antagonist tied to pivotal events in the story.

Personality Traits

Chaotic, destructive, intense, and overwhelming. The Minulican holds disdain for mortals, exuding a menacing and dark presence.

Abilities/Magic

The Minulican pulses with unstable energy and wields unpredictable power. Its abilities include emitting lightning bursts, absorbing flames into its aura, manipulating energy, and creating devastating bolts of chaos.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Minulican appears to work alone but has a mysterious connection to Lucas and Mina, requiring them for an unknown purpose.

Appearance

A large, twisted, and hulking figure with demon-like curling horns, thick limbs, glowing eyes, and crackling energy emanating from its form. Flames lick its skin as unstable energy pulses through its body.

Character Arc/Goal

The Minulican is a force of chaos unleashed upon the world, responding to a mysterious call from the night sky. It seeks Lucas and Mina, possibly for the completion of its own goal, and holds ties to the larger events of the prophecy.

Combat Training

The Minulican demonstrates the ability to engage in violent and destructive combat, using its energy manipulation to attack and overpower adversaries.

Inventory

The Minulican possesses a faintly glowing crystal it absorbed from a facility, a key item in its mysterious purpose.
