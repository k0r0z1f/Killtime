Role/Background

The Minulican is a mysterious and hulking creature that was unleashed during an arcane storm. It is possibly linked to the powers of Lucas and Mina, and is described as a monstrous, chaotic entity. As one of the primary antagonists in the chapter, its emergence is tied to the unfolding events surrounding Lucas and Mina, and it is noted to have left the scene eastward after its appearance.

Personality Traits

The creature exhibits a chaotic and destructive demeanor, characterized by intense and overwhelming energy. It shows a clear disdain for mortals, and its very presence is menacing and dark, evoking fear and uncertainty in those who encounter it.

Abilities/Magic

The Minulican pulses with unstable energy and possesses raw, unpredictable power. It is capable of emitting bursts of lightning, absorbing flames into its aura, manipulating energy, and creating devastating bolts of chaos. Additionally, as it departs, it leaves behind a faint trace of energy.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Although The Minulican appears to operate alone, it seemingly requires the involvement of Lucas and Mina for reasons that have not been fully explained. Its alliances remain ambiguous, with its actions suggesting an underlying purpose connected to the larger events of the narrative.

Appearance

The Minulican is depicted as a large and twisted figure with a formidable presence. It has horned features, including demon-like curling horns, and its thick limbs add to its menacing stature. Its eyes glow with a fierce intensity, and crackling energy runs throughout its body. Unstable energy pulses visibly through its form, while flames can be seen licking its skin, emphasizing its chaotic nature.

Character Arc/Goal

This creature acts as a force of chaos unleashed upon the world. It responds to a mysterious call from the night sky and appears to be in pursuit of Lucas and Mina, possibly to fulfill its own enigmatic purpose. There is an implication that its actions are connected to a larger prophecy, although it is currently not present with the group.

Secret

No secret information has been provided for The Minulican.

Combat Training

While not formally trained, The Minulican demonstrates the ability to engage in violent and destructive combat by utilizing its energy manipulation powers to launch devastating attacks.

Inventory

The only noted item in The Minulican's inventory is a faintly glowing crystal, which it absorbed from the facility.
