Character

Ceylan is a mentor figure, known for his deep connection to the mystical forces of The Watcher. He rarely emerges from his isolated lifestyle, and most who seek his guidance must journey to him.

Birth Year

The exact year of Ceylan's birth is unknown. His advanced age and the weathered lines on his face suggest that he has long outlived most men of his era.

Role/Background

As a mentor to Candice, Ceylan resides in a secluded cave on The Watcher mountain. He passes on his spiritual wisdom to guide her in uncovering the truth about the Children of Hybris, an elusive group he believes holds the key to the world’s protection.

Personality Traits

Ceylan is eccentric and deeply wise. He is quiet and contemplative, speaking only when necessary. At times, he displays a rare sense of urgency, and his cryptic advice can be both enlightening and confusing. He has a tendency to manipulate situations to suit his perceived greater good, and he occasionally forgets crucial details due to his declining mental state. His reclusive nature underscores a life spent in solitude.

Abilities/Magic

He possesses spiritual ties to The Watcher, channeling its energy to perform unique rituals or gain insights. Though these connections grant him unusual knowledge, they do not translate into traditional combat prowess.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Ceylan’s primary allegiance lies with the Nythari Tribe. Though his seclusion has distanced him from many tribe members, he is still recognized as one who once held authority and respect among them.

Appearance

Ceylan is an aged, weathered man with a long, unkempt beard. His eyes reflect a lifetime of knowledge and sacrifice. Dressed in worn robes and leaning on a simple staff, he looks every bit the hermit sage one might expect to find on an isolated mountaintop.

Character Arc/Goal

Ceylan's overarching goal is to guide Candice on her journey to find the Children of Hybris and safeguard the world from impending threats. He believes the prophecy of The Watcher indicates a growing darkness and that only Candice’s success can thwart the rise of catastrophic forces.

Secret

Ceylan suspects the Empire’s ascension, along with its chosen Champion. He hints that a dark prophecy foretells a devastating chain of events. The weight of this knowledge—and the spiritual sacrifice he once made—have eroded his mind. His visions are increasingly clouded and his health deteriorates, foreshadowing his eventual death before the conflict truly unfolds.

Combat Training

Ceylan is not a warrior. He relies almost entirely on mystical insight and spiritual communion with The Watcher to navigate conflicts. Although he can defend himself with basic spells or subtle manipulations, he avoids direct confrontation whenever possible.

Inventory

No notable inventory is listed for Ceylan. His primary “possessions” are his worn garments, a simple staff, and the intangible knowledge he carries.
