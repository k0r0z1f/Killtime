Birth Year

Eryndor was born in 1611, making him 160 years old as of 04-06-1771.

Role/Background

A skilled elf hunter who serves as the protector of the Nythari village. Eryndor is highly knowledgeable about the treacherous paths surrounding the Watcher, a crucial skill for guarding his people against external threats.

Personality Traits

Though gruff and sharp-tongued, Eryndor deeply cares for his people. His fierce protectiveness often masks a softer, compassionate side that surfaces in moments of vulnerability or trust.

Abilities/Magic

Eryndor is an expert tracker and marksman, with survival skills honed over decades. He has moderate proficiency in nature magic, which includes communicating with animals, minor healing, and understanding the forest's natural rhythms.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Eryndor is a loyal member of the Nythari Tribe, whose survival depends on his skills and leadership.

Appearance

Eryndor is tall and lean, with weathered features that tell of countless years spent in the wild. His long dark hair is tied back, and his sharp brown eyes are ever-watchful. He wears a cloak of furs, blending seamlessly with the wilderness.

Character Arc/Goal

Eryndor’s primary goal is to safeguard the Nythari people and protect the ancient secrets of the Watcher, ensuring the tribe’s survival and harmony with the forest.

Combat Training

Eryndor is highly skilled in archery, stealth, hand-to-hand combat, and the use of traps. His combat expertise makes him a formidable defender of his people.

Inventory

Eryndor carries a longbow with a quiver of enchanted arrows, a hunting knife, survival tools, a charm tied to Nythari traditions, and a satchel of dried herbs for medicine and rituals.
