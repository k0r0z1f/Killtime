Character

The Beast is a formidable guardian and predator inhabiting the northern cliffs. Described as a massive, hulking, fur-covered creature, it embodies both raw savagery and a surprising intelligence, making it a fearsome presence in its territory.

Birth Year

Birth year not provided.

Role/Background

The Beast serves as the guardian of the lair on the northern cliffs. It is a predatory force of nature that relentlessly closes in on intruders, capturing, testing, and hunting Lucas. Initially perceived solely as a relentless predator, it is later revealed by Aphyrosia that its actions were those of a guardian rather than a mindless hunter, marking it as an enemy creature with a hidden purpose.

Personality Traits

Exhibiting a menacing and predatory demeanor, The Beast is singularly focused on its prey. Its traits include relentless savagery, fury, and adaptability, combined with calculated patience and observant caution. Despite its unyielding nature, there are moments when it shows acknowledgment of defeat, hinting at an ability to reassess its tactics.

Abilities/Magic

The Beast boasts immense physical strength, speed, agility, and adaptability. Its exceptional ferocity is complemented by a keen intelligence and remarkable memory recall, enabling it to effectively study and counter its opponents.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Classified as a mythical being, The Beast operates alone without any known alliances or clan affiliations, driven solely by its inherent nature and ancient duty.

Appearance

In life, The Beast is depicted as an enormous, bipedal creature with a thick, matted fur coat. Its features include imposing saber-tooth-like fangs, glowing (or golden) eyes, massive claws, and visible steam curling from its nostrils, all of which enhance its intimidating presence. After death, its appearance transforms to show burned fur, charred muscle, twisted sinew, and lifeless, ember-like eyes, further emphasizing its fallen state.

Character Arc/Goal

The Beast’s arc centers on its duty to protect its lair and the artifacts contained within. It is driven to capture or kill the intruding group—especially targeting Dira—and to test and overcome its opponents. Over time, its role shifts from that of a relentless predator to a more complex guardian, as seen in its method of testing Lucas and gauging his worth by comparing him to echoes of its past.

Secret

Deep within, The Beast harbors a secret memory of a figure wreathed in flame. This recollection hints that it was once more than a mere predator, serving instead as a guardian with a mysterious, nuanced past.

Combat Training

Without formal combat training, The Beast excels in physical combat through its natural prowess. It leverages its immense strength and speed to overpower foes, studying its opponents carefully and relying on innate hunting instincts.
