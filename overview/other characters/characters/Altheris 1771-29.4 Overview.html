Role/Background

Altheris guides the party and is knowledgeable about the mission and Queen Aphyrosia's involvement. As the commandant of the expedition, he enforces military discipline and maintains vigilance, constantly scanning for threats. He was formerly a guard and scout, responsible for patrols and rear vigilance.

Personality Traits

Altheris is composed, respectful, and duty-bound. His calm, pragmatic demeanor reflects his focus and protective instincts. He is observant, vigilant, and serious, with a loyal and authoritative presence.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Altheris is the Commandant of Haliriel's Defense and is closely aligned with Queen Aphyrosia. He is a loyal member of the Children of Hybris and plays a pivotal role in the expedition group.

Appearance

Altheris is a composed figure with a larger frame for an elf, exuding an imposing and battle-hardened presence. He is muscular with sharp, observant eyes and practical, well-dressed attire.

Character Arc/Goal

Altheris’s primary goal is to ensure the group’s safety and that the mission’s objectives are achieved. He seeks to protect the Queen, consult Ceylan’s wisdom, and successfully ascend the mountain, defending against threats such as the Minulican and the Beast.

Secret

Altheris saw a mysterious, hairy figure watching the camp but has kept this information secret, as instructed by Queen Aphyrosia.

Combat Training

Altheris has extensive military training, excelling in physical combat and defense techniques.

Inventory

Altheris carries a fencing sword, which he wields with skill and precision.
