Role/Background

The Resistance is a coalition formed by various factions including native tribes, outlaws, and former Empire soldiers who oppose the Imperial control over Vardis. They are dedicated to protecting their land, people, and the planet's mystical forces.

Personality Traits

The Resistance members are determined and defiant, using their resourcefulness to navigate the challenges they face. They are pragmatic and suspicious of outsiders, often choosing to rely on their own survival skills and knowledge of the land.

Abilities/Magic

Many members of the Resistance possess elemental magic that ties them to the forces of nature, as well as advanced guerrilla tactics and survival skills. They are highly adaptable, using the terrain to their advantage in their ongoing struggle against the Empire.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Resistance is primarily made up of the indigenous tribes of Vardis, along with outlaws and defectors from the Empire. Their shared goal is to protect the mystical powers of Vardis and resist Imperial exploitation.

Appearance

The Resistance members have varied appearances depending on their backgrounds. Some wear rugged, functional clothing suited for guerrilla warfare, while others don tribal symbols, camouflage gear, or even elemental tattoos that reflect their affinity with the land and its magic.

Character Arc/Goal

The primary goal of the Resistance is to prevent the Empire from exploiting Vardis. They aim to harness the planet's mystical powers for their own defense and ensure that the land remains free from outside influence.

Secret

The Resistance is not a united front. Some factions within it secretly engage in negotiations with the Empire, while others use the planet's mystical forces for their own purposes. These internal divisions complicate the overall mission and make their ultimate goals uncertain.
