Summary

The group gathers in Aria’s hut, where Queen Aphyrosia discusses their urgent mission to ascend the mountain and consult with Ceylan. She shares a legend about the Watcher and its chosen protectors, the Children of the Watcher, inspiring the group with ancient wisdom. A ritual is performed to strengthen their resolve before the journey.

Important Events

  1. Aphyrosia outlines the group’s need to reach Ceylan for guidance on the prophecy.
  2. Candice suggests preparation and gathering mountain conditions from Eryndor.
  3. Aria recommends visiting key locations: the eastern temple, villages, and beast’s lair.
  4. Aphyrosia shares the Watcher’s legend and its connection to the group.
  5. Aria performs a ritual to inspire resilience in the group.

Locations

Aria’s hut: A cozy, secluded dwelling where the group gathers to discuss their mission and perform the ritual.
