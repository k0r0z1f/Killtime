31.4.1: The Dying Light Speaks

The group enters Ceylan’s cave and finds him frail and mentally deteriorated. Immediately recognizing Lucas and Mina, he speaks in cryptic riddles, referencing cycles, fate, and a mysterious "sleeping name." His erratic behavior unsettles the group, especially when he names Candice as his "vessel" and momentarily accuses Thomas of being linked to an impending attack. Candice is deeply troubled by witnessing her mentor in such a broken state, while Lucas realizes that the clarity he seeks will not come from Ceylan. Determined to uncover the truth, Lucas opts to rely on his own abilities.

Key Themes & Moments: Ceylan’s mental decline, cryptic prophecy with cycles and fate, Candice's emotional turmoil, Thomas’s defensive reaction, and Lucas’s decision to use his own abilities.

31.4.2: The Tomb That Watches

Confronting Ceylan in his further deteriorated state, the group seeks answers. Despite his fragmented mind, he speaks of a hidden prison of mirrors and sarcophagi—a "tomb that watches." Aphyrosia reveals that she anticipated Ceylan’s decline as a result of an old sacrifice, while Candice demands clarity on their purpose given his lost condition. Once again referring to Candice as his "vessel," Ceylan hints at concealed forces manipulating fate through walls of reflection. Lucas and Mina are left with the unsettling feeling that his words hold significant meaning, with Candice linking them to the sacred Temple of Crystals as a potential new lead.

Key Themes & Moments: Fractured guidance, prophetic clues of fate and sacrifice, the symbolism of mirrors and hidden tombs, and the introduction of the Temple of Crystals as a mysterious lead.

31.4.3: Visions and Frustrations

In an effort to delve deeper into Ceylan’s mind, Lucas and Mina experience powerful visions. Lucas is involuntarily thrust into the traumatic perspective of a past researcher—his own tormentor from Kingston—reliving a memory of being a child bound to a chair and subjected to cruel experimentation. Overwhelmed by painful memories, he storms out of the cave in rage and despair. Concurrently, Mina witnesses a surreal vision in which an enigmatic being offers two crystals—one red and one blue—to a powerful entity seated on an ancient throne. Later, Aphyrosia confirms that Mina’s vision mirrors Ceylan’s earlier sacrifice. The revelations intensify the group’s tension, with Lucas isolating himself and Mina struggling to decipher the cryptic significance of her experience.

Key Themes & Moments: Lucas’s painful reliving of past trauma, Mina’s cryptic vision of crystals and sacrifice, Aphyrosia’s confirmation, and the resulting escalation of tension and isolation within the group.
