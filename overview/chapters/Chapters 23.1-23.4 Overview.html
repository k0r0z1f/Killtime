Chapter 23.1 - Arrival and Meeting the Guide

The group arrives at the moss-covered temple of the Sent Ones, where they meet Dira, their guide. Inside, they encounter cultists, some of whom are particularly intrigued by Mina's powers. The group begins to feel uneasy as Dira becomes increasingly nervous, leading them deeper into the temple. The air grows thick with ominous energy, and something feels off about the situation.

Chapter 23.2 - The Beast in the Shadows

The party battles a grotesque sewer beast in the dark tunnels. Afterward, Dira's true allegiance as a spy for the Cult of the Unbound is revealed. Tension rises between Dira and the group, culminating in a confrontation where Lucas uses his telepathy to expose her betrayal. They head towards the Sent Ones' headquarters, bringing Dira for judgment.

The group experiences a rare moment of calm before the conflict ahead. Their training reaches new extremes, particularly for Lucas and Mina. Goldar delivers a final speech, and the group departs for the frontlines, readying themselves for the battles to come.
