25.1 The Trial Begins

Lucas, Mina, and Thomas are put on trial in Brum'korath, accused of collaborating with the Cult of the Sent Ones. They defend their actions, recounting the battle against the Disciple and revealing the Empire's involvement. Tensions rise as Thomas uncovers hidden political agendas and Mina receives a cryptic warning.

25.2 Unraveling Alliances

As Tolgar’s influence grows, the trio receives offers from multiple factions. Lucas becomes suspicious of Tolgar’s intentions after learning of secret meetings, and Mina struggles with mounting pressure. The group’s decision to ally with the dwarves leads to new risks and the discovery of a hidden temple.

25.3 The Council’s Divide

The trio navigates a splintering council, torn between Tolgar’s efforts to unite the Sent Ones and Dira’s aggressive tactics. Amid growing tensions, Lucas questions the loyalty of those around him. The group must choose whether to trust Tolgar or carve their own path in a divided council.

25.4 The Relic’s Power

The group explores a hidden chamber where they encounter ancient guardians. After a fierce battle, Lucas discovers that the relic controls the guardians, and in a desperate attempt to destroy it, the relic fuses to his wrist, halting the guardians and leaving the group with more questions than answers.

25.5 The Elves' Request

The council informs the group that the elves of Haliriel have requested a meeting regarding the prophecy and artifacts. The elves remain distrustful of the Cult and the dwarves. The group prepares for the meeting, realizing the growing importance of the relics and the role of the elves in unraveling the prophecy.
