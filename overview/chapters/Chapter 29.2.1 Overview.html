Summary

The Children of Hybris arrive in the Nythari village and are welcomed with reverence and curiosity. Through a demonstration of their unique powers, Lucas and Mina earn the trust of the villagers. However, Elder Aria warns them of a mysterious stranger seeking a hidden temple, adding urgency to their mission.

Important Events

  • The Children of Hybris arrive in the Nythari village.
  • Aphyrosia is presented with gifts and treated with reverence.
  • Jarek bids farewell to the group and departs.
  • Lucas and Mina demonstrate their abilities, gaining the villagers' trust.
  • Elder Aria warns of a stranger seeking a hidden temple.

The events take place in the Nythari Village and the surrounding jungle paths.
