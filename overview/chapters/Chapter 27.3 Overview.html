27.3.1 - Mission Briefing: Preparing for the Eastern Continent

The party is briefed on their mission to the Eastern Continent, where they must study the Disciple of Air and prevent it from escaping. Altheris introduces the importance of the mission, while tensions rise among the party members as they discuss different strategies for handling the threat. Lucas questions why they are needed, and Candice insists on consulting the Watcher. However, Altheris dismisses the idea. Dira suggests a compromise, and the group views the Windfighter, the transport vessel for the mission. Altheris also gives Lucas access to the magi-tech lab to study his relic.

27.3.2 - Introducing the Elven Researchers

Lucas, Mina, and Thomas visit a magi-tech laboratory where their relics are examined by elven researchers. Tensions arise as Lucas feels uncomfortable in the facility. Altheris introduces Aranel, the lead researcher, and explains their task. The researchers begin scanning the relics and uncover strange anomalies in their energy and DNA synchronization. Lucas expresses his frustration, and Aranel acknowledges the anomaly, showing interest in Lucas's understanding of the relics. However, the relics remain an enigma, and Lucas's uncertainty deepens. Mina reassures him, offering her support.

27.3.3 - Candice's Plea: A Queen's Test

Candice pleads with the Queen of Elves to alter their course, seeking a detour to the Watcher. The Queen reveals that she has already planned a journey to the Watcher but will test Candice's resolve. The Queen sets two conditions for the detour: Candice must persuade her companions and they must depart in two days. The Watcher is revealed to be a place where deep truths about the prophecy will be revealed.
