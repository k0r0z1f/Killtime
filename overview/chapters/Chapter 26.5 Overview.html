Arrival in Haliriel and a Mysterious Presence

The party arrives in Haliriel and is met with a sense of awe, as the city reveals its beauty and political significance. They are introduced to the Queen of the Elves, who demonstrates her mastery of Air Magic, leaving a lasting impression on the group. As they settle in, the prophecy and the role they will play in the coming events become even clearer, with tensions rising among the elven factions.

The Unseen Presence

While settling into Haliriel, Lucas and Mina begin to sense an unknown presence stirring through their telepathic link. They feel drawn to something or someone, a force that is both familiar and unsettling. Meanwhile, Candice, who has arrived in Haliriel, is taken by guards to meet with the High Elves, unaware that her fate is intricately tied to the group’s destiny.

The Weight of Fate

At the High Temple of Nature, Candice and her companions stand before the High Elves, who deliberate on Candice's fate. Tension runs high as Candice grapples with the weight of their scrutiny and her growing connection to Lucas and Mina. As her telepathic bond with them deepens, it becomes clear that she holds a significant role in the prophecy, though the full extent of her involvement remains uncertain.
