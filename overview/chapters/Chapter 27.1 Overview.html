Candice realizes the chaotic presence in her mind might be linked to her mentor, Ceylan. She inadvertently identifies Lucas and Mina as the Children of Hybris. The High Elves Council confirms this, but the Queen of Elves seems to have anticipated this moment. The chapter ends with lingering questions about the Queen’s knowledge and motives.
