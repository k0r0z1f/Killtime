Chapter 24.1 - Leadership Struggles (Part 1)

With Goldar captured, the cult is thrown into disarray under Torgar’s leadership. His attempts to rally the cultists are met with distrust and skepticism. Lucas, Mina, and Thomas realize they cannot rely on the cult for protection. They begin training on their own, preparing for the upcoming war. Meanwhile, Dira reveals important secrets about the relics in the temple and their potential as weapons, urging Mina to consider her role in the prophecy of the Sent Ones.

Chapter 24.2 - The Disciple’s Arrival (Part 2)

The Disciple arrives at Brum'karoth, devastating the city with his overwhelming power. His cryptic demand for a weapon against the Empire throws the city into chaos, with the cult forced to confront this new threat. Lucas, Mina, and Thomas decide to take matters into their own hands, preparing to face the Disciple head-on. As they delve deeper into the mystery of the hidden weapon, they prepare for a confrontation that will decide the city’s fate.

Chapter 24.3 - Fractured Fronts (Part 3)

The group executes a daring plan to exploit the Disciple’s vulnerability, using thermal shock to weaken its rocky armor. As they succeed, Mina refuses to let the creature recover and pursues it, despite the risks. Lucas, weakened but determined, follows her. The battle escalates, and the group must push themselves beyond their limits to prevent the Disciple from recovering.

Chapter 24.4 - Wallace Joins the Fray (Part 4)

Wallace arrives to aid the group, providing a much-needed tactical advantage. However, the Disciple adapts to their strategy, and the battle grows increasingly difficult. Wallace is injured, and the group retreats to regroup and plan their next move. With time running out, they finalize a strategy to target the Disciple’s core. Lucas, though exhausted, resolves to continue the fight.

Chapter 24.5 - The Final Showdown (Part 5)

After a fierce battle, the Disciple is finally defeated, but at great cost. Lucas is unconscious, his strength spent, while Mina fights recklessly, driven by her determination. It is only when Thomas intervenes that the Disciple is truly vanquished. The city lies in ruins, and the group surveys the aftermath, dealing with the toll of the battle on both their bodies and spirits. Their journey, however, is far from over, and the future remains uncertain.
