Chapter Number

Chapter Title

Last Stops and the Queen’s Reflection

Summary

Candice and her group visit Eryndor, the Nythari hunter, to learn about possible routes to the Watcher. Eryndor shares details about the Pass of the Hawk (clear), the Pass of the Fox (impassable due to snow), and the Pass of the Bear (treacherous but possible). Accepting gifts from villagers at the southern edge of the village, they begin their climb. An earthquake rattles the mountain, prompting concern and a sense of the Watcher’s awareness. The Queen reflects on her past visits to see Ceylan and on his declining health, reaffirming their mission’s urgency. They continue onward, aiming to reach the Watcher’s cave by evening.

Important Events in Order

  1. Candice and the Queen arrive at Eryndor’s hut to gather information on the routes to the middle slope.
  2. Eryndor explains the conditions of the Passes of the Hawk, Fox, and Bear.
  3. The group departs the village, receiving gifts and blessings from elders and children.
  4. The Queen reflects on Ceylan’s diminishing condition and the importance of the journey.
  5. An earthquake occurs during their ascent, causing unease but not deterring them.
  6. The group continues toward the Watcher’s cave, determined to meet Ceylan.
