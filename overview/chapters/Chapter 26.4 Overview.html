26.4.1 - Shadows of Justice and Light of Discovery (Part 4)

The party is summoned to the Dwarven Council, where an elf envoy from Haliriel, Altheris, presents a high-stakes mission involving a powerful Disciple of the Empire, linked to the Sent Ones' prophecy. Altheris offers protection and sanctuary from the World Police in exchange for their help, but the party is left with lingering doubts about trusting the elves and the risks of the mission.

26.4.2 - Tensions and Terms

The negotiation between the party and the dwarven council becomes more heated as both sides struggle with differing priorities. The party seeks assurances for their safety and autonomy while the dwarves demand compensation for their involvement. Altheris mediates, but underlying distrust and rivalries continue to surface.

26.4.3 - The Weight of Promises

Lucas and the party face the elves and dwarves in the council chamber, negotiating terms for their mission and future freedom. Trust is in short supply as the political tensions rise, and the group demands written assurances of their safety and freedom. Despite reaching an agreement, Lucas remains distrustful, sensing that they are being manipulated.
