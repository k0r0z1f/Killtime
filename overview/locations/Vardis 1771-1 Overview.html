Population

Vardis has a mixed population, with both indigenous tribes and imperial settlers. The indigenous people live in small, tight-knit communities, while imperial settlers have created fortified outposts and settlements to exploit the planet's resources.

Appearance

The planet's surface is diverse, featuring rugged, rocky mountains, dense forests, expansive plains, and scars of war from past conflicts. The landscape has a stark contrast between natural beauty and remnants of violent histories.

Energy & Atmosphere

Vardis exudes a tense and conflicted atmosphere, with a mix of primitive magic and imperial presence. The indigenous tribes view the land with reverence and seek to protect it, while the Empire views the planet as a resource to be exploited.

Architecture

The architecture on Vardis is a stark contrast between the primitive tribal villages and the imperial settlements. Tribal villages are built with natural materials and have an organic feel, while imperial settlements are fortified and utilitarian, built with advanced technology and military purpose.

Symbolism & Carvings

The indigenous tribes use tribal symbols to represent nature, spirits, and magic, while imperial outposts bear imperial insignia. The juxtaposition of these symbols reflects the ongoing conflict and cultural clash on Vardis.

Defenses

The planet is defended by a mix of guerrilla fighters, tribal alliances, imperial military forces, and fortified outposts. These defenses are constantly shifting as the indigenous tribes grow stronger and resist imperial control.

Legends & Lore

Vardis is home to powerful magical beings, tribal heroes, and the remnants of fallen empires. Ancient lore suggests that the planet itself holds incredible magical power, and there are stories of past empires that fell after attempting to harness it.

Hidden Dangers

There are many hidden dangers on Vardis, including natural hazards like treacherous terrain, as well as imperial exploitation of the planet's resources. Wild magical forces and supernatural occurrences, such as mysterious lights, whispers in the wind, and ghostly apparitions, add to the planet's mystique.

Link to the Party

Vardis plays a critical role in the party's journey, especially for John, whose main task is to guide the 'messiah' figure through the dangers of the planet. The conflict between the Empire and the indigenous resistance will shape the party's actions.

Surrounding Terrain

The terrain of Vardis includes rocky mountains, dense forests, and expansive plains, all of which provide both cover and challenges for those who traverse the planet.

Weather

Vardis is experiencing increasingly erratic and strange storms, which some believe are influenced by the planet’s mystical forces. These storms are believed to be both an omen and a sign of the planet’s power.

Recent Developments

The Empire has intensified its presence on Vardis with fortified outposts, while suppressing resistance movements from the native tribes. The resistance is growing stronger, and there are rumors that they have begun to tap into the planet’s ancient magic.
