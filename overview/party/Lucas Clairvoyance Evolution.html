Lucas Clairvoyance Evolution
Stage Abilities Key Milestones Limitations/Challenges
Entry Unconscious Visions Receives flashes of images or emotions without control (e.g., brief, random glimpses). Lacks conscious awareness; visions are disjointed and fragmented.
Basic Partial Future/Past Visions Experiences fleeting moments from the past or near future, often unrelated to current events. Difficult to control; unclear in focus or relevance.
Intermediate Directed Visions Can focus on specific individuals or events, receiving clearer visions of past or immediate future actions. Still limited to near events or specific contexts; requires intense concentration.
Advanced Accurate Short-Term Predictions Sees clearer visions of the near future or the past (e.g., within hours or days). Can influence decisions based on these insights. Difficulty controlling the scope of the vision; may not always align with reality.
Mastery Long-Term Past and Future Vision Gains insight into past events or future outcomes over extended periods (e.g., months or years). Intense strain; visions are emotionally taxing and may blur with reality.
Expert Possible Future or Past Scenarios Can see multiple possible outcomes or scenarios related to a person or event. Visualizes different paths taken by the same object or person. Overwhelmed by potential outcomes; decisions may be clouded by too many possibilities.
Elite Temporal Layering and Shifting Sees multiple timelines concurrently, exploring alternate histories or futures. Can perceive how small changes alter events. Difficult to manage; the complexity of timelines can create confusion and instability.
Final Infinite Temporal Vision Can see infinite possibilities across the entire spectrum of time (past, future, parallel realities) for any object or creature. Complete disconnection from the present; overwhelming mental toll.
