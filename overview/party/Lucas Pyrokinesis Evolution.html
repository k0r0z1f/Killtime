Lucas Telekinesis Evolution

Stage Abilities Key Milestones Limitations/Challenges
Entry Small Heat Control Raises or lowers the temperature of objects (e.g., warming a cup, cooling small surfaces). Limited to small, non-living objects; slow effect.
Basic Flame Creation Generates small flames from his hands. Flames are weak and easily extinguished.
Intermediate Fire Manipulation Shapes and sustains moderate flames (e.g., fireballs). Difficulty controlling size and spread.
Advanced Thermal Shielding Creates a barrier of heat to block attacks. Requires focus; drains energy rapidly.
Mastery Combustion Control Ignites or extinguishes flames in a wide radius. Risk of harming allies in uncontrolled areas.
Expert Temperature Manipulation Raises or lowers ambient temperature drastically (e.g., superheating air, flash freezing water). Affects large areas; risks environmental damage.
Elite Molecular Thermal Control Alters the kinetic energy of molecules to manipulate matter states (e.g., turning solid to liquid or gas instantly). High strain on body and mind.
Final Absolute Temperature Control Reaches near-absolute zero Kelvin or near-maximum heat (Planck temperature). Extreme energy cost; risks catastrophic consequences like breaking molecular bonds or inducing plasma states uncontrollably.
