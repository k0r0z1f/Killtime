Birth Year

Mina was born in 1754 and is currently 17 years old in the year 1771.

Role/Background

Mina is one of the Children of Hybris, a key figure in a prophecy that intertwines her fate with Lucas. She was revealed as part of this prophecy during the Elven Council meeting and shares a telepathic link with Lucas. She plays a critical role in the group, always highly concerned about the mission and its success.

Personality Traits

Mina is calm, composed, and deeply focused on her destiny, which is tied to the prophecy. She is sharp, loyal, and fiercely protective of those she cares about. Some of her most notable traits include:

  • Independent: Fiercely autonomous, values self-reliance.
  • Empathetic: Strong ability to connect with and understand others’ emotions.
  • Observant: Notices small details others might miss.
  • Brave: Faces danger without hesitation, but with careful thought.
  • Optimistic: Sees hope in difficult situations.
  • Grounding for Lucas: Helps calm Lucas and focus him when needed.
  • Trustworthy: Loyal and protective of those she cares about.

Abilities/Magic

  • Superhuman Speed and Agility: Mina possesses exceptional speed, agility, and acrobatic skills.
  • Hyper-sensitive Senses: Mina’s senses are heightened, but difficult to control.
  • Telepathic Link with Lucas: Mina shares a permanent mental connection with Lucas.
  • Athletic Combat: Focused on quick movements, acrobatic feats, and hit-and-run tactics.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

  • Lucas: Telepathically connected and deeply trusted partner.
  • Thomas: Respects and has mutual trust with Thomas.
  • Cult of the Sent Ones: Indirectly associated through her connection to Lucas and the prophecy.
  • Candice: Not yet an ally, but could play a role in the future.

Appearance

Mina has green eyes and bright blonde hair, styled in a practical way to suit her active lifestyle. She is athletic and physically strong, but her experiences have weathered her appearance. Her demeanor is calm and focused, exuding strength without being overly aggressive.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina’s primary goals revolve around protecting her friends, particularly Lucas, and understanding her role within the Children of Hybris prophecy. She seeks self-discovery and struggles with uncertainty about her future and responsibilities. Despite her doubts, she is determined to navigate her destiny and fulfill her purpose.

Secret

Mina is conflicted about her role in the prophecy, uncertain of her significance and what it ultimately means for her and the world.

Combat Training

Mina excels in athletic combat, using her speed and agility to overwhelm opponents with hit-and-run tactics and acrobatic maneuvers.

Inventory

  • 90 doumis: Currency.
  • Travel Clothes: Practical attire for adventures.
  • Survival Gear: Essential tools for surviving in the wild.
  • Dagger: Compact weapon for close-quarters combat.
  • Relic: A relic tied to the prophecy of the Sent Ones, which she cannot remove.
