Birth Year

1754 (current date 03-06-1771, he's 17 years old)

Role/Background

Hero of Brum'korath; short-time former and recently released Brum'korath prisoner, having taken part in the Cults War and the Disciple's rampage; main protagonist of the party; connected to ancient relics prophecy and fused with one; strategist; outlaw of the Central Lands for the Kingston incident (unrelated to Brum'korath); victim of medi-laboratory experiments from birth to age 11; aspiring college student as a teen; escapee since Kingston incident; One of the Children of Hybris, central to the prophecy. Member of the group preparing for the Eastern Continent expedition. Part of a team trying to unlock relics' power to stop the Disciple of Air; holds a relic bound to his wrist; central figure, researcher, driven to unlock the power of the Relics; connected to the prophecy and studying an ancient relic.

Personality Traits

Determined, analytical, introspective, calculating, self-doubting, practical, cautious, curious, calm under pressure, protective, resolute, skeptical, doubtful, brave, frustrated, observant, burdened by hidden truths, burdened by guilt, feels the weight of scrutiny, uncertain, thoughtful, empathetic, intense, inquisitive, anxious, critical, questioning, wary, gradually more at ease with the group, vulnerable, focused, willing to take risks, burdened by responsibility, pragmatic, occasionally reckless; anxious but hopeful, determined to contribute to the mission, reflective, feels the weight of the prophecy.

Abilities/Magic

Telepathy (Stage 1: Surface Thought Reading - DMN-Focused, Stage 2: Deeper Thought Access and Emotional Sensory Tuning, Stage 3: Memory Access and Short-Term Influence), entry-level Clairvoyance (unconscious or basic vision), intermediate Telekinesis (multiple small objects or one bigger object up to 30kg), Pyrokinesis (entry-level heat control, basic flame creation, intermediate fire manipulation); senses chaotic thoughts and mental presences; permanent telepathic link with Mina; connected to the Sent Ones prophecy; emerging mastery of arcane technology; can telepathically communicate with the Relics; affected by the ancient relic he’s studying; potential magical connection to the prophecy; other unexplained powers.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Aligned with Mina and Thomas; Cult of the Sent Ones (indirectly, via prophecy); Dwarven Council, Brum'korath, World Police (now enemy), Haliriel (temporary alliance); part of the mission team; Children of Hybris (implied); working with a group of researchers.

Appearance

5'10", brown eyes, serious look; lean; light-brown hair, disheveled; typically wears a worn satchel; usually stressed or focused expression.

Character Arc/Goal

Overcoming insecurity to embrace his potential and confront his mysterious past; grappling with his role as one of the Sent Ones; feeling the growing pressure of the coming war and the Disciple’s approach; seeking redemption; uncovering the truth behind the explosion in Kingston; discovering the origin and purpose of the relics; unlocking their mysteries and powers; standing firm against the World Police; protecting his companions; preparing for the coming war; surviving; uncovering hidden motives; conflicted over political and personal motivations; discovering his role in the prophecy and his connection to Candice; unraveling the mysteries that bind him to her and the others; understanding his place in the prophecy with Mina, especially regarding the Disciple of Air; also aims to understand the relic’s nature, contribute to stopping the Disciple of Air, and protect the group, seeking answers about the prophecy and the relics.

Secret

Struggling with memory of the Kingston explosion and guilt over its consequences; he, Mina, and Thomas each have a fused relic; possibly misled about being the chosen one for the relics; resentful about political conflicts; deep mistrust of elves and dwarves due to past manipulations; has a minor anomaly in his DNA leading to instability in relic synchronization; doubts his worthiness for the relics; the relic’s effects on him remain unclear but seem to be worsening.

Combat Training

Strategic and warfare knowledge; telekinetic defense; basic weapon training; basic pyrokinesis and intermediate telekinesis; skilled with basic weaponry; moderate combat training; tactical and strategic thinker from deep study; capable in a leadership context.
