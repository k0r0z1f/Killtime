Birth Year

Lucas was born in 1754, making him 17 years old.

Role/Background

Hero of Brum'korath; short-time former and recently released Brum'korath prisoner, having taken part in the Cults War and the Disciple's rampage; main protagonist of the party; connected to ancient relics prophecy and fused with one; strategist; outlaw of the Central Lands for the Kingston incident (unrelated to Brum'korath); victim of medi-laboratory experiments from birth to age 11; aspiring college student as a teen; escapee since Kingston incident; one of the Children of Hybris, central to the prophecy; part of the mission team preparing for the Eastern Continent expedition; trying to unlock relics' power to stop the Disciples’ threat; connected to the Sent Ones prophecy; supporting the journey to Ceylan's cave on the Watcher; previously had a fever and disappeared (with Mina) before rejoining the group, missing memories of that time; sensitive to a mysterious presence; emerged from Ceylan’s cave, linked to a cryptic prophecy and the dying seer’s words; a person in peril whom Mina is determined to save from the Beast; someone Mina is seeking after being captured by the Beast; a captive forced into confrontation, resourceful and determined to escape from the Beast's lair; formerly restrained and fought for survival against the Beast in a cavern; a combatant wielding fire magic; central figure in the battle against the Beast who fought alone and pushed himself to the limit; a battle-wear traveler on a quest, part of a group searching for answers and uncovering ancient mysteries; explored the mysterious mansion and experienced its disappearance.

Personality Traits

Determined, analytical, introspective, calculating, self-doubting, practical, cautious, curious, calm under pressure, protective, resolute, skeptical, doubtful, brave, frustrated, observant, burdened by hidden truths and guilt and responsibility, feels the weight of scrutiny, uncertain, thoughtful, empathetic, intense, inquisitive, anxious but hopeful, critical, questioning, wary, willing to take risks, pragmatic, occasionally reckless, shy, hesitant, easily embarrassed (love drunk), friendly, reflective, serious, focused, vulnerable, strong-willed, conflicted, fearful of his own powers, resilient; somewhat unsettled after returning; sensitive, enraged, haunted by past; desperate; exhausted; uneasy; instinctive; adaptive; quick-thinking; sharp-minded; steady; precise; battle-worn.

Abilities/Magic

Telepathy (Stage 1: Surface Thought Reading - DMN-Focused, Stage 2: Deeper Thought Access and Emotional Sensory Tuning, Stage 3: Memory Access and Short-Term Influence), entry-level Clairvoyance (unconscious or basic vision), intermediate Telekinesis (multiple small objects or one bigger object up to 30kg), Pyrokinesis (mid-level heat control, moderate flame creation, intermediate fire manipulation, able to shape and direct flames); permanent telepathic link with Mina; emerging mastery of arcane technology; can telepathically communicate with the relics; potential magical connection to the prophecy; other unexplained powers; enhanced telekinesis linked to Mina's telepathy; possesses destructive powers but struggles to control them; moves with unusual ease, seemingly unaffected by cold, exhaustion, or normal physical strain after reappearing and waking up; feels and senses a strange energy; affected by the healing magic of the vanished mansion.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Aligned with Mina and Thomas; Cult of the Sent Ones (indirectly, via prophecy); Dwarven Council, Brum'korath, World Police (declared enemy of the state), Haliriel (temporary alliance); Children of Hybris; part of the mission team currently ascending the Watcher; allies with Candice, Dira, The Queen, and Altheris; part of the traveling group; associated with Ceylan and Mina.

Appearance

5'10", brown eyes, serious look; lean; light-brown hair, disheveled; typically wears a worn satchel; usually stressed or focused expression; wears a coat and boots; noted to have a relic attached to his wrist; marked by emotional strain from battle; well rested after waking up from the mansion.

Character Arc/Goal

Overcoming insecurity to embrace his potential and confront his mysterious past; grappling with his role as one of the Sent Ones; seeking redemption; uncovering the truth behind the explosion in Kingston; discovering the origin and purpose of the relics; unlocking their mysteries and powers; standing firm against the World Police; protecting his companions; surviving; uncovering hidden motives; conflicted over political and personal motivations; discovering his role in the prophecy and his connection to Candice; unraveling the mysteries that bind him to her and the others; understanding his place in the prophecy with Mina, especially regarding the Disciple of Air; assisting in unraveling the prophecy by reaching Ceylan's cave and supporting the journey up the Watcher; aims to understand the relic’s nature, contribute to stopping the Disciple of Air, and protect the group, seeking answers about the prophecy and the relics; seeking answers and freedom from past trauma; uncovering the truth behind Ceylan’s cryptic words; the group suspects something has changed about him; struggling to survive against overwhelming odds and overcome inner turmoil; his captured status from the Beast drives Mina’s quest; aims to free himself from captivity and overcome The Beast; fights for survival against the Beast by using strategy and embracing his inner power; questions if he has crossed a point of no return here on the Watcher; follows a prophecy to discover the truth behind mysterious events; seeks to understand the mansion and its strange visions; trying to make sense of a disorienting situation and move forward despite uncertainty.

Secret

Struggling with memory of the Kingston explosion and guilt over its consequences; he, Mina, and Thomas each have a fused relic; possibly misled about being the chosen one for the relics; resentful about political conflicts; deep mistrust of elves and dwarves due to past manipulations, slowly fading towards the elves; has a minor anomaly in his DNA leading to instability in relic synchronization; doubts his worthiness for the relics; the relic’s effects on him remain unclear.

Combat Training

Strategic and warfare knowledge; telekinetic defense/offense/utility; basic weapon training; intermediate pyrokinesis and intermediate telekinesis; skilled with basic weaponry; moderate combat training; tactical and strategic thinker from deep study; capable in a leadership context; shown to have powers that he struggles to control; effective combat reactions and tactical thinking.
