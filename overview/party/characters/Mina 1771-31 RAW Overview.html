No birth year has been provided for Mina.

Role/Background

Mina is a member of the traveling group and serves as Lucas’s companion. She experienced a mysterious disappearance, during which she suffered from fever and memory loss. Upon reappearing, she exhibited an unusual perception of energy and began having prophetic visions, suggesting a connection to events beyond the ordinary.

Personality Traits

Mina comes off as confused and quiet, yet she is keenly observant. She often displays frustration and a sense of urgency, balanced by a practical approach to her challenges. Her sensitivity and empathy underline her concern for others, making her a complex character in the midst of mysterious events.

Abilities/Magic

Mina has the remarkable ability to perceive strange energy presences. She moves with an unusual ease, seemingly defying the normal physical limitations imposed by cold or exhaustion. Additionally, she experiences clairvoyant visions that hint at events and truths not immediately visible to others.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

As a member of the traveling group, Mina is aligned with the collective journey and mission of her companions, which includes her close association with Lucas.

Appearance

In a vision observed from younger Ceylan’s perspective, Mina is depicted as tall and impossibly thin, with wavy skin. Her long, multi-jointed fingers contribute to an otherworldly and ethereal presence.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina’s journey is driven by her need to understand the cause and nature of her mysterious disappearance. She aims to rejoin the group as they head toward the Pass of the Hawk, hoping to unravel cryptic messages and gain clarity on her prophetic visions, all while supporting Lucas in his quest for answers.
