Birth Year

Mina was born in 1754, making her 17 years old in the current year of 1771.

Role/Background

Mina is an adventurer and one of the Heroes of Brum'korath. She is telepathically connected to Lucas, playing a pivotal role in his journey and the prophecy of the Children of Hybris.

Personality Traits

Mina is fiercely independent, but deeply empathetic and observant. She is brave and optimistic, often offering grounding support to Lucas, who trusts her implicitly. Her loyalty and protective nature define her relationships.

Abilities/Magic

Mina possesses superhuman speed and agility, allowing her to perform acrobatic feats and evade opponents with ease. Her heightened senses are a double-edged sword, being difficult to control. She also shares a telepathic link with Lucas, giving her unique insight into his thoughts. Her combat style is centered around speed-based tactics, overwhelming foes through swift movements and hit-and-run techniques.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Mina is allied with Lucas, with whom she shares a strong, telepathic bond. She respects and trusts Thomas, with whom she has fought alongside. While she is indirectly connected to the Cult of the Sent Ones due to her role in the prophecy, she is not yet allied with Candice, who remains uncertain about Mina’s role.

Appearance

Mina has striking green eyes and bright blonde hair styled practically. Her athletic build is the result of years of training and experience, though it has left her weathered and resilient. She carries herself with a calm demeanor, often focusing intensely on the task at hand. While strong, she is not overly aggressive, preferring to approach challenges with careful consideration.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina’s primary goal is to protect her friends, particularly Lucas and Thomas. She seeks to understand her role in the Children of Hybris prophecy, though uncertainty about her future and responsibilities weighs heavily on her. As she unravels the mystery of her true purpose, she is forced to confront her own insecurities and doubts.

Secret

Mina struggles with uncertainty about her role in the prophecy and what it means for her future and the world. She feels conflicted about her place in the larger story and fears the consequences of her actions.

Combat Training

Mina is highly skilled in athletic combat, specializing in speed-based tactics. Her agility allows her to outmaneuver opponents, often using acrobatics and quick strikes to disorient and overwhelm her enemies.

Inventory

Mina carries 90 doumis, practical travel clothes, survival gear, a dagger for close-quarters combat, and a relic tied to the prophecy of the Sent Ones that she cannot remove.
