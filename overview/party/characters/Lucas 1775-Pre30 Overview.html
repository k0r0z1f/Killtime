Birth Year

Lucas was born in the year 1754. He is currently 21 years old.

Role/Background

Lucas is a central hero in the world of Brum'korath and has played significant roles in the Cults War, as well as dealing with the threat of the Disciples. His involvement with the Sent Ones prophecy and connection to ancient relics makes him a key player in his world's unfolding events.

Personality Traits

Lucas is a deeply introspective and determined character, carrying the weight of his past mistakes and the powerful abilities he struggles to control. He is complex and torn between his role as a hero and the burden of his powers, often questioning his worth and potential. His strong will and protective nature drive him to support his companions, even when he feels conflicted.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas possesses advanced telepathic abilities, starting from surface-level thought reading to full manipulation of thoughts and memories. His Clairvoyance allows him to glimpse the past and future, while his Telekinesis lets him move objects with his mind. Lucas also has limited Pyrokinesis, capable of creating and controlling fire, and his connection to the relics gives him even more potential for magical abilities.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas has strong ties with several key factions, including the Children of Hybris, the Resistance against the Empire, and various allies such as Mina, Thomas, and Dira. He has also worked closely with the Dwarven Council and Brum'korath, though his past with the World Police was adversarial.

Appearance

Lucas is 5'10" tall with brown eyes and disheveled light-brown hair. He has a lean physique and often appears stressed or focused, his expression showing the mental and emotional fatigue from his burdens. He carries a satchel with relic information and personal tokens.

Character Arc/Goal

Lucas is focused on finding redemption, learning about his powers, and understanding his place in the prophecy. His struggle revolves around overcoming the fear of his own destructive powers and coming to terms with his past mistakes, all while protecting those he cares about and uncovering the mysteries surrounding the relics.

Secret

Lucas feels haunted by the consequences of his past actions, particularly in relation to the relics and the people he loves. He struggles with the guilt of losing his fused relic and the consequences that followed, including the scattering of his companions.

Combat Training

Lucas has advanced strategic and tactical knowledge, telekinetic combat skills, and experience with weaponry, relying heavily on his powers in battle. He is capable of leading teams in high-pressure situations, utilizing both his wits and his telekinetic abilities.

Inventory

Lucas keeps various relics and magi-tech parts related to his powers. His satchel contains notes, prophecies, and relic data. He also carries memorial tokens and remnants of his lost fused relic, as well as various small gifts from supporters.
