Birth Year

Born in 1753, Thomas is currently 17 years old in the year 1771.

Role/Background

Thomas is a leader and strategist, known as a Hero of Brum'korath. Once a student with an athletic background, he is now a fugitive from the Central Lands, marked as an enemy of the state. A member of Lucas's group, he is working to unlock relics and stop the Disciple of Earth.

Personality Traits

Thomas is a highly strategic and level-headed individual, with a protective nature towards his companions. Though skeptical of cults, he remains calm and confident under pressure. He is driven by a deep sense of purpose, observing situations carefully and acting pragmatically to ensure the survival of his friends.

Abilities/Magic

Thomas does not possess magical abilities, relying instead on his combat training, leadership, and tactical mind.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thomas is closely allied with Lucas and Mina, and although he is indirectly linked to the Cult of the Sent Ones through prophecy, he considers them an indirect part of his journey. He is also allied with the Brum'korath and is an enemy of the World Police.

Appearance

Standing at 6'5" with a broad, athletic build, Thomas's imposing presence is evident. His sharp eyes reflect his focus, and he often dresses in worn clothing suited for travel and survival.

Character Arc/Goal

Thomas’s primary goal is to protect his companions, Lucas and Mina, and help uncover the truth behind the relics. He acts as a protector and strategist, performing heroic feats to ensure the safety of his group. Throughout the journey, he struggles with his morality in a time of war.

Secret

Although Thomas is suspicious of the cult’s influence, he also feels something changing within him due to the relic bound to his wrist. He remains unaware that the relic is affecting his behavior, and struggles with the moral dilemmas surrounding the war.

Combat Training

Trained extensively in combat, Thomas is skilled in both swordplay and hand-to-hand combat. His tactical leadership has proven invaluable in battle, especially in ensuring the survival of his group during conflicts.

Inventory

Thomas carries 86 doumis, a backpack with survival gear, and weapons (sword and shield). He is also bound to a powerful relic, which may play a significant role in his journey.
