Birth Year

Lucas' birth year is currently unknown.

Role / Background

Lucas is one of the Children of Hybris, central to a prophecy. He is a member of the group preparing for the Eastern Continent expedition, working as part of a team tasked with unlocking the relics' power to stop the Disciple of Air. His connection to the relic bound to his wrist plays a pivotal role in this mission.

Personality Traits

Lucas is a critical and cautious individual, often questioning things around him. He's thoughtful, wary, and at times, frustrated with the group's progress. Despite being burdened by responsibility, he is determined and focused on his mission. While he's occasionally reckless, Lucas is driven by a strong sense of duty, becoming more willing to take risks as he grows comfortable with the team.

Abilities / Magic

Lucas possesses telekinesis, enabling him to move objects with his mind. He has unexplained powers linked to the relic bound to his wrist, and a telepathic connection with Mina. He can sense the power within the Relics, and it seems that his connection to them may grant him other abilities if he can unlock their full potential.

Alliances / Clans / Factions

Lucas is part of the team preparing for the Eastern Continent expedition, as well as potentially connected to the Children of Hybris. He works alongside a group of scientists and researchers on a critical mission involving the Relics and the Disciple of Air.

Appearance

Lucas has light-brown, disheveled hair and often wears a worn satchel. His expression is typically stressed or focused, reflecting the weight of his responsibility and the ongoing mystery surrounding the relics.

Character Arc / Goal

Lucas' primary goal is to understand his role in the prophecy and the significance of the Relics. He seeks to unlock their potential and prevent the Disciple of Air from succeeding in their mission. Along the way, he must confront his doubts and uncertainties about his worthiness to wield the power of the relics.

Secret

Lucas hides the truth of his connection to the relic, its potential powers, and parts of the prophecy. He also harbors a minor DNA anomaly that makes his synchronization with the relic unstable, casting doubt on his readiness to handle the immense power bound to him.

Combat Training

Lucas has basic combat training, which is sufficient for him to lead in certain situations. He has shown the ability to defend himself, though his primary focus remains on the mission and the Relics.

Inventory

Lucas' most important possession is the relic bound to his wrist, which may hold immense power. He also carries a satchel containing notes, theories, and scraps of information about the Relics, providing key insights into their potential and purpose.
