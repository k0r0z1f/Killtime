Birth Year

The exact birth year of Lucas remains unknown.

Role/Background

Lucas is a former prisoner of Brum'korath, now an integral member of the party. He has connections to ancient relics, is a skilled strategist, and carries a telepathic link to Candice. His past as an outlaw intertwines with his current path, making him both an ally and a wildcard in the group.

Personality Traits

Lucas is determined and burdened by guilt, always seeking redemption. His introspective and calculating nature often makes him cautious, but he is brave and empathetic when it matters. His skeptical outlook and calmness under pressure make him a reliable but sometimes distant ally.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas possesses telepathic abilities, enabling him to sense chaotic thoughts and mental presences. His permanent telepathic link with Mina is a unique and powerful connection, adding layers of complexity to his role in the party.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas has ties to the Dwarven Council, Brum'korath, and the World Police (now his enemies). He has formed a temporary alliance with Haliriel, navigating through complex political dynamics and seeking leverage where possible.

Appearance

Lucas has brown hair and a lean build, which reflects his pragmatic nature and ability to blend into various environments.

Character Arc/Goal

Lucas seeks redemption for the explosion in Kingston and to uncover the truth behind the ancient relics. He is driven to understand and unlock their powers, navigate complex political and personal motivations, and protect his companions while preparing for the coming war.

Secret

Lucas struggles with the guilt of the Kingston explosion and harbors deep mistrust due to being caught up in political conflict. He is also conflicted about his role in the prophecy surrounding the relics and the potential manipulation behind his involvement.

Combat Training

Lucas has basic combat training and is skilled in tactics. His strategic thinking and knowledge from deep study make him a valuable asset in the field, even though his physical combat skills are moderate.
