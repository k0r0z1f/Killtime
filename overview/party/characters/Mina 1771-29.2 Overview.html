Birth Year

Mina was born in 1754, making her 17 years old at the time of the story. She is a Child of Hybris, tied to a larger prophecy that shapes her destiny.

Role/Background

- Adopted daughter of a powerful businessman and incognito criminal boss.
- Her pivotal role was revealed during the Elven Council meeting, marking her as a key figure in the prophecy.
- She is one of the Children of Hybris alongside Lucas.
- Mina provides emotional support to Lucas, deeply tied to him through a telepathic bond.
- She is highly involved in the mission concerning the Watcher and the prophecy of the Sent Ones.
- Determined to uncover the truth about the Watcher, believing it is connected to her fate.
- Has demonstrated leadership and confidence during critical trials.

Personality Traits

- Calm, composed, and logical but also displays emotional depth.
- Steady, supportive, sharp, and loyal; provides a grounding presence for those around her.
- Fiercely autonomous, yet her permanent telepathic bond with Lucas has made her slightly dependent on him.
- Empathetic and observant, able to pick up on subtle emotional cues.
- Brave, facing danger with careful thought; optimistic, seeking hope in dire situations.
- Playful and teasing in her interactions with Lucas, using humor to strengthen their bond.
- Embraces reverence during spiritual moments (e.g., Aria’s ritual).
- Determined, caring, and struggles with the weight of the prophecy but remains unwavering.
- Exhibits youthful resilience and a measure of uncertainty, reflecting her age.

Abilities/Magic

- Superhuman Speed and Agility: Moves exceptionally fast and can execute acrobatic maneuvers.
- Hyper-sensitive Senses: Feels magic and environment changes more intensely, which can be overwhelming.
- Telepathic Link with Lucas: A permanent mental connection, allowing shared thoughts and emotions.
- Athletic Combat (Speed-Based Tactics): Relies on quick, agile movements and hit-and-run strategies.
- Strong coordination in group challenges, especially when working with Lucas via their shared bond.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

- Lucas: Deep trust, telepathically connected partner.
- Thomas: Mutual respect and trust, part of the prophecy circle.
- Cult of the Sent Ones (Indirect): Involvement through her role as a Child of Hybris.
- Candice: Cautious trust after only three days of acquaintance; they share the same mission.
- Dira: Considers her a friend but remains watchful due to Dira’s past actions.
- Also indirectly connected to her father’s criminal enterprises, but seeks to use that power for justice.
- Tied to the Nythari Tribe’s spiritual traditions through the prophecy.

Appearance

- Bright green eyes, distinctive and full of determination.
- Bright blonde hair styled practically for agility and combat.
- Athletic build with a strong physique, carrying a few scars from past experiences.
- Youthful but resolute presence; can appear calm and graceful, shifting to playful at times.
- Alternates between lighthearted confidence and a reverent demeanor in significant moments.

Character Arc/Goal

- Struggles to understand her true purpose in the Children of Hybris prophecy.
- Determined to protect Lucas, Thomas, and her allies while unraveling the mystery of the Watcher.
- Confronts uncertainty about her future and responsibilities as a prophesized figure.
- Seeks self-discovery: balancing her desired independence with the weight of her destiny.
- Aims to reassure and strengthen Lucas as he wrestles with the prophecy’s burdens.
- Embraces both playful challenges and moments of profound significance to maintain group morale.

Secret

- Mina was born during a catastrophic arcane storm that destroyed a hospital, leaving only her and one other boy alive.
- She has not yet shared this detail with Lucas, unsure of how it might affect their bond.
- Her miraculous survival deepens the mystery of her connection to the prophecy.

Combat Training

- Specializes in athletic, speed-based combat, executing hit-and-run techniques.
- Well-trained due to her father’s support, focusing on agility over raw strength.
- Uses her exceptional dexterity to outmaneuver opponents, leveraging acrobatic feats and quick reflexes.

Inventory

- 90 doumis (local currency).
- Practical travel clothes suited for diverse terrains.
- Survival gear, including essentials for wilderness journeys.
- A dagger for close-quarters protection.
- A relic tied to the prophecy of the Sent Ones, which she cannot remove.
- Light silk robe and simple camisole for comfort on the road.
- A small token gifted by villagers they encountered.

Lucas

- Best friend and most significant companion; they share a permanent telepathic link.
- Engaged in playful teasing to maintain levity in tense situations.
- Mina provides emotional support and grounding for Lucas, helping him focus on their goals.
- Their bond suggests deeper feelings, though complicated by the prophecy and their young age.

Thomas

- Trusted ally, valued for his strategic mind and unwavering support.
- Shares in the responsibilities of protecting one another, part of the prophecy group.
- Respects Thomas for his loyalty and calm reliability in dire situations.

Candice

- Acquainted for only three days, but they share a common mission related to the prophecy.
- Mina respects Candice’s abilities while remaining cautious due to the latter’s past betrayal experiences.
- Recognizes Candice’s importance in their group, especially regarding the mission to the Watcher.

Dira

- Has known Dira for less than a month; they have become friends but trust is still developing.
- Aware of Dira’s troubled past and hopes she finds a path to self-forgiveness.
- Appreciates Dira’s presence, but maintains caution due to the unpredictability of their quest.
