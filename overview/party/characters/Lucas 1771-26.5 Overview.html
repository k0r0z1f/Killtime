Birth Year

1754

Role/Background

Hero, former Brum'korath prisoner, member of the party, connected to ancient relics, strategist, outlaw, telepathic link to Candice; victim of medi-laboratory experiments as a child; aspiring college student as a teen; escapee since Kingston incident.

Personality Traits

Brave, analytical, self-doubting, burdened by hidden truths, determined, calm, protective, intense, frustrated, burdened by guilt, feels the weight of scrutiny, cautious, introspective, practical, calculating, doubtful, inquisitive, anxious, resolute, thoughtful, skeptical, calm under pressure, empathetic, observant, curious, uncertain, brave.

Abilities/Magic

Telepathy (Stage 1: Surface Thought Reading - DMN-Focused, Stage 2: Deeper Thought Access and Emotional Sensory Tuning, Stage 3: Memory Access and Short-Term Influence), Entry-level Clairvoyance (Unconscious or Basic Vision), Basic Telekinesis (Multiple Small Object Movement), Pyrokinesis: Entry - Small Heat Control, Basic - Flame Creation, Intermediate - Fire Manipulation; senses chaotic thoughts and mental presences, permanent telepathic link with Mina; connection to the Sent Ones prophecy.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Aligned with Mina and Thomas; *Cult of the Sent Ones* (indirectly, through prophecy); Dwarven Council, Brum'korath, World Police (now enemy), Haliriel (temporary alliance).

Appearance

5'10", brown eyes, short light-brown hair, serious look; lean.

Character Arc/Goal

Lucas' arc revolves around overcoming insecurities, embracing his potential, and coming to terms with his connection to the Sent Ones prophecy. He faces increasing pressure from his role, the growing war, and the dangerous relics he is bound to.

Secret

Lucas harbors guilt over the Kingston explosion and struggles with the consequences. His connection to the relics is more complex than he initially believed, and his role in the prophecy is under question. He also feels betrayed by both the elves and dwarves, whose manipulation has left deep scars.

Combat Training

Lucas is a skilled strategist with moderate combat training. He is capable of telekinetic defense, basic pyrokinesis, and strategic warfare knowledge. His training makes him a valuable asset in tactical operations.

Inventory

Lucas carries a mix of personal and magical items: 1,000 doumis, a rare battery hybrid magic-tech worth 20,000 doumis, new travel clothes, a backpack with survival gear, dry food, a change of clothes, and relics tied to his wrist.
