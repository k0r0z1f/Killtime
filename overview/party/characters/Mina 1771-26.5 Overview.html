Birth Year

1754

Role/Background

Mina is an adventurer, hero of Brum'korath, and a key party member. She is a protector and prophecy bearer, allied with Lucas and Thomas, and plays an important role in navigating the complex web of fate. Her pragmatic nature helps ground her companions, and she is possibly tied to a larger, more mysterious destiny.

Personality Traits

Mina is fiercely independent and often skeptical, yet optimistic. Her personality is a complex mixture of intuition, protectiveness, and determination. She is observant and thoughtful, balancing recklessness with empathy, and providing a steady influence on Lucas. Mina is unafraid of the law, but her defiance often comes with subtle fears about trust and destiny.

Abilities/Magic

Mina possesses a permanent telepathic connection with Lucas, enabling them to communicate silently. She has enhanced speed and agility due to a magical growth incident, and her senses are hypersensitive, though somewhat uncontrollable. These powers grant her a unique edge in combat, though they can be unpredictable.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Mina is allied with Lucas and Thomas. While she is indirectly tied to the *Cult of the Sent Ones* through prophecy, her relationship with this faction is more complex and uncertain.

Appearance

Mina has green eyes, a strong but worn demeanor, and a fierce posture. She is 5'11" with an athletic build, her very bright blonde hair tied in a tight ponytail. She has a magnetic presence and a warm smile, though faint scars remain from past injuries to her head and abdomen.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina's goal is to protect her friends and navigate the complex prophecy. She faces the challenge of learning to balance personal freedom with the weight of surveillance and responsibility, all while preparing for the war and discovering more about her connection to the larger fate unfolding around her.

Secret

Mina is unsure about her role in the prophecy and harbors doubts about who she can truly trust, a struggle that impacts her decisions as she moves forward in her journey.

Combat Training

Mina is highly trained in athletic combat, including track and martial arts. She uses speed-based tactics and is preparing for larger-scale battles and responsibilities in the war, readying herself for the physical and mental challenges ahead.

Inventory

Mina carries 90 doumis, new travel clothes, a backpack with survival gear, dry food, a change of clothes, a dagger, and a relic she has yet to fully understand.
