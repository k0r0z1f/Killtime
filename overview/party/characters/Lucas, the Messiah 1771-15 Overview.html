Unspecified

Role/Background

Lucas is the central figure of the mission, known as the 'messiah' figure. His powers are significant and potentially world-changing, capable of disrupting Imperial systems and possibly altering the fate of entire factions.

Personality Traits

Lucas is weighed down and hollow, yet determined. Despite his burden, he forms subtle emotional connections, particularly with Erika, who plays a key role in his journey. His personality reflects his deep internal struggle.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas has world-altering potential, with the ability to disrupt Imperial systems. The full extent of his powers is still unknown, but they include powers that could influence the fate of the factions involved in his journey.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas is aligned with Erika, who is likely connected to the Resistance. His involvement with these factions shapes his journey and the mission he is a part of.

Appearance

Lucas's appearance is unspecified, though it is noted that his hair obscures his face, and he appears physically drained and bound. His current state reflects his struggle and the pressure of the mission.

Character Arc/Goal

Lucas’s primary goal is to find Mina, which is a life-or-death situation for him. Throughout the mission, he seeks survival and protection, while his powers slowly begin to awaken and could change the outcome of the factions involved.

Secret

Lucas’s true abilities remain hidden. He is starting to unlock his powers, such as the ability to disrupt Imperial signals. However, the full scope of his powers is still unknown, and his potential impact on the world is unclear.
