Birth Year

Thomas was born in 1753. At 17 years old, he is a young but capable leader.

Role/Background

Thomas is the Hero of Brum’korath, a former student with an athletic background, and a fugitive from the Central Lands. He is marked as an enemy of the state. Thomas plays a key role as a pragmatic protector within his group, assisting with physical challenges during their ascent of the Watcher and defending during crises. He is responsible for Lucas and Mina’s well-being and actively works with Lucas to unlock relics and stop the Disciple.

Personality Traits

Thomas embodies a range of traits including strategic thinking, level-headedness, protectiveness, and resilience. He is calm under pressure, commanding, and quick to act when needed. However, he is also resolute, sometimes impatient, and carries an air of authority. His practicality and determination shine through as he balances his responsibilities and inner struggles.

Abilities/Magic

Thomas is physically strong and capable of shielding others in combat. He wields a sword and a relic bound to his wrist. The relic transforms into a cylindrical, piston-like device with otherworldly power, which he uses strategically in battle. His combat adaptability and tactical leadership make him a formidable force.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thomas is a crucial member of Lucas’s group and an ally of Brum’korath. He has been declared an enemy of the World Police, adding complexity to his journey. He is also tied to the climbing group known as Candice’s group, all of whom aim to reach the Watcher’s cave.

Appearance

Standing tall at 6'5", Thomas has a broad, athletic build and sharp eyes that convey his commanding presence. He wears practical clothing suited for travel and survival, emphasizing his role as a protector and strategist.

Character Arc/Goal

Thomas’s journey is marked by his resolve to protect his friends, especially Lucas and Mina, while confronting the Disciple of Earth. He struggles with the relic’s influence on his morality and decision-making, creating internal conflict. Thomas aims to ensure his group’s survival and success, navigate the political challenges of Brum’korath, and uncover the truth behind the prophecy. His ultimate goal is to ascend the Watcher’s mountain, protect his companions, and stop the larger catastrophe posed by the Minulican and the Beast.

Secret

Thomas harbors suspicions about the cult’s influence and feels guilt over the relic’s impact on his morality and decisions. He struggles with isolation due to these internal battles and fears the potential consequences for his friends.

Combat Training

Thomas has extensive combat training, excelling in swordsmanship, hand-to-hand combat, and battlefield strategy. He is adept at using his relic in unpredictable situations and remains vigilant and adaptive during battles.

Inventory

Thomas’s inventory reflects his meticulous preparation. He carries a sword, shield, relic bound to his wrist, survival gear, rope, sturdy boots, and 86 doumis. These items ensure he is ready for any challenges that arise during his journey.

Lucas

Lucas is Thomas’s best friend and a source of hope and purpose for him. Thomas is fiercely protective of Lucas and relies on him for guidance and support in their journey.

Mina

Mina is another of Thomas’s closest friends. He admires her capabilities and strength, striving to balance his role as her protector with respecting her independence.

Candice

Candice is a recent acquaintance of Thomas, having known her for only four days. Thomas harbors an unconscious crush on her, which subtly influences his actions and decisions, adding complexity to their interactions.

Dira

Dira has been known to Thomas for less than a month. Though cautious of her initially, Thomas respects her as long as she proves truly repentant. He finds her insight valuable but is also somewhat intimidated by her presence.
