Birth Year

Thomas was born in the year 1753.

Role/Background

Thomas is a leader and companion in the group, having fought against the Disciple in Brum'korath. A strategist by nature, he was once a student with an athletic background and a fugitive in his past. He is now marked as an enemy of the state and continues to fight for survival.

Personality Traits

Thomas possesses a complex range of traits. He is strategic, level-headed, and protective, though often skeptical of external influences, particularly cults. He remains calm under pressure, but his inner frustration and impatience occasionally surface. He struggles with a sense of isolation, especially in terms of his beliefs. His leadership role is reinforced by the relic he wears, although its influence on him is unknown.

Abilities/Magic

Thomas has no magic abilities, relying on his combat skills, intellect, and leadership abilities to guide the group.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

He is closely aligned with Lucas and Mina, and also indirectly connected to the Cult of the Sent Ones through prophecy. He has allies in Brum'korath and enemies in the World Police.

Appearance

Thomas stands tall at 6'5" with a broad, athletic build. He has sharp, observant eyes and an imposing presence. He tends to dress practically in worn clothing suited for travel and combat.

Character Arc/Goal

As a protector and strategist, Thomas works to keep the group safe, performing heroic deeds to ensure their survival. His personal journey revolves around navigating complex political landscapes, understanding his place in the prophecy, and coming to terms with the moral dilemmas posed by the war. His actions have been pivotal in defeating the Disciple, and he remains dedicated to uncovering the truth and securing the future of his friends and the world.

Secret

Thomas is suspicious of the cult's influence and struggles with the moral implications of the ongoing war. He feels increasingly isolated in his beliefs and is aware that the relic he wears is changing him in ways he doesn't fully understand.

Combat Training

Thomas has extensive combat training, including tactical leadership and proficiency with sword combat. His strategic mind and experience on the battlefield make him a formidable leader in combat.

Inventory

Thomas carries 90 doumis, a backpack with survival gear, a sword and shield for defense, and the relic that is bound to his wrist. The relic is a crucial part of his character, though its full influence on him remains a mystery.
