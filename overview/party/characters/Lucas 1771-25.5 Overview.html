Birth Year

Lucas was born in 1754. His early life was marked by trauma, beginning with his involvement in experimental projects that shaped his abilities and perspective.

Role/Background

Lucas's past includes being a victim of medi-laboratory experiments as a child. By his teen years, he aspired to be a college student, but his life changed drastically after the Kingston incident. Since then, Lucas has been on the run, trying to understand his abilities and his place in the world. His role as a key figure in the Sent Ones prophecy becomes central to his journey.

Personality Traits

Lucas is a deeply conflicted character with many layers to his personality:

  • He is brave, always stepping up to face danger even when uncertain.
  • Lucas is analytical, often thinking through situations from multiple angles before taking action.
  • His self-doubt can sometimes hold him back, but his determination pushes him forward.
  • He feels burdened by hidden truths about his past, leading him to keep many things to himself.
  • Despite these struggles, he remains calm in the face of adversity, though he is prone to moments of intense frustration.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas possesses several telepathic and psychic abilities, though they evolve as the story progresses:

  • Telepathy: Stage 1 involves surface thought reading, focused on DMN signals. Stage 2 allows deeper thought access and emotional sensory tuning. Stage 3 grants memory access and limited influence over short-term actions.
  • Clairvoyance: Lucas has basic, unconscious visions that sometimes provide glimpses of the future or hidden truths.
  • Telekinesis: He can move small objects with concentration, but only within his line of sight. As his powers grow, he gains greater control over multiple objects.
  • Pyrokinesis: At the basic level, Lucas can control the temperature of objects. He can generate small flames and manipulate fire at an intermediate level, creating moderate fireballs and other fire constructs.
  • He also has a potential connection to the Cult of the Sent Ones, which remains a mystery to him.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas is aligned with Mina and Thomas, forming a strong bond with them as they face various challenges together. He is indirectly tied to the Cult of the Sent Ones through prophecy, though the implications of this connection remain unclear.

Appearance

Lucas is described as being 5'10" tall with brown eyes and short, light-brown hair. His serious demeanor is often reflected in his appearance, marking him as someone who has experienced considerable hardship in his young life.

Character Arc/Goal

Lucas's journey is one of self-discovery. He struggles with insecurity, especially when confronted with his powers and his role in the Sent Ones prophecy. Throughout the story, he works to overcome these feelings and embrace his potential. The growing pressure of the coming war and the mystery surrounding the Disciple weigh heavily on him as he strives to find his place in the world and to face the truths about his past.

Secret

Lucas hides several truths about himself. A laboratory researcher is hunting him, knowing much about his origins and abilities. He is also connected to the Sent Ones prophecy, though he doesn't fully understand the significance. Additionally, Lucas is keeping a secret from Tolgar, further adding to the mystery surrounding his past.

Combat Training

Lucas has received strategic and warfare knowledge, particularly related to his telekinetic abilities. He has basic weapon training and has been prepared for the war to come. His telekinetic powers provide him with a natural defense, and his pyrokinesis allows him to contribute offensively in battle. His training is essential as the conflict intensifies.
