Role/Background

Thomas is a fighter and protector who is part of a traveling group. He acts as a skeptical observer and notably catches Dira when she slips.

Personality Traits

Thomas is determined, frustrated, protective, impatient, sarcastic, confrontational, anxious, observant, and cautious. These traits collectively underscore his vigilant nature and readiness to engage in conflict when necessary.

Abilities/Magic

He can perform a relic-enhanced punch, and his relic-covered arm is capable of absorbing impacts.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thomas is a member of the traveling group, which plays a central role in his life as a protector.

Appearance

He is characterized by a broad frame and physical strength. His visible breath in cold air, clenched fists, and tense posture emphasize his readiness for combat.

Character Arc/Goal

Thomas’s primary goal is to protect others and defeat the Beast. He is determined to find Mina and Lucas, uncover the truth behind past events, remain vigilant, and he remains suspicious of the sudden changes in Lucas and Mina, prompting him to question Ceylan’s actions.
