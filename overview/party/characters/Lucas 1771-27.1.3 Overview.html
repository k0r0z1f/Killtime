Birth Year

Lucas was born in 1754, making him 17 years old in the current year, 1771.

Role/Background

Lucas is the Hero of Brum'korath and was involved in the Cults War and the Disciple's rampage. Once a prisoner in Brum'korath, he is now free but an outlaw in the Central Lands due to his involvement in the Kingston incident. Lucas is also connected to an ancient relics prophecy, one of the "Children of Hybris," and has been fused with one of the relics. He was also a victim of scientific experiments as a child.

Personality Traits

Lucas is a determined, introspective, and strategic thinker, but he struggles with self-doubt, feeling burdened by hidden truths and guilt. He is practical, cautious, and calm under pressure, but also curious, inquisitive, and often anxious. He is protective of those he cares about, especially Mina and Thomas, and feels the weight of scrutiny due to his role in the prophecy.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas possesses advanced telepathy (with varying stages of mastery), entry-level clairvoyance, and intermediate telekinesis. He can read surface thoughts, influence emotions, access memories, and move small objects or one larger object (up to 30kg). His pyrokinesis allows him to manipulate fire, from creating small flames to controlling fire. He also has a permanent telepathic link with Mina, allowing them to communicate over great distances.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas has alliances with Mina and Thomas, with whom he shares a deep bond. He is indirectly aligned with the Cult of the Sent Ones through the prophecy, and has connections with the Dwarven Council and Brum'korath. However, he is an enemy of the World Police after the Kingston incident. His alliances are constantly shifting as the political landscape changes.

Appearance

Lucas stands 5'10" with brown eyes and short light-brown hair. His appearance gives off a serious, intense look, often reflecting the burden he carries due to his past and current situation. He is lean and physically fit, with the agility to match his strategic mind.

Character Arc/Goal

Lucas is on a journey of self-discovery, trying to understand his role in the prophecy, overcome his insecurities, and unlock the powers tied to the ancient relics. His path involves grappling with the consequences of the Kingston explosion, uncovering hidden truths, and preparing for the war that looms ahead. He must navigate the complex political landscape while protecting his companions and uncovering the mysteries that bind him to the world and to others like Candice.

Secret

Lucas harbors guilt over the Kingston explosion, the details of which remain a mystery. He may have been misled about being the chosen one for the relics and has deep resentment about being caught up in political conflict. He feels the weight of the world on his shoulders as he tries to piece together his past.

Combat Training

Lucas has moderate combat skills, including strategic thinking and telekinetic defense. He has received basic training in weaponry and pyrokinesis, as well as combat tactics. His greatest strength lies in his ability to think strategically and use his telekinetic powers to defend and control the battlefield.

Inventory

Lucas carries 812 doumis, a rare hybrid magic-tech battery worth 20,000 doumis, travel clothes, survival gear, dry food, a change of clothes, and a relic tied to his wrist. These items are essential for his survival and his journey ahead.
