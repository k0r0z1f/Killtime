Birth Year

Mina was born in 1754 and is currently 17 years old.

Role/Background

Mina is the adopted daughter of a powerful businessman and an incognito criminal boss. She is a key figure in the Children of Hybris prophecy, providing emotional support to Lucas. Mina serves as a scout and lookout for the party while ascending the Watcher. Her connection to relics and the prophecy defines much of her role in the group's mission.

Personality Traits

Mina is calm, composed, and logical, with a steady and supportive demeanor. She is empathetic, observant, and brave, often grounding Lucas emotionally. Her playful and quick-witted nature contrasts with her deep reverence during spiritual moments. She is fiercely autonomous but values her bond with Lucas, even as she struggles with her dependence on him.

Abilities/Magic

Mina possesses superhuman speed and agility, along with a telepathic link to Lucas, amplifying his telekinetic powers. She has heightened sensitivity to magical forces and exceptional coordination, making her a formidable scout and fighter. Her athletic combat style relies on speed-based tactics, such as hit-and-run maneuvers.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Mina is deeply connected to Lucas, Thomas, Candice, and the Children of Hybris prophecy. She respects and cautiously trusts Dira, despite past betrayals. Her alliances include indirect ties to the Cult of the Sent Ones and alignment with Queen Aphyrosia and Altheris. The group seeks the wisdom of Ceylan at the Watcher.

Appearance

Mina has bright blonde hair styled practically, striking green eyes, and an athletic build. She carries scars from her experiences and exudes a determined, confident presence. Her demeanor shifts between playful teasing and solemn reverence during critical moments.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina’s arc revolves around balancing her autonomy with the prophecy’s weight. She seeks to uncover the Watcher’s truth and her role in the Children of Hybris. Her goals include protecting Lucas and her friends, embracing her spiritual journey, and finding her true purpose amidst uncertainty.

Secret

Mina was born during an arcane storm that destroyed a hospital, leaving her and another boy as the only survivors. This event ties her to the prophecy, but she has not yet revealed this to Lucas. The storm’s energy affects her, leading to levitation and other mysterious phenomena.

Combat Training

Mina’s combat style is rooted in agility and speed-based tactics. She uses hit-and-run techniques and acrobatics, leveraging her athletic background. While not relying on brute strength, her strong legs make her a formidable opponent in close-quarters combat.

Inventory

Mina carries practical travel clothes, survival gear, a compact dagger, and 90 doumis. She also possesses a relic tied to the prophecy, which she cannot remove. Her inventory reflects her role as a scout and her preparedness for challenges during the group’s journey.

Relationships

Lucas: Mina shares a telepathic bond with Lucas and deeply trusts him. Their relationship is marked by playful teasing, emotional support, and unwavering trust.
Thomas: Mina respects Thomas and relies on his strategic thinking. Their camaraderie is built on mutual trust and friendship.
Candice: Although Mina has only known Candice for four days, she respects her and recognizes her importance to the mission.
Dira: Mina sees Dira as a friend but remains cautious due to Dira’s past betrayal. She hopes Dira can forgive herself and move forward.
