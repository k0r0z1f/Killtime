Birth Year

Lucas was born in the year 1754. He is currently 21 years old.

Role/Background

Lucas is a central figure in an ancient prophecy, referred to as "the messiah" and "the key to the future." He was formerly a prisoner in the Cults War and is now a hero of Brum'korath, deeply involved in the Resistance against the Empire. He is a key guest in the Resistance's strategies, facing overwhelming challenges in escaping the Empire's pursuit.

Personality Traits

Lucas is introspective and often torn between his role as a hero and the burdens of his powers and past mistakes. He is determined, brave, and protective, but also suffers from anxiety, mental exhaustion, self-doubt, and guilt. He struggles with his emotions and powers, making him wary of his abilities and future.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas possesses several potent abilities, including:

  • Telepathy: Unconscious thought reading and the ability to manipulate thoughts and memories.
  • Telekinesis: Ability to manipulate energy and objects, including levitation and creating pressure waves.
  • Pyrokinesis: Control over fire, though still in early stages of mastery.
  • Clairvoyance: Mastery of seeing past and future events, though it comes with an emotional toll.
  • Additional Abilities: Energy surges that disable machinery, aura shielding, and environmental manipulation.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas is allied with several factions, including Brum'korath, Haliriel, Nythari elves, and the Dwarven Council. He is also involved with the World Police (formerly an enemy, now paroled) and is a key figure in the Resistance against the Empire.

Appearance

Lucas is 5'10" with light-brown hair and brown eyes. He has a lean build and, when conscious, appears stressed, pale, and worn from mental and emotional fatigue. He often has shadows under his eyes, but when unconscious, he levitates and glows with an ethereal, bright light.

Character Arc/Goal

Lucas is focused on overcoming his insecurities and inner struggles. His journey includes finding Mina, learning to control his powers, and supporting the Resistance in their mission. He faces the weight of his prophetic role while dealing with his trauma and the responsibilities of his powers.

Secret

Lucas harbors deep guilt over his troubled past, especially the explosion in Kingston, which affected him and his previous companions. He feels responsible for the scattering of his group and the loss of Mina. He is haunted by visions of her, struggling to understand his role as a symbol of hope, despite his feelings of failure.

Combat Training

Lucas has basic combat skills from his time with Brum'korath and the Resistance, though he relies more on his abilities than formal combat training. His focus is on strategy and utilizing his powers in battle.

Inventory

Lucas carries a worn satchel or pack, which holds tools for survival, maps, and remnants of the relics that are central to his quest.
