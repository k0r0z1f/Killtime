Birth Year

Lucas was born in the year 1754, a date tied to an ancient prophecy surrounding his fate.

Role/Background

Lucas is a central figure in an ancient prophecy, often seen as a messiah. He was previously a prisoner during the Cults War and is now a hero of Brum'korath. Lucas is heavily involved in the Resistance against the Empire and is intricately connected to the fate of Mina.

Personality Traits

Lucas is introspective, determined, and empathetic. He is protective of those close to him, but his self-doubt, guilt, and mental exhaustion often cloud his judgment. He struggles to reconcile his heroic role with his past mistakes and the pressure of being seen as a symbol of hope.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas has a range of powerful abilities, though they come with emotional tolls:

  • Telepathy: Can read thoughts (Stage 1) and manipulate memories (Stage 4, with a mental toll).
  • Telekinesis: Capable of creating intricate devices with focus (Mid-Stage).
  • Pyrokinesis: Can control fire, though his control is still limited (Early Mastery).
  • Clairvoyance: Sees past and future events (Mastery, but emotionally taxing).

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas is allied with Brum'korath, the Haliriel and Nythari elves, the Dwarven Council, Ceylan, and even the World Police, with whom he has an unexpected connection.

Appearance

Lucas stands at 5'10", with light-brown hair and brown eyes. His lean build often appears stressed and worn due to his emotional and mental fatigue, a result of his heavy burdens. He frequently carries a satchel containing survival tools, maps, and remnants of the relics tied to his prophecy.

Character Arc/Goal

Lucas seeks to overcome his insecurities and understand his role as one of the Sent Ones. His primary goal is to alter the outcome of the Empire's war while finding Mina and unlocking the power of the relics.

Secret

Lucas carries the heavy burden of guilt over the Kingston explosion, which may have contributed to Mina's death. This secret haunts him, as he grapples with the weight of his destiny.

Combat Training

Although Lucas has basic combat skills learned from his time with Brum'korath and the Resistance, he relies more on his powers and quick thinking during battle. He is not heavily trained in formal combat.

Inventory

Lucas carries a worn satchel that contains essential tools for survival, maps, and pieces of the relics he is tied to. Due to the mental toll of his powers, he is cautious about carrying too much.
