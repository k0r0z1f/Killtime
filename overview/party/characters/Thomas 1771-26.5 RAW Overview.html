Role/Background

Thomas is a member of the party, a strategist with a strong athletic background. He was once a fugitive but now leads the group as their protector. He is marked as an enemy of the state, navigating the world with skepticism and caution.

Personality Traits

Stoic and pragmatic, Thomas remains calm under pressure and is determined to ensure the party’s survival. His protective nature makes him a strong leader, and while firm and authoritative, he is also diplomatic and thoughtful. Driven by a sense of purpose, he tends to be cautious, observant, and reflective. His relic also affects his behavior, making him more protective of Lucas and Mina.

Abilities/Magic

None specified for magical abilities, but his expertise lies in strategy, tactics, and leadership.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thomas is considered an enemy by the World Police but is closely allied with Lucas, Mina, Dira, and the rest of the group.

Appearance

Thomas stands tall with an athletic build and sharp eyes that give him an imposing presence. His clothing is practical and suited for travel, often worn but functional.

Character Arc/Goal

Thomas is focused on ensuring the party’s survival amidst growing threats, navigating complex political environments, and striving to understand the prophecy that looms over them. His ultimate goal is to protect his friends and fight for the truth, all while managing the pressures of being surveilled and marked as an enemy.

Secret

Thomas’s secret remains unknown, even to himself, though it may be tied to the relic he wears and its influence on his behavior towards Lucas and Mina.

Combat Training

Thomas has received extensive combat training and is highly skilled in battle tactics and strategy. His experience makes him an invaluable asset in the field.

Details of Thomas’s inventory are not specified at the moment.
