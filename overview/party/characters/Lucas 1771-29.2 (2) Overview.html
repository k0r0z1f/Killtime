Population

Lucas, 17 years old (Born 1754). Former short-time prisoner of Brum'korath, he has been pulled into major conflicts across the land from an early age. He is one of the Children of Hybris, marked by prophecy. Officially considered an outlaw in the Central Lands after the Kingston incident, he escaped custody and has been on the run. Despite his precarious status, his intellect and resolve have kept him alive.

Appearance

Lucas stands at around 5’10” with a lean build. He has light-brown, disheveled hair, and brown eyes that often carry a serious or worried look. His expressions typically reflect the stress he endures or the focus of his contemplations. He often wears a worn satchel slung across his shoulder, containing notes, relic information, and survival gear. His posture can be slightly guarded, revealing both caution and vigilance.

Energy & Atmosphere

Lucas’s personality is a swirling mix of determination, analytical thinking, introspection, and caution. He is curious by nature, but also deeply weighed down by guilt and mistrust born from past events. He can be skeptical and reserved, yet protective of those close to him. Socially, he may be shy or hesitant, even easily embarrassed when it comes to romantic feelings, but his sense of duty pushes him to adapt. This makes him at times anxious but hopeful, gradually warming up to the group as he realizes the importance of unity.

Architecture

Lucas possesses a range of psychic and elemental abilities.

  • Telepathy: Three stages—Surface Thought Reading, Deeper Thought Access/Emotional Sensory, and limited Memory Access. Permanent telepathic link with Mina.
  • Telekinesis: Intermediate control, allowing multiple small objects or one larger object (up to ~30kg).
  • Pyrokinesis: Basic flame creation and intermediate fire manipulation.
  • Entry-level Clairvoyance: Unconscious or rudimentary visions, not well controlled.
Additionally, he demonstrates emerging mastery of arcane technology—capable of communicating telepathically with the relics, and potentially unlocking deeper powers linked to the prophecy.

Symbolism & Carvings

Lucas’s affiliations are both formal and informal. He is aligned with Mina and Thomas, key companions who also bear fused relics. Though an outlaw to the World Police (due to misunderstandings and the Kingston incident), he also holds a temporary alliance with Haliriel and has ties to the Children of Hybris. He indirectly finds himself connected to the Cult of the Sent Ones through prophecy. Despite these complexities, he moves forward with the mission team that aims to undertake the Eastern Continent expedition.

Defenses

In terms of combat, Lucas leans on his strategic thinking and psychic powers. While he has basic weapon training and moderate combat experience, it is his telekinesis and pyrokinesis that provide him with an edge. His time in Brum'korath and the constant need to evade capture have honed his survival instincts, pushing him to innovate on the fly. He is capable of leadership in tense situations, though he often feels the weight of responsibility keenly.

Legends & Lore

Lucas is central to ancient relic prophecies as one of the Children of Hybris and part of the Sent Ones prophecy. He, along with Mina and Thomas, has a fused relic, linking him to the unfolding events. His involvement in the Cults War and the Disciple’s rampage has placed him in a precarious spotlight. Many see him as a key figure capable of unlocking the relics’ power to stop the Disciples’ threat. Yet rumor and speculation persist—some believe he may be misled about being “chosen,” while others think the relic’s chaotic synergy with him is a sign of deeper destiny.

Hidden Dangers

The greatest hidden danger for Lucas lies in the memory of the Kingston explosion and the guilt he harbors over it. Furthermore, he is burdened by an anomaly in his DNA that could cause instability with the relic’s synchronization. He has deep-seated mistrust of elves and dwarves, resulting from manipulations in his past, and feels uncertain about how far the prophecy might push him. His experiences in medical laboratories from birth to age 11 have left scars, both mental and physical, that continue to influence him.

Link to the Party

  • Mina: Best friend, loves her deeply, telepathically linked.
  • Thomas: Best friend and de facto leader figure.
  • Candice: Known for 3 days, unsure but feels compelled to believe her; senses a parasitic presence in her mind.
  • Dira: Met less than a month ago. Despite her past betrayal, he trusts her research and resolve; a friend.

Weather

Beneath the surface, Lucas carries the lingering burdens of medical experimentation that define much of his past. From infancy to age 11, he was subjected to various procedures aimed at enhancing or controlling his latent abilities. This invasive history creates an undercurrent of anxiety, especially regarding institutional forces like the World Police or powerful factions he cannot fully trust.

Recent Developments

Having recently been released from Brum'korath, Lucas is now traveling to prepare for an upcoming Eastern Continent expedition intended to confront the Disciple of Air. He aims to understand the nature of the relics, stop the looming threat of the Disciples, and protect his companions. Uncertain political motivations swirl around him, yet his main goal remains the same: to discover the truth behind the relics and the prophecy, and to ensure the safety of those he cares about.
