Birth Year

Thomas was born in 1753 and is currently 17 years old (as of the current date, 04-06-1771). This makes him notably young for his leadership role and current responsibilities.

Role/Background

Thomas serves as a leader, companion, and the Hero of Brum'korath. Formerly a student with an athletic background, he became a fugitive of the Central Lands and is now marked as an enemy of the state. Despite these circumstances, Thomas is a critical member of Lucas’s group, trained in combat and dedicated to unlocking relics to stop the Disciple.

His pragmatic nature and imposing, no-nonsense attitude make him a natural battlefield leader. He adapts quickly to unpredictable scenarios, balancing his role as a protector with the necessity of dealing decisively with threats.

Personality Traits

Thomas is strategic, level-headed, and protective. He exhibits calmness, confidence, and a commanding presence. His focus and calculating mindset allow him to remain steady and resilient in the face of danger. He is loyal, yet can be impatient or frustrated when pressed. Cautious and determined, he maintains diplomatic awareness and is passionate in his pursuits.

The relic he wears makes him more overprotective of Lucas and Mina, intensifying an internal conflict between his protective instincts and his respect for his friends’ autonomy. He struggles with guilt over decisions that could harm his companions and strives to find balance.

Abilities/Magic

Thomas possesses a relic bound to his wrist which subtly affects his morality and decision-making. This connection creates inner turmoil, influencing his actions in ways that remain unknown to both himself and his allies. The magic or power of the relic is not overt but steadily amplifies his protective tendencies, potentially driving him into morally grey areas.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thomas is a friend and traveling companion of Lucas and Mina. Through prophecy and circumstance, he is indirectly connected to the Cult of the Sent Ones. Additionally, he has allies in Brum'korath. However, Thomas and his group are declared enemies of the state by the World Police, which places him in constant danger and under surveillance.

Appearance

Standing at 6'5" with a broad, sturdy build, Thomas is notably tall and athletic. He has sharp eyes and carries himself with an imposing presence. His clothing is practical and geared toward travel, reflecting his life on the run and the constant preparedness required of him.

Character Arc/Goal

Thomas acts as both protector and strategist within his group, focusing on survival and the completion of epic tasks to safeguard Lucas, Mina, and the entire party. He plays a pivotal role in battles, serving as a battlefield leader, particularly in conflicts against the Disciple of Earth.

He struggles with his morality and the weight of war, ultimately delivering a final blow to end the Disciple of Earth’s threat. Thomas aims to shield his friends while helping them uncover the truth. He navigates Brum'korath’s political landscape, endures continual surveillance, and remains steadfast in the pursuit of stopping catastrophe. At the same time, he seeks to understand the prophecy that guides them, all while coping with his own shifting moral compass influenced by the relic.

Secret

Thomas harbors deep suspicion of various cults’ influence and remains uncertain about the moral implications of the ongoing war. He feels isolated in his skepticism and struggles with guilt over the impact his choices may have on his friends. Unbeknownst to him, the relic is changing his mind and reinforcing his overprotective nature, causing him to question his own judgment.

Combat Training

Thomas has extensive combat training and tactical leadership skills. He is adept with a sword and proficient in hand-to-hand combat, showing remarkable adaptability in fast-changing scenarios. This expertise, along with his measured approach, makes him a formidable force on the battlefield.

Inventory

Thomas keeps 86 doumis on his person, alongside a backpack containing survival gear. He wields a sword and shield, and is meticulous about maintaining a state of readiness. The relic bound to his wrist is his most crucial possession, though its deeper effects on him remain a mystery.

Lucas

Lucas is Thomas’s best friend and a source of hope and purpose. Thomas feels a fierce obligation to protect Lucas, often going to great lengths to ensure his safety. Their bond and camaraderie drive much of Thomas’s decisions as he values Lucas’s life above all else.

Mina

Mina is also a best friend to Thomas. He admires her abilities and respects her independence. Although he feels the same protective instinct toward Mina, he strives to grant her the space and freedom to act on her own terms, reflecting the conflict between his leadership role and his desire to respect those he cares about.

Candice

Thomas has known Candice for only two days. There is an unconscious crush that he is not fully aware of, yet it adds subtle complexity to his decisions. This potential affection introduces a layer of vulnerability and confusion, particularly in tense situations where split-second judgments are necessary.

Dira

Thomas has been acquainted with Dira for less than a month. He regards her with caution, but also a measure of respect, as long as her intentions remain sincere. Her insights and strong will can be intimidating, yet he values her contributions to the group dynamic when trust is established.
