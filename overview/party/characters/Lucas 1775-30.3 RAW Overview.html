Role/Background

Lucas is a key figure in the Resistance, often referred to as "the messiah" and "the key to the future." He is a strategist who is central to a mission linked to a prophecy, being pursued by the Empire while navigating the sewers with his group.

Personality Traits

Lucas is a quiet and distant person, often anxious and struggling with trauma. Despite his inner turmoil, he is determined and steady, a strategic thinker who feels a deep sense of guilt over past events.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas possesses several powerful abilities, including telepathy and latent powers that allow him to emit bursts of light and vibrate the air. He can manipulate raw energy, levitate, generate pressure waves, disable machinery, create aura shields, and manipulate the environment around him.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas is allied with the Resistance, Erika, and John. However, he is also targeted by a rival faction within the Resistance and opposes the Empire.
