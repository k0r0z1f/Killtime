Birth Year

Thomas was born in the year 1753. As of the current year 1771, he is 17 years old.

Role/Background

Thomas serves as the leader, companion, and Hero of Brum'korath. He is a former student with an athletic background who became a fugitive from the Central Lands. Currently marked as an enemy of the state, he is a member of Lucas's group. Thomas is trained in combat and works alongside Lucas to unlock relics and stop the Disciple.

Personality Traits

Thomas is strategic, level-headed, and protective, with a skeptical view of the cults. He remains calm, confident, and commanding, maintaining focus and calculating his moves carefully. His steady and resilient nature makes him loyal, though he can be impatient and frustrated. Thomas is cautious and desperate when necessary, vocal and tense under pressure, yet remains weary and unsettled. His curiosity drives him to stay concerned but calm, showcasing a stoic and pragmatic demeanor. He is determined, resolute, and firm, employing diplomatic skills when needed. Passionate and restless, Thomas is sharp and authoritative, often thoughtful and reflective. Driven by a sense of purpose, he is observant and practical, providing unwavering support to his allies. The relic he wears makes him overprotective of Lucas and Mina and reinforces his leadership, though the fact that the relic changes his behavior is unknown to everyone, including himself.

Abilities/Magic

No abilities or magic have been provided for Thomas.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thomas is a friend and travel companion of Lucas and Mina. He is indirectly associated with the Cult of the Sent Ones through prophecy and maintains alliances with the Brum'korath allies. However, he stands against the World Police, who are considered enemies.

Appearance

Thomas stands at 6'5" with a broad and sturdy build, giving him an imposing and athletic presence. He has sharp eyes that reflect his focused nature. His attire is practical, often consisting of worn clothing suitable for travel, emphasizing his readiness for any situation.

Character Arc/Goal

Thomas acts as a protector and strategist for the group, supporting Lucas and Mina with a focus on survival. He performs epic actions in battle to ensure the group's safety and serves as the battlefield leader while fighting the Disciple of Earth. Throughout his journey, Thomas struggles with his own sense of morality in the face of war. He is deeply involved in bringing down the Disciple of Earth, ultimately delivering the coup de grace to kill it. His overarching goal is to protect his friends, help them uncover the truth, and ensure their survival. Thomas navigates the political landscape of Brum'korath, ensuring the party’s success despite surveillance. He remains sharp under pressure, fights for truth and survival, and strives to stop a larger catastrophe. Additionally, he faces challenges in Haliriel and attempts to understand the prophecy that binds their fates.

Secret

Thomas is suspicious of the cult’s influence and uncertain about the moral implications of the ongoing war. He feels isolated in his beliefs and senses that something is changing inside his mind due to the relic he wears.

Combat Training

Thomas has undergone extensive combat training, including tactical leadership and skilled sword combat. He is also trained in hand-to-hand combat, making him a formidable opponent in various combat scenarios.

Inventory

Thomas carries 86 doumis, a backpack, and essential survival gear to aid in his travels and missions. His primary weapons are a sword and shield, which he wields with expertise. Additionally, he possesses a relic bound to his wrist, which plays a significant role in his abilities and leadership.

Lucas

Lucas is Thomas's best friend, and Thomas is committed to protecting him at all costs.

Mina

Thomas is also a best friend to Mina and admires her prowess.

Candice

Thomas has known Candice for 2 days and considers her an acquaintance. He harbors an unconscious crush on her.

Dira

Thomas has known Dira for less than a month and regards her as a friend. He is cautious around her but respects her as long as she is truly repentant.
