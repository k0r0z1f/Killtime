Birth Year

Lucas was born in 1754, making him 17 years old. He is central to the ancient relics prophecy and one of the Children of Hybris.

Role/Background

Lucas is the Hero of Brum'korath and a key member of the team preparing for the Eastern Continent expedition. Formerly a prisoner of Brum'korath, he is connected to the Sent Ones prophecy and the relics, having been fused with one. His background includes being a victim of medi-laboratory experiments and escaping the Kingston incident, which made him an outlaw of the Central Lands. He is burdened by his past involvement in the Cults War and the Disciple's rampage.

Personality Traits

Lucas is a complex individual. He is determined, analytical, and introspective, yet burdened by guilt and responsibility. His traits include being cautious, brave, protective, and resilient. He is often conflicted, feeling the weight of scrutiny and questioning his worthiness for the relics. Despite his vulnerabilities, he remains focused, empathetic, and curious, showing a willingness to take risks when needed.

Abilities/Magic

Lucas has telepathy at varying stages, from surface thought reading to deeper memory access. He also possesses intermediate telekinesis, entry-level pyrokinesis, and emerging clairvoyance. His powers include a permanent telepathic link with Mina and the ability to communicate with relics. Additionally, he shows mastery of arcane technology and has unexplained destructive powers linked to the relics, which he struggles to control.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Lucas is aligned with Mina, Thomas, Candice, and Dira. He is a member of the Children of Hybris and the group ascending the Watcher. His alliances include the Dwarven Council, temporary alliances with Haliriel, and indirect ties to the Cult of the Sent Ones through the prophecy. He is also declared an enemy of the state by the World Police.

Appearance

Lucas stands at 5'10" with a lean build, light-brown disheveled hair, and brown eyes. His typical expression is stressed or focused, reflecting his burdened and serious nature. He often carries a worn satchel as part of his gear.

Secret

Lucas harbors guilt over the Kingston explosion and doubts his worthiness for the relics. He has a DNA anomaly causing instability in relic synchronization, and the full effects of the relic on him remain unclear. He also mistrusts elves and dwarves due to past manipulations.

Combat Training

Lucas is trained in basic weaponry and has intermediate telekinetic combat skills. His strategic mind and tactical knowledge make him capable in leadership contexts. Despite his struggles with control, his abilities in telekinesis and pyrokinesis enhance his combat effectiveness.

Inventory

Lucas carries an extensive inventory, including 810 doumis, a rare battery hybrid worth 20,000 doumis, survival gear, dry food, a change of clothes, and magi-tech parts for testing relics. He also has a relic attached to his wrist, a satchel with notes and theories, and a blanket secured by Candice.

Mina

Mina is Lucas's best friend and confidante. They share a deep bond, further strengthened by their telepathic link. Lucas loves her deeply and relies on her unwavering support in their shared journey.

Thomas

Thomas is Lucas's best friend and a natural leader. Lucas trusts him implicitly, valuing his guidance and strength in the face of their challenges.

Candice

Lucas has known Candice for just four days, but he senses a parasitical presence in her mind that feels familiar yet unsettling. Despite this, he believes in her potential and cautiously works alongside her.

Dira

Dira has been a part of Lucas's life for less than a month, but her dedication to their shared goals has earned his trust. Lucas admires her research skills and has forgiven her for past betrayals, valuing her resolve and commitment to their mission.
