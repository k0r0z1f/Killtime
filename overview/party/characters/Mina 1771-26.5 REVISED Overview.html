Birth Year

Mina was born in the year 1754, making her 17 years old. She is a young adventurer with a complex past tied to the prophecy.

Role/Background

Mina is an adventurer, the Hero of Brum'korath, and a key member of the group. She has become a trusted protector, both to Lucas and Thomas, while navigating the complex world shaped by the Cults War and the prophecy that binds them.

Personality Traits

Mina is fiercely independent, having broken free from her father's mansion after college. She is intuitive, empathetic, and courageous, though she carries a streak of recklessness. Her deep connection with Lucas gives her a grounding presence, helping him navigate the chaos around them.

Abilities/Magic

Mina possesses superhuman speed and agility due to a magical growth incident. She shares a permanent telepathic link with Lucas, and her senses are hypersensitive, making her acutely aware of her surroundings. Her healing abilities are enhanced, allowing her to recover more quickly than most non-magical individuals.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Mina is closely allied with Lucas and Thomas, and she indirectly has ties to the Cult of the Sent Ones through their shared connection to the prophecy.

Appearance

Mina has striking green eyes and a strong, athletic build. Standing at 5'11", she carries herself with a fierce presence. Her bright blonde hair is usually pulled back in a tight ponytail, and though she bears faint scars from past battles, her demeanor is magnetic and full of life.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina's goal is to protect her friends, especially Lucas, and navigate the murky waters of the prophecy. She seeks to understand her connection to the events unfolding around her, grappling with the balance between personal freedom and the looming surveillance of larger powers. Ultimately, she aims to prevent a catastrophe and find answers about her role in the prophecy.

Combat Training

Mina is trained in advanced athletic combat, with a focus on speed-based tactics. Her expertise in martial arts and track has helped her become a formidable combatant. She is preparing for larger-scale battles and responsibilities in the war that lies ahead.

Inventory
