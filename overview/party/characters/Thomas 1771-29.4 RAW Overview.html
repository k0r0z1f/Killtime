Role/Background

Thomas is a protective member of the climbing group, assisting with physical challenges during the ascent of the Watcher. He takes responsibility for caring for Lucas and Mina, acting as a defender during crises.

Personality Traits

Thomas is firm, cautious, supportive, composed, strong, commanding, and reassuring. He is vigilant, concerned, anxious, determined, protective, courageous, and quick to act, occasionally expressing anger when needed.

Abilities/Magic

Thomas possesses significant physical strength, allowing him to shield others effectively. He wields a sword and uses a relic that transforms into a cylindrical, piston-like device with otherworldly power.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

He is a member of the climbing group aiming to reach the Watcher’s cave, also known as Candice’s group, currently seeking Lucas and Mina.

Appearance

Thomas has a larger, powerful frame that emphasizes his physical strength.

Character Arc/Goal

Thomas aims to help the group ascend the mountain despite significant challenges, protect Lucas and Mina, and overcome threats posed by the Minulican and the Beast.

Secret

Not provided.

Combat Training

Thomas is trained with a sword and capable of effectively using the relic in combat.

Inventory

Thomas carries a coil of rope, sturdy boots, a sword, and a relic bound to his wrist.
