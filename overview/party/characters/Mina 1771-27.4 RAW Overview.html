Birth Year

Mina's exact birth year is unknown, though her connection to the prophecy suggests she has been a part of a greater fate for many years.

Role/Background

Mina is one of the Children of Hybris, a key figure in the prophecy with Lucas. She was revealed during the Elven Council meeting and has since played a vital role in the group's mission. Her telepathic link with Lucas helps guide him through moments of uncertainty, solidifying her as a crucial member of the team.

Personality Traits

Mina is calm and composed, always thinking strategically. She is fiercely loyal and highly focused on the mission, but still retains a playful side that helps keep spirits high during tough times. She is sharp, reassuring, and often steps in to provide clarity when the mission or the group faces doubt.

Abilities/Magic

Mina's most notable ability is her telepathic link with Lucas, allowing them to communicate and assist each other mentally. She may possess other latent abilities connected to the prophecy and ancient relics, though these remain largely unexplored at this stage.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Mina is part of the Children of Hybris, a powerful and mysterious faction tied to the prophecy. She is also temporarily allied with the Elven factions, working alongside them to accomplish her mission.

Appearance

Mina has bright blonde hair, giving her a striking and ethereal appearance. Her playful demeanor contrasts with the serious nature of her mission, allowing her to form deep connections with the group while maintaining focus on their goal.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina's goal is to navigate her destiny as one of the Children of Hybris, playing a key role in potentially saving or reshaping the world. Throughout the story, she works closely with Lucas and the group, offering reassurance, strategic advice, and strength in moments of doubt.

Secret

Mina’s secret remains unknown, though it is hinted that her role in the prophecy may reveal hidden truths about her past and her connection to powerful forces.

Combat Training

Mina has undergone speed-based combat training, focusing on agility and swift movements. Her abilities allow her to outmaneuver opponents, relying on quick strikes and dodging rather than raw strength.

Inventory

Mina’s inventory remains unspecified, though it is likely she carries tools and relics related to her role in the prophecy.
