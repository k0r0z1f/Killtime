Role/Background

Mina is a key member of her group, serving as a scout and lookout. Her connection to a prophecy and relics makes her integral to the group’s mission. She is tied to an arcane storm that brought her into the world, and her role is closely linked to Lucas and the Minulican’s energy.

Personality Traits

Mina is brave, optimistic, and empathetic, maintaining a steady demeanor. She is also urgent, determined, and deeply supportive of her companions.

Abilities/Magic

She possesses superhuman speed and agility, along with a telepathic bond with Lucas that amplifies his telekinetic powers. Her connection to the arcane storm affects her in unique ways, including levitation and sudden disappearances.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Mina is part of a group including Lucas, Candice, and Dira. They are allied with Queen Aphyrosia and Altheris in their quest to seek wisdom from The Watcher.

Appearance

She has an athletic build and striking green eyes, complementing her active role in the group.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina aims to live her own life, free from the control of her adoptive father and the constraints of prophecy. She is integral to the group’s mission, supporting them in their challenges and deepening her bond with Lucas while seeking to unravel the prophecy tied to her existence.
