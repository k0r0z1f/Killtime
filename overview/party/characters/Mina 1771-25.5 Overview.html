Birth Year

Mina was born in 1754, a year after Thomas, and has had a contrasting life experience. Raised as an upper-class individual, her life took a drastic turn when she joined the fight against the Empire and learned of the Sent Ones' return.

Role/Background

Mina is a skilled adventurer and an integral member of the group alongside Lucas and Thomas. Once a celebrity and college student, her life was transformed when she escaped with Lucas and joined the fight against the Empire. Her background gives her a unique perspective and adds to her drive to protect those she loves.

Personality Traits

Mina is an ambitious and resilient character who is deeply loyal to those she cares about. She is a brave individual who faces challenges head-on, even when doubts creep in. Her cautious approach toward the Sent Ones highlights her analytical mind, but her determination ensures that she remains a key ally in the battle against the Empire. At her core, she is a caring and protective individual.

Abilities/Magic

Mina possesses superhuman speed and agility due to a magical growth incident. This allows her to move at extraordinary speeds, making her a formidable combatant. However, her magic is not without its flaws, as she experiences small, uncontrolled hypersensitive senses. Additionally, she shares a telepathic link with Lucas, a connection that proves invaluable during their journey.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Mina's primary alliances include her close relationship with Lucas, her role as a friend and traveling companion to Thomas, and her indirect connection to the Cult of the Sent Ones through prophecy. While her views on the Sent Ones are cautious, she is drawn into the larger conflict because of their involvement in the war.

Appearance

Mina stands at a height of 5'11", with bright blonde hair tied in a tight ponytail. Her green eyes give her an intense and focused look. With an athletic build, she has a presence that belies her grace and speed. She also has faint scars from head and abdomen wounds, remnants of past battles.

Character Arc/Goal

Mina's character arc revolves around her development as a protector. As she supports Lucas, she begins to find her role in the larger conflict against the Empire. Throughout the journey, she wrestles with the implications of the Sent Ones' return and the growing realization of her true purpose in the war. Her growth as a fighter and leader parallels her inner struggle with the responsibilities she faces.

Secret

Mina has a strong intuition about the Sent Ones and their role in the conflict. Despite her outward confidence, she is unsure of her capacity to face the challenges ahead. She questions her place in the unfolding war, and whether she is truly prepared for the burden that lies ahead.

Combat Training

Mina is highly trained in athletic combat, including track and martial arts, which allows her to execute speed-based tactics in battle. Her training has made her highly adaptable, and she is preparing for larger-scale conflicts where her abilities will play a crucial role in the fight against the Empire.
