Birth Year

1753 (17 years old, current year 1771)

Role/Background

Thomas is a leader and companion, originally a student with an athletic background. He became a fugitive after the Kingston incident, marking him as an enemy of the state. He is a hero of Brum'korath, known for his strategic mind and natural leadership abilities.

Personality Traits

Thomas is calm under pressure and incredibly focused. He's strategic, protective of his companions, and skeptical of outside forces, especially the cults. He tends to be authoritative, driven by a strong sense of purpose, and is extremely loyal to his friends. His sense of morality is challenged by the ongoing war, and he's often frustrated by the changes he’s going through, particularly his growing isolation due to the relic's influence.

Abilities/Magic

Thomas does not possess any magical abilities but makes up for it with his exceptional combat skills and tactical acumen.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thomas is a close friend and travel companion to Lucas and Mina. He is indirectly tied to the Cult of the Sent Ones through the prophecy and has formed strong alliances with Brum'korath allies. He remains an enemy of the World Police due to his involvement in the Kingston incident and subsequent actions.

Appearance

Standing at 6'5" with a broad and sturdy build, Thomas is imposing and easily noticeable. His sharp eyes and athletic build reflect his training, and his practical clothing is worn and suited for travel. His presence alone often commands respect and attention.

Character Arc/Goal

Thomas' primary goal is to protect his friends and ensure their survival, all while navigating the tumultuous political landscape of Brum'korath. He struggles with the pressure of leadership and is often forced to make tough decisions in the heat of battle. As the party faces new challenges, Thomas works tirelessly to uncover the truth behind the prophecy and stop a larger catastrophe from unfolding.

Secret

Thomas is unsure about the cult's influence on him and is struggling with his growing sense of isolation. The relic he wears is subtly changing his behavior, though he remains unaware of its effects. He also harbors doubts about the moral implications of the war and his place in it.

Combat Training

Thomas is an expert in tactical leadership and sword combat. His combat skills have been honed through years of training and battlefield experience, particularly during the fight against the Disciple.

Inventory

Thomas carries 90 doumis, a backpack filled with survival gear, and a sword and shield for combat. He also has a relic bound to his wrist, which has a significant impact on his behavior, though he is unaware of its influence.
