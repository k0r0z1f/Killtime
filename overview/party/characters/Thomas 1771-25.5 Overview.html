Birth Year

Thomas was born in 1753, a figure from an earlier time, and has lived through many trials and challenges by the time of the events described in Chapter 25, Part 5.

Role/Background

He is a key leader and companion to the main protagonists, Lucas and Mina. His involvement with the Disciple in the battle at Brum'korath signifies a pivotal point in the story. As a strategist, Thomas plays a critical role in organizing and executing battle plans to help his allies prevail. His leadership comes from a place of strength and experience, particularly in the chaos of war.

Personality Traits

Thomas is a complex character with a wide range of emotional and psychological traits:

  • He is strategic and level-headed, always thinking ahead and keeping his emotions in check, especially in the midst of battle.
  • He is deeply protective of those he cares about, especially Lucas and Mina, though he also feels a sense of responsibility for their survival.
  • Thomas is skeptical about the cults, particularly the Cult of the Sent Ones, seeing through their potential for manipulation and deceit.
  • At times, he can seem calm and confident, but these moments can quickly be overshadowed by his deeper, more frustrated and impatient sides, especially as he grows increasingly weary from the ongoing war.
  • He often feels isolated in his beliefs, finding himself questioning the very foundation of the cause they’re fighting for.
  • His loyalty to his friends, especially Lucas and Mina, remains unwavering, though his desperation grows as the war unfolds.

Abilities/Magic

Unlike many characters in this world, Thomas has no magical abilities. His strength lies in his combat training and his ability to lead. His skills are more tactical and strategic rather than mystical or arcane.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thomas is primarily allied with Lucas and Mina, forming a small but tight-knit team. He is indirectly tied to the Cult of the Sent Ones through prophecy, though his views on the cult are distrustful and critical. He also shares an alliance with the Brum'korath forces in their fight against the Disciple.

Appearance

Thomas is described as standing tall at 6'5", with a broad and sturdy build. He is likely to show physical signs of wear from his battles, his appearance marking him as a warrior hardened by conflict. His imposing figure is complemented by a certain gravitas, both on and off the battlefield.

Character Arc/Goal

Throughout the story, Thomas's journey is one of self-discovery and protection. He acts as both a protector and strategist, working tirelessly to ensure the safety and success of Lucas and Mina. His personal struggle with morality is central to his arc, particularly as the war drags on and his convictions are put to the test. He is caught between the desire to fight for what is right and the harsh realities of the battle. The act of giving the coup de grace to the Disciple is both a triumph and a heavy burden, encapsulating his internal conflict. His overarching goal is to protect his friends, uncover the truth behind the cults and the war, and find a way to survive the harsh realities of the world around him.

Secret

Thomas harbors a deep suspicion of the cult’s influence. He is also grappling with uncertainty about the moral implications of the war. This internal struggle, combined with his sense of isolation, causes him to feel as though something is changing within him—he believes he is being affected by a relic bound to his wrist, though he cannot fully understand its power or implications.

Combat Training

Thomas is extensively trained in sword combat and tactical leadership, which makes him a formidable force in battle. His ability to remain calm and think strategically allows him to lead his allies with precision and effectiveness, even under the most chaotic of circumstances.
