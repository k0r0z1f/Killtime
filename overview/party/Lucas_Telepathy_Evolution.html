Evolutionary Path of Lucas's Telepathy

Stage Description Abilities Limitations
Stage 1: Surface Thought Reading (DMN-Focused) Lucas begins by accessing ambient thoughts without focusing deeply, essentially tapping into the target’s default mode network (DMN). Detects immediate feelings, idle thoughts, and reflections without concentrated effort. Cannot access or influence deeper, guarded thoughts or long-term memories.
Stage 2: Deeper Thought Access and Emotional Sensory Tuning With practice, Lucas learns to delve beyond surface thoughts, accessing emotions, sensory memories, and intentional (active) thoughts. He can pick up on complex thoughts, personal biases, and emotional undertones. Still lacks the power to influence or alter these thoughts and emotions.
Stage 3: Memory Access and Short-Term Influence Lucas begins accessing stored memories, navigating past experiences, and learns to send brief "suggestions," giving him subtle influence over thoughts or feelings. Can explore memories, finding connections between them, and induce small suggestions or emotional nudges. Suggestions are subtle and short-lived; deeper changes require more concentration.
Stage 4: Memory Editing and Enhanced Thought Influence Lucas gains the ability to modify certain neural pathways related to memories and exert greater influence over active thoughts. Can edit recent memories, create false associations, or manipulate thought patterns. Memory changes are limited to recent events; long-term traits remain unchanged.
Stage 5: Comprehensive Memory Rewriting and Full Thought Manipulation Lucas learns to rewire specific neural circuits, allowing for comprehensive memory editing and full thought manipulation. Can create or erase memories and script thoughts and emotional responses. Maintaining influence is energy-intensive and requires significant focus.
Stage 6: Full Neural Control and Possession At the highest level, Lucas can rewire neurons, alter memories, and take full control of a person’s motor, sensory, and cognitive functions. Full control over the person’s body and mind, with permanent changes to memories and personality possible. Full possession is intense and difficult to maintain; overuse risks Lucas's identity.
