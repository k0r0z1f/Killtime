Location

Located in the heart of Brum'karoth, the Sent Ones HQ is hidden within towering, ancient structures. It is isolated from the outside world by high walls and guarded entrances, making it extremely difficult for outsiders to enter.

Appearance

The Sent Ones HQ is an imposing structure with gothic spires and stained-glass windows that depict celestial and mystical beings. The dark stone is etched with arcane symbols, and the interior is cold, silent, and haunting. Flickering candlelight casts shadows throughout, adding to the mysterious ambiance.

Energy & Atmosphere

The building exudes an atmosphere of control and power. There is a constant, oppressive sense that one is being watched by unseen forces. The air is thick with the energy of dark rituals and impending war, creating an unsettling feeling of both reverence and tension.

Architecture

The architecture is designed for both intimidation and function. The long, narrow corridors are lined with vaulted ceilings and dark passageways, many of which lead to hidden chambers and secret rooms that enhance the sense of mystery.

Symbolism & Carvings

The walls of the headquarters are covered in symbols that reflect the beliefs of the Sent Ones. Celestial motifs and sacred geometric patterns are carved into the stone, and some images are eerily prophetic, alluding to the arrival of a chosen one—possibly tied to Lucas’s fate.

Inner Sanctum

At the heart of the headquarters lies the Inner Sanctum, a large chamber where sacred rituals are performed. An ancient altar stands in the center, and the room hums with energy. It is perpetually lit by candles, contributing to its somber, ritualistic atmosphere.

Entrance

The entrance to the HQ is marked by a massive iron door engraved with cult symbols. It is heavily guarded and vibrates with an ancient, arcane power. Only those of high rank within the cult are allowed to pass through.

Defenses

The headquarters is surrounded by powerful magical wards and guarded by a disciplined force of sentries. The building is filled with hidden traps, ensuring that only trusted individuals can navigate its complex layout. Some cult members possess enhanced magical abilities that serve as an additional layer of defense.

Secret Chambers/Rooms

Secret chambers beneath the headquarters contain ancient texts, scrolls, and arcane artifacts. These rooms are crucial to the cult’s rituals, with some holding powerful secrets tied to Lucas’s abilities and the cult’s mysterious future.

Legends & Lore

The Sent Ones HQ is at the center of ancient prophecies regarding a "chosen one" who will shape the cult’s future. These prophecies intertwine with the life of Lucas, who may either fulfill or disrupt the cult’s ultimate plans.

Spiritual Significance

For the Sent Ones, the HQ is a sacred place where they commune with divine or otherworldly forces. Rituals conducted within its walls are believed to channel cosmic energies, and only the highest-ranking members are allowed to perform certain sacred ceremonies.

Hidden Dangers

The headquarters is filled with hidden threats—creatures bound to the building as part of dark rituals, and dangerous secrets buried deep within its walls. If these secrets were revealed, they could bring ruin to the entire city.

Link to Lucas & the Cult

The Sent Ones HQ is integral to the mystery of Lucas’s powers. The cult believes that Lucas is the key to fulfilling their prophecies, but his arrival will either solidify their vision or disrupt their plans entirely.

Surrounding Terrain

The area surrounding the HQ is filled with narrow streets, hidden alleys, and high walls, giving the cult full control over who enters and exits. There are signs of the cult’s influence in the nearby neighborhoods, with an atmosphere of fear and oppression growing stronger.

Weather

The weather around the headquarters is often gloomy, with dark clouds and frequent storms. The oppressive atmosphere seems to mirror the mood of the cult and its impending conflict, with fog and rain adding to the eerie vibe of the building.

Other Groups Interested

Rival factions, including political groups and other cults, are taking an interest in the Sent Ones HQ. Their desire for power and knowledge has led them to spy on or infiltrate the cult’s activities, further complicating the conflict.

Recent Developments

The cult is gearing up for war, with skirmishes and growing tension surrounding the HQ. Lucas, Mina, and Thomas are beginning to uncover the cult’s deep involvement in these conflicts, realizing that the fate of the city could hinge on their actions.

Cult Leadership

The leadership of the Sent Ones is under strain, with Torgar’s authority being challenged by internal factions. This tension threatens to undermine their preparations for war, creating divisions that may prove costly.

Internal Strife & Division

The cult’s internal conflicts have deepened, weakening their ability to unite for the coming war. Torgar’s leadership is in question, and factions within the cult have begun to form around differing ideologies.

New Developments

The arrival of the Disciple in Brum'karoth has forced the cult to rethink its goals. Torgar and his followers are now considering whether the Disciple’s arrival is tied to the ancient prophecies that have guided their actions for centuries.
