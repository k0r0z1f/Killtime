Birth Year

The birth year of Elder Aria is unknown, though she is considered to be quite elderly, having held her position for a significant period.

Role/Background

Elder Aria is the respected leader of the Council of Brum’korath. She holds a position of immense political power within the city, overseeing the enforcement of laws and ensuring that the council's decisions are carried out. Her leadership is largely one of command and strategy, and she has seen countless events unfold over her years in power.

Personality Traits

Elder Aria is a calculated and commanding figure:

  • She is calm under pressure and maintains an authoritative presence, never allowing emotions to cloud her judgment.
  • She is known for being sharp and decisive, capable of making difficult choices without hesitation.
  • Her authoritative nature can sometimes come off as cold and distant, creating a sense of mystery and tension around her.
  • Suspicion is a part of her nature, often questioning the motivations of those around her. This makes her suspicious and firm in her decisions.

Abilities/Magic

Elder Aria has no magical abilities. Her strength lies in her influence, wisdom, and experience, which allow her to maintain control over the city and its council.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Elder Aria is primarily aligned with the Council of Brum’korath, where she holds a leadership role. While she does not directly participate in many external alliances, her position grants her political power that influences the city and its relationships with surrounding factions.

Appearance

Elder Aria is characterized by a piercing gaze that seems to see through people. She is likely elderly, with a dignified and wise appearance that reflects her long years of leadership and experience.

Character Arc/Goal

Elder Aria’s primary goal is to maintain order and ensure justice within the city. She seeks to preserve the stability of Brum'korath, making decisions that balance the needs of the people with the greater political dynamics at play.

Secret

Although she presents herself as a staunch and logical leader, there are rumors of hidden motives. Elder Aria may harbor suspicious thoughts about her allies and rivals, leading to a complex web of secrecy and calculated actions.

Combat Training

Elder Aria has no formal combat training, as her role is more political and strategic in nature. Her influence, however, is just as formidable as a weapon, and she is skilled in navigating the complex social and political landscapes of Brum'korath.

Inventory

Official robes, marking her high status within the council.
A council seal, symbolizing her authority.
Various administrative tools such as scrolls, ink, and other symbols of her leadership role.
