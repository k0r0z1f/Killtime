Birth Year

Elder Gorla was born in 1635, making her one of the oldest and most experienced members of her factions.

Role/Background

Gorla serves as a Council Member for both the Cult of the Sent Ones and Brum'korath, holding significant power and influence within both groups. She is a key figure whose influence could shape the course of events in the ongoing conflict.

Personality Traits

Gorla is an enigma, combining wisdom and firm determination with a calculating and unreadable nature. She is deeply observant, always watching and analyzing the dynamics around her. She is not one to reveal her true thoughts easily, which makes her difficult to read. Despite this, her determination and ability to influence those around her make her a powerful figure.

Abilities/Magic

No specific magical abilities are noted for Elder Gorla, though her power lies in her strategic mind and political influence rather than arcane powers.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Elder Gorla is deeply involved with the Cult of the Sent Ones, a religious faction, and also holds a position of authority in Brum'korath, a key military ally. Her interests in these factions are tied to her search for control over the powers of Mina and the larger course of the war.

Appearance

Gorla is a female dwarf with a short, stout build. Her long silver hair and beard are both symbols of her age and wisdom. She is often seen adorned in ceremonial armor and robes, signifying her high rank within both the Cult and Brum'korath.

Character Arc/Goal

Gorla’s primary focus is on Mina’s powers. She suspects there is more to them than is immediately apparent and is quietly exploring ways to harness these powers for the Cult’s advantage. Her goals may extend beyond mere service to the Cult, with the potential for deeper, personal ambitions to unfold as her story progresses.

Secret

Gorla may be secretly investigating ways to manipulate or control Mina’s powers, using her influence within both the Cult and Brum'korath. Her hidden agenda is unclear, but it’s possible that she has plans beyond what she publicly reveals to her allies.

Combat Training

While not much is known about Gorla’s combat abilities, her strength lies more in politics, strategy, and influence. It is likely that she has basic training, but her role as a council member and political leader positions her away from direct combat.

Inventory

No specific inventory details are provided for Gorla, though her position and status imply she likely possesses ceremonial items and possibly artifacts related to the Cult and Brum'korath.
