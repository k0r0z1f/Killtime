Birth Year

Tolgar was born in 1542, a seasoned leader whose life has been defined by his role within the Cult of the Sent Ones and his strategic leadership through numerous trials and battles.

Role/Background

Tolgar is a Rockbinder Kharf, a primary cleric within the Cult of the Sent Ones, and the Interim Leader of the organization after the arrest of Goldar. He also serves as the Leader of the Hidden Temple and a Senior Council Member within the Cult. He has a deeply spiritual and influential role, tasked with guiding his followers and preserving the faith amidst growing internal turmoil.

Personality Traits

Tolgar is a character of strength and authority, but also one deeply weighed down by the responsibility of leadership. His personality can be described as:

  • Stern and authoritative, he commands respect and runs the Cult with discipline.
  • Deeply spiritual, Tolgar is motivated by faith, though his internal struggles with the cult's future often leave him calculating and concerned.
  • Resilient and tactical, he approaches leadership with a careful mind, though the mounting pressure has left him exhausted and sometimes guarded in his decisions.
  • Despite his strength, he is also gruff, stoic, and protective of his people, trying to hold the Cult together while facing an uncertain future.

Abilities/Magic

Tolgar’s magical abilities are somewhat limited but still potent. His earth magic is weak, but he compensates with deep knowledge of ancient artifacts, including those of the Guardian and elvish origin. It’s suggested he has some level of magical sensitivity, which helps him navigate the mystical forces at play within the Cult.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Tolgar is firmly aligned with the Cult of the Sent Ones and holds a powerful position as a Senior Council Member. He also plays a crucial role in the Council of the Sent Ones and has an influential presence in the ongoing internal battles and political strife within the Cult.

Appearance

Tolgar stands tall for a male dwarf, his body muscular and battle-hardened. He wears dark robes, often smudged with dirt and blood, signifying his many trials. His thick beard and sharp, perceptive eyes give him an imposing, wise presence. Adorned with ritualistic symbols, he carries the weight of his role as a leader, constantly on edge, yet calm in the face of uncertainty.

Character Arc/Goal

Tolgar's arc revolves around maintaining control over the Cult of the Sent Ones, despite internal divisions and distrust. As the Cult's leader, he must rebuild its foundation, uncover hidden knowledge, and protect his followers. His leadership is challenged as he tries to balance survival, the ancient prophecy, and the demands of his followers while navigating the growing crisis. His ultimate goal is to restore faith in the Sent Ones and guide them through their darkest hour.

Secret

Tolgar hides the true purpose of the Cult’s mission and is manipulating others' beliefs to serve his own spiritual goals. He also harbors secrets regarding the Unbound’s involvement and possibly is haunted by the weight of past losses. His hidden agendas, though masked by his leadership, are a key part of his internal conflict.

Combat Training

Tolgar is well-trained in diplomacy and leadership, but his physical combat skills are equally strong. He is skilled in swordsmanship, axe fighting, and defensive tactics. His knowledge of dwarven combat techniques makes him a powerful warrior, and he has proven to be capable of handling both leadership challenges and physical threats alike.
