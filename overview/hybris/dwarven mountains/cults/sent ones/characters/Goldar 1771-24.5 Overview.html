Birth Year

Goldar was born in 1381, making him a long-lived and influential figure within the Cult of the Sent Ones. His many years of existence have allowed him to accumulate vast spiritual knowledge and power.

Role/Background

Goldar serves as the High Leader of the Cult of the Sent Ones, a powerful and secretive faction with far-reaching influence. He is a central figure in the cult’s machinations, guiding its direction and shaping its secretive goals. His leadership is strong, but cracks have begun to appear in his control as certain followers question his authority.

Personality Traits

Goldar is a figure of great authority and command. His personality can be described as:

  • Commanding and authoritative, maintaining an iron grip on the Cult of the Sent Ones.
  • Insightful, with a deep understanding of spiritual and metaphysical forces, which he uses to manipulate and guide others.
  • Goldar’s leadership can sometimes seem distant, as he remains focused on his greater goals, even as doubts begin to form around him.

Abilities/Magic

Goldar’s powers are rooted in his spiritual influence and his vast knowledge of ancient rituals and practices. He has access to forbidden knowledge that strengthens his control over others and enhances his influence within the Cult of the Sent Ones. His magical abilities are more metaphysical, tied to the manipulation of energy and minds, rather than direct combat magic.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Goldar is deeply embedded within the Cult of the Sent Ones, which he leads. His allegiance is solely to the cult, though he is strategic in forming alliances that further his goals. His true motivations may lie beyond mere survival and involve manipulating those like Lucas and Mina for the cult's larger schemes.

Appearance

Goldar is a tall, imposing figure, often described as having a long white beard and wearing intricate robes of gold and black. His appearance commands attention and respect, serving as a reflection of his high status within the Cult of the Sent Ones. His presence is both awe-inspiring and unnerving, marking him as a powerful spiritual leader.

Character Arc/Goal

Goldar’s journey is one of maintaining control over his cult and advancing its secretive and potentially dangerous goals. His once unchallenged leadership is beginning to falter as cultists question his authority. His long-term goals, especially regarding Lucas and Mina, remain hidden, and the true extent of his manipulation is still unknown. Goldar must navigate these challenges while continuing to work towards the Cult’s greater purpose.

Secret

Goldar’s true long-term goal is not simply to survive the growing Empire but to use Lucas and Mina’s powers for the cult’s larger purposes. The full extent of his plans remains unclear, but it’s clear that his motivations are far-reaching and involve manipulating those around him to secure the future of the Cult of the Sent Ones.
