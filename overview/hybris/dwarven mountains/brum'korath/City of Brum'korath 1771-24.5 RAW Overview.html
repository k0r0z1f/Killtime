Population

The population includes civilians, soldiers, and cultists. Amid the chaos, these groups are struggling to survive, with some defending the city and others turning to the Disciple's cause.

Appearance

Brum'korath, once a proud city, now shows signs of severe damage. Twisted stone pillars rise above shattered walls, the aftermath of the Disciple's devastating earth magic. The skyline is now marked by ruined spires, while the streets are littered with debris and crumbling buildings.

Energy & Atmosphere

The city is in a state of panic, with destruction and fear permeating the air. The Disciple’s looming presence only heightens the tension, making it feel as though the city itself is holding its breath, awaiting further devastation.

Architecture

The architecture of Brum'korath is majestic, with many stone structures standing tall despite their damage. The Disciple's earth magic has shattered much of the city's integrity, leaving ruins scattered amidst military fortifications hastily constructed in recent years.

Symbolism & Carvings

The city’s carvings depict symbols of power and protection, though many have been defaced or destroyed by the Disciple’s influence. These symbols once conveyed strength and unity, but now tell the story of the city’s fall.

Defenses

Brum'korath’s defenses are minimal, with the Disciple's overwhelming power rendering most of the city's fortifications ineffective. The military and cultists are struggling to hold their ground, but the city’s outer defenses have been severely compromised.

Legends & Lore

Local legends speak of a weapon hidden within a secret temple, a weapon that could potentially turn the tide against the Disciple and the Empire. This artifact plays a key role in the city's myths and in the ongoing struggle for control.

Hidden Dangers

The city’s remaining structures are unstable, and tremors from the Disciple’s magic only increase the risk of collapse. Hidden beneath the ruins are further dangers, including forgotten magic and hidden traps set by the Disciple’s forces.

Link to the Party

The party becomes directly involved in Brum'korath's defense. Whether as defenders or seekers of the hidden weapon, their actions could significantly shape the city’s future, as they confront both physical and magical dangers.

Surrounding Terrain

Brum'korath is surrounded by craggy mountains and uneven terrain, which makes the city both strategically advantageous and vulnerable. The harsh landscape limits movement and makes it difficult for outside forces to approach or retreat.

Weather

The skies above Brum'korath are choked with dust, remnants of the destruction caused by the Disciple. Tremors continue to shake the earth, and the weather is chaotic, with unpredictable storms adding to the sense of despair.

Recent Developments

The city is in a state of crisis. The Disciple’s assault continues unabated, and the military is retreating to the armory. The balance of power is shifting, and Brum'korath's future is uncertain, hinging on the discovery of the weapon and the party’s intervention.
