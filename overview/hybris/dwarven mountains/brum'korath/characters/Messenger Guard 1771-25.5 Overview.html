Birth Year

The Messenger Guard's birth year is unknown, as their role focuses more on duty and function rather than personal history.

Role/Background

The Messenger Guard is a trusted member of the Dwarven Council, serving as both a guard and messenger. Their role is vital in the secure and efficient transmission of messages across Dwarven territories. Their job includes ensuring the safety of the royal orders and relaying critical information between different factions of the Council.

Personality Traits

Messenger Guards are typically neutral in their approach, focused on their duty rather than personal feelings. They are known for being efficient, direct, and dependable. Their role demands a level of professionalism and composure, as they often find themselves in delicate situations where impartiality is required.

Abilities/Magic

The Messenger Guard has no magical abilities. They rely on physical and tactical training for their duties, especially when it comes to the delivery of important messages under potentially dangerous circumstances.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Messenger Guard is directly aligned with the Dwarven Council, following their commands and working in close proximity to other Dwarven military and political figures to ensure the safety and efficiency of communication.

Appearance

As is typical of most dwarves, the Messenger Guard has a short, stocky build, and wears protective armor suited for their role. They are often seen carrying a scroll, sealed with the royal insignia of the Dwarven Council, which signifies their position and importance.

Character Arc/Goal

The Messenger Guard's primary goal is to faithfully deliver messages and maintain the security of those important communications. They may encounter obstacles that challenge their neutrality or force them into action, but their overarching mission remains focused on fulfilling their duty to the Council.

Secret

There are no known secrets for the Messenger Guard. They are a straightforward character, focused solely on the completion of their mission and maintaining their integrity.

Combat Training

The Messenger Guard has basic guard training, allowing them to defend themselves if needed. Their primary strength lies in their ability to remain calm under pressure and ensure the safety of the message they carry, but they are trained to handle situations involving combat if necessary.

Inventory

The Messenger Guard carries a scroll sealed with the royal seal of the Dwarven Council. This scroll represents their official duties and must be delivered safely to the intended recipients. They may also carry basic gear for personal protection and survival on their journeys.
