Birth Year

The exact birth year of the Young Brum'korath Council Member is unknown, but they are young for a council member, possibly in their early thirties by dwarven standards.

Role/Background

The Young Brum'korath Council Member serves as a member of the prestigious Brum'korath Council, a governing body that oversees the strategic and political decisions of the dwarven kingdom. This individual is a skeptic of Goldar, a figure gaining power within the kingdom, and has been vocal in questioning Goldar's true motives and intentions.

Personality Traits

The Young Council Member is known for being:

  • Skeptical about the actions of those around them, especially regarding Goldar’s rise to power.
  • Critical of others, particularly those who blindly follow Goldar or act out of self-interest rather than the good of Brum'korath.
  • Determined to protect the integrity of the dwarven council and ensure Brum'korath's safety, even if it means making difficult decisions.

Abilities/Magic

The Young Brum'korath Council Member does not possess any magical abilities. Their strength lies in political maneuvering and understanding the intricate relationships within the council and the kingdom.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

As a member of the Dwarven Council, the Young Council Member is aligned with the dwarven people and their governing body. Their skepticism of Goldar places them at odds with certain factions but still within the bounds of the council's politics.

Appearance

The Young Brum'korath Council Member is typically seen with a short, stocky build, common among the dwarves. They have sharp eyes that convey intelligence and a well-kept beard, which is customary in dwarven culture. Their attire includes the robes of the council, adorned with various insignias of authority.

Character Arc/Goal

The main goal of the Young Brum'korath Council Member is to ensure the safety of Brum'korath and its people. They are determined to maintain order within the council while secretly investigating Goldar’s intentions. The rising tensions and shifting allegiances put them in a precarious position, but their determination and loyalty to Brum'korath’s ideals remain steadfast.

Secret

Unknown to most, the Young Brum'korath Council Member harbors suspicions about Goldar and his true intentions. They believe that Goldar’s rise is part of a larger scheme to undermine the council's power and the dwarven kingdom’s stability. They are working quietly to uncover the truth without alerting Goldar’s supporters.

Combat Training

While the Young Brum'korath Council Member is not a warrior by trade, they have received basic training in dwarven warfare and tactics to understand the practical side of defending their people. However, their true strength lies in their intellect and political acumen.

Inventory

The Young Council Member carries a council insignia to signify their authority, communication scrolls for sending secret messages, and a small dagger for personal defense.
