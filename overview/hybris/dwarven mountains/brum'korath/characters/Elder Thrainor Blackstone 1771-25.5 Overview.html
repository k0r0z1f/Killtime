Birth Year

Elder Thrainor Blackstone was born in 1771, a long-lived Dwarven leader who has seen his fair share of change in the underground halls of Brum'korath.

Role/Background

Thrainor serves as the Elder of the Brum’korath Council, overseeing the safety and governance of his people. He has long acted as a guide in the tunnels beneath Brum’korath, leading the dwarves through the treacherous underground passages that keep their civilization safe. His leadership is marked by wisdom and respect.

Personality Traits

Thrainor is a figure of great firmness and authority. He is stoic and often quiet, preferring to listen and observe before acting. He carries himself with a reverent and grave demeanor, respecting the traditions of his people while being careful not to rush into decisions without thorough thought.

Abilities/Magic

Thrainor does not possess any magical abilities. His power lies in his leadership, wisdom, and knowledge of Brum'korath’s history and tunnels.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Thrainor is a central figure within the Brum'korath Council, and his alliances are primarily within his own people. His influence over the dwarves is respected, and he ensures the council’s decisions reflect the needs and safety of the Brum'korath people.

Appearance

Thrainor is a short, stocky figure, typical of the dwarven physique. His long, graying beard and sharp eyes give him a commanding presence. Though his body is not as youthful as it once was, his stature and demeanor make him seem much larger than life, exuding an aura of wisdom and experience.

Character Arc/Goal

Thrainor’s primary goal is to maintain Brum’korath's sovereignty against external threats and internal turmoil. As a leader of his people, he is dedicated to ensuring the survival and prosperity of his kingdom. Throughout the story, he seeks to guide his people through turbulent times, trying to balance tradition with the changing world around him.

Secret

Thrainor harbors a secret knowledge about an ancient artifact buried deep beneath Brum’korath, one that could hold the key to the future of his people. Though he is aware of its power, he is hesitant to reveal it due to the potential consequences it might bring.

Combat Training

While Thrainor is no stranger to combat, having fought in his youth, he now prefers diplomacy and leadership over direct battle. His experience in war is more in the art of strategy and guiding his people through challenging times, though he is still capable of defending himself if necessary.

Inventory

Thrainor's belongings include old maps of the Brum'korath tunnels, a ceremonial axe used in council meetings, a cloak of authority to signify his status, and personal writings that document the history and decisions of the Brum'korath people.
