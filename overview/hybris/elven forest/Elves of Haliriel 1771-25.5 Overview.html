Birth Year

The Elves of Haliriel do not follow a traditional calendar, and their birth dates are not documented in the same manner as other cultures. Their lives are measured in centuries, often taking a more timeless approach to aging and history.

Role/Background

The Elves of Haliriel are an elusive and secretive group whose history is deeply entwined with the prophecy and powerful artifacts. They possess knowledge that is crucial to the events unfolding in the present, and while they have remained hidden for many years, their ties to the prophecy now compel them to make formal contact with the party. Their alliance with other factions is delicate, and they must carefully navigate their position to protect their ancient knowledge.

Personality Traits

The Elves of Haliriel are defined by their secrecy and caution. They are a strategic group, always thinking several steps ahead and operating from the shadows to ensure their survival and the preservation of their knowledge. While not inherently aggressive, they are willing to take calculated risks to protect the sacred truths they hold.

Abilities/Magic

While the Elves of Haliriel are not known for traditional spellcasting, they possess deep arcane knowledge tied to the prophecy and artifacts that could wield great power if properly unlocked. Their wisdom in using magical objects and ancient knowledge could be a crucial asset to the party.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Elves of Haliriel are an independent elven faction, standing apart from other elven groups due to their unique role in the prophecy. They have no allegiance to kingdoms or empires, instead focusing on the preservation of their ancient knowledge and their connection to the unfolding events. They are tied to the prophecy and have been watching the party's actions with keen interest.

Appearance

The Elves of Haliriel are characterized by their enigmatic and secretive appearance, often seen in muted, natural tones that blend seamlessly with their surroundings. They carry an air of mystery and aloofness, seldom revealing their true emotions or intentions to outsiders. Their physical appearance may vary, but they are generally tall, with sharp features and an otherworldly grace.

Character Arc/Goal

The main goal of the Elves of Haliriel is to reveal or protect their knowledge related to the prophecy. Their involvement with the party grows as they weigh the risks of sharing their wisdom. Their actions will likely involve a balance between revealing critical information to aid the party and keeping their secrets to protect their ancient knowledge.

Secret

The Elves of Haliriel hold a deep, hidden secret about the true nature of the prophecy and its potential consequences. They are cautious about sharing too much, fearing that revealing certain truths may cause irreversible damage to the world or to themselves.

Combat Training

While the Elves of Haliriel are not primarily warriors, they are skilled in the art of diplomacy and are adept at navigating tense situations. However, if forced into combat, they are capable of defending themselves with grace and precision, using their knowledge of ancient elven techniques and their deep connection to nature.

Inventory

The Elves of Haliriel typically carry few material possessions. Their focus lies in their ancient knowledge and the sacred artifacts they protect. Any physical items they possess are likely to be relics or tools related to the prophecy, possibly including magical objects that can influence the fate of the world.
