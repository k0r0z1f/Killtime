Population

Haliriel's population is primarily elven, with a mix of human visitors in the bustling Market District. The upper districts are more exclusive, inhabited mainly by the elven elite and scholars.

Appearance

Majestic trees intertwine seamlessly with Haliriel's architectural marvels. The vibrant canopy of iridescent Serran trees casts shimmering hues over cobblestone paths. The Market District boasts a fusion of human and elven styles.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere in Haliriel shifts between the tranquil serenity of the Second District and the vibrant energy of the Market District. The city resonates with a harmonious blend of natural and magical energy.

Architecture

Haliriel's architecture is a seamless blend of organic forms and elegant elven craftsmanship. Its carved stairways lead to elevated parks that offer breathtaking views of the surrounding forests.

Symbolism & Carvings

The iridescent Serran trees serve as a central symbol in Haliriel. Their leaves, reflecting light in countless colors, signify the city's reverence for nature and its mystical harmony.

Defenses

Guards are stationed at stairways connecting districts, and permits are required for outsiders to access the upper levels. These measures maintain the city's security and exclusivity.

Legends & Lore

Haliriel is often referred to as the Citadel of the Elves, a place where natural harmony and elven culture converge. Legends tell of its founding and the ancient beings who shaped its destiny.

Link to the Party

Haliriel serves as a destination for Candice and Jarek and a meeting point with Lucas and Mina. Its connection to the Sent Ones' prophecy adds depth to the party's journey.

Surrounding Terrain

Haliriel is surrounded by ancient forests, clear lakes, and vibrant flora, making the journey to the city both enchanting and challenging. Its environment is as much a part of its identity as its architecture.

Weather

Haliriel experiences warm and golden daylight, with mesmerizing hues during sunset. Evenings are tranquil, offering a peaceful retreat from the day's activities.
