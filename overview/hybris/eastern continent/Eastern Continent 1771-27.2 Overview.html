Appearance

The Eastern Continent features varied regions, some of which remain untouched by colonization. The land is untamed and wild, with landscapes that evoke both awe and danger.

Energy & Atmosphere

The energy of the Eastern Continent is one of grave danger. It is untamed and unpredictable, especially due to the presence of the Disciple of Air, whose influence is felt strongly across the land.

Architecture

The architecture across the continent is varied. Some regions feature very rare ancient structures, remnants of a time long past.

Symbolism & Carvings

Details regarding symbolism and carvings are not provided for this location.

Defenses

The defenses of the Eastern Continent involve natural barriers, such as mountains, forests, and other obstacles that make the land difficult to navigate and conquer.

Legends & Lore

The Eastern Continent is known as a site of grave danger, particularly due to its connection to the Disciple of Air, a powerful figure whose influence spreads across the land.

Hidden Dangers

The Disciple of Air’s power is weakening its containment, which could lead to unforeseen consequences for the continent.

Link to the Party

The Eastern Continent is connected to the Sent Ones’ prophecy and is closely tied to the Disciple of Air, whose presence will play a significant role in the party's future adventures.

Surrounding Terrain

The surrounding terrain of the Eastern Continent is diverse, featuring untamed wilderness, rugged landscapes, and wild regions that have yet to be explored or tamed.

Weather

The weather on the Eastern Continent is unpredictable, with sudden changes in climate and conditions that make travel difficult and dangerous.

Recent Developments

Recent events have seen the capture of the Disciple of Air, but the continent remains an uncertain and distant place for the group. It may serve as a potential future location for their travels, although its precise role remains unclear.
