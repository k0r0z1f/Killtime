Population

The Eastern Continent hosts a varied population, including nomads, tribal communities, and some settlers from the Empire. Some regions, however, remain entirely untouched by colonization, preserving ancient cultures and beliefs.

Appearance

The continent’s appearance is diverse, with dense, lush jungles filled with hidden dangers, vast barren plains, and towering mountain ranges. There are regions untouched by time, where nature reigns supreme and few dare to venture.

Energy & Atmosphere

The energy in the Eastern Continent is unpredictable, much like the Disciple of Air itself. The land feels volatile, as though ancient powers are awakening beneath its surface. A grave sense of danger looms over much of the land, tempered by the unpredictable and untamed nature of the place.

Architecture

Architectural styles vary across the continent. Some regions still boast ancient stone temples and arcane-infused structures, while others are filled with natural barriers like dense jungles and mountains. Few man-made structures are found here, with nature itself standing as the greatest form of defense.

Symbolism & Carvings

Ancient symbols and carvings are rare, but when found, they are often tied to powerful forces of nature or the Disciple of Air. These markings are mostly hidden in remote places, their meanings long lost to most.

Defenses

The continent's natural defenses are formidable, with dense jungles, untamed wildlife, and towering mountains making travel difficult. The presence of the Disciple of Air also serves as a form of defense, though his power is weakening.

Legends & Lore

The Eastern Continent is steeped in ancient myths and legends, many of which concern the Disciple of Air and the ancient powers he once controlled. It is said that the continent holds secrets of immense power, waiting to be unlocked by those brave or foolish enough to venture into its depths.

Hidden Dangers

The biggest danger in the Eastern Continent is the Disciple of Air, whose containment is weakening. His power could once again tear through the land, and ancient forces stir beneath the surface, waiting to be unleashed.

Link to the Party

The Eastern Continent is intrinsically tied to the Sent Ones’ prophecy and the Disciple of Air’s presence. The party’s involvement here could change the course of history, as they face the growing threat of the Disciple’s power and unravel the secrets hidden in the continent's depths.

Surrounding Terrain

The terrain surrounding the Eastern Continent is wild and untamed. Thick jungles, deep valleys, and towering mountains make it difficult to traverse. Many areas remain unexplored due to the dangers lurking within.

Weather

The weather is unpredictable, with sudden violent storms and heavy rains that can last for days. There are occasional calm spells, but they are brief, and the land is never fully at peace.

Recent Developments

The Disciple of Air has been captured, but his power still lingers, making the land a volatile and dangerous place. Factions are already preparing for the possibility of his return, and tensions within the continent grow by the day.
