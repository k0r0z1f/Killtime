Population

The Nythari Tribe consists of around 280 elves, with Candice’s family and tribe members being the core of the community. It’s a small, tight-knit group that values connection to nature and spiritual tradition.

Appearance

The Nythari Tribe is resilient and connected to nature. They have an elegant and striking appearance, with amber eyes and long brown hair, blending harmoniously with their environment.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere in Candice’s village is calm and grounded, with an ancient connection to nature. While peaceful, there is an underlying tension due to the constant threat of external forces.

Architecture

The tribe’s architecture is simple and nature-integrated. Their huts are made from wood, stone, and plants, and they blend seamlessly into the surrounding jungle. Treehouses and sacred sites are common structures.

Symbolism & Carvings

Symbols of the mountain hawk and carvings tied to ancestral beliefs are prominent. These symbols reflect the tribe’s deep spiritual connection to nature and the land.

Defenses

The village is protected by stealth-based traps, natural obstacles, and Nythari warriors. The tribe’s strong connection to nature offers a form of magical defense, although there are no advanced fortifications.

Legends & Lore

The Nythari are protectors of The Watcher, an ancient entity tied to forces older than the Empire. The tribe’s ancestors survived an exile from the Empire, which led to a spirit of resilience and defiance.

Hidden Dangers

The jungle is both a protector and a danger. External threats, such as mysterious strangers or competing factions, may pose a challenge to the tribe’s safety.

Link to the Party

Candice’s village is where she plans to vouch for her companions’ entry. She has a deep sense of responsibility towards her people and home.

Surrounding Terrain

The surrounding terrain consists of dense jungle, steep mountain slopes, high cliffs, rivers, and valleys. Thick vegetation makes the area hard to navigate, with occasional hidden ruins adding mystery to the land.

Weather

The climate is warm and humid, with sporadic rain showers typical of jungle environments. At higher elevations, it is cooler.

Recent Developments

There have been rumors surrounding the explosion in Kingston and potential connections to the Children of Hybris. Candice’s departure from the village signifies her readiness to fulfill her mission.
