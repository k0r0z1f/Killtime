Birth Year

1754

Role/Background

Candice is a Nythari warrior, apprentice to the esteemed mentor Ceylan. She has embarked on a journey to find two "children of Hybris" (Lucas and Mina), believing that they hold the key to preventing future catastrophes. She is both an adventurer and an outsider, navigating a world she doesn't entirely belong to while fulfilling her mentor's guidance and discovering her own path.

Personality Traits

Candice is confident, resilient, and deeply connected to nature. She is compassionate, resourceful, and protective of her tribe's traditions. Despite her strength, she carries a sense of unease about her future task, balanced by optimism and strong intuition. She is empathetic to others' struggles and introspective about her role in the world, making her both grounded and deeply thoughtful.

Abilities/Magic

Candice does not possess overt magical abilities, but she is highly skilled in survival techniques, climbing, and agile combat, having been trained in the ways of the Nythari tribe. Her connection to nature enhances her abilities, and her agility allows her to navigate difficult terrain with ease.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Candice is part of the Nythari Tribe and is deeply connected to her mentor, Ceylan, who has guided her throughout her journey. She is also indirectly tied to the *Cult of the Sent Ones*, through the prophecy that links her to the children of Hybris.

Appearance

Candice is tall with a slender build, her earthy-toned skin complementing the natural hues of her environment. She has long dark hair that she wears in a braid, and amber eyes that reflect the depth of her thoughts. Her attire consists of light, fitted leather armor in forest tones, and she wears a woven cloak with a mountain hawk brooch, symbolizing her tribe. Her overall appearance is both strong and worn, indicative of the trials she has faced.
