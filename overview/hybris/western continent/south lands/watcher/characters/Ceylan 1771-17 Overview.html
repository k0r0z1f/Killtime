Role/Background

Ceylan serves as a mentor to Candice and resides in a secluded cave on The Watcher mountain. He is integral to guiding Candice on her mission to find the Children of Hybris and protect the world from impending threats.

Personality Traits

Ceylan is an eccentric and wise individual, deeply connected to the land around him. He is generally quiet and contemplative, though when necessary, he can display rare urgency. He is somewhat cryptic, occasionally manipulative, and forgetful at times, making him a complex figure to interact with.

Abilities/Magic

Ceylan's abilities are tied to his deep spiritual connection with The Watcher. This connection grants him insight and a sense of ancient knowledge, though the extent of his magical powers is unclear.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Ceylan is aligned with the Nythari Tribe, a group known for their connection to nature and their spiritual beliefs. His role within this tribe, as well as his relationship with them, is tied to his mission to guide Candice.

Appearance

Ceylan has an aged and weathered appearance, marked by a long, full beard. His physical form reflects years of living in solitude in the mountains, with deep lines etched into his face and a gaze that holds ancient wisdom.

Character Arc/Goal

Ceylan's primary goal is to guide Candice on her mission to locate the Children of Hybris and to ensure the safety of the world. Throughout this journey, he may uncover dark knowledge about the prophecy that could alter the course of their mission.

Secret

Ceylan suspects the rise of the Empire and its Champion, suggesting that he has a deeper understanding of the prophecy. He hints at possessing dark knowledge that could significantly impact the fate of the world, though he has not fully revealed these insights.

Combat Training

Ceylan's combat training is largely spiritual, as his power stems from his connection to The Watcher. His combat abilities are not traditionally martial but are rooted in ancient wisdom and spiritual insight.
