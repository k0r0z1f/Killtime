Birth Year

Candice was born in 1721, making her 49 years old in the current year, 1771.

Role/Background

Candice is a member of the Nythari tribe, a skilled warrior, and an apprentice to Ceylan. Her role as a Seeker involves protecting her people and finding those fated by prophecy.

Personality Traits

Candice is agile, confident, and curious, yet torn between her loyalty to Ceylan and other forces. Her introspective nature reflects her inner conflicts, and her protective instincts make her deeply committed to nature and the worlds she seeks to protect.

Abilities/Magic

Her skills include expert-level survival techniques, climbing, stealth, archery, blow darts, and short blades. Candice is a highly capable warrior and survivalist, adapting quickly to various environments.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Candice currently counts Jarek, a guide and ally, as part of her circle but has not yet allied with Lucas and Mina, despite her growing doubts about Ceylan’s intentions.

Appearance

Candice is athletically built, with striking amber eyes and long brown hair styled in a treaded fashion. She wears forest-toned leather armor, a cloak, and carries a mountain hawk brooch, signifying her tribe’s connection to nature.

Character Arc/Goal

Candice’s goal is to protect the worlds by guarding the two 'Children of Hybris' and uncovering the truth behind her role in an ancient prophecy. She is trying to reconcile her growing internal conflict as she learns more about Lucas and Mina.

Secret

Candice struggles with the uncertainty of an internal chaotic presence that Lucas claims exists in her mind. She is also torn between loyalty to Ceylan and the revelations she’s learning about Lucas and Mina being the Children of Hybris.

Combat Training

Candice is highly skilled in stealth, climbing, survival tactics, and various forms of combat, including archery, blow darts, and short blades. Her training makes her a deadly opponent in both close-quarters and ranged combat.

Inventory

Candice carries a pouch of iridescent stones (used as Derek’s currency), a small stone fragment from Nythari ruins, a sarbacane (blow dart weapon), and Nythari survival gear for her travels.
