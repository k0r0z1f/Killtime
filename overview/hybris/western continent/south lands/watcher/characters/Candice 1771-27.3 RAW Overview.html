Role/Background

Candice is a member of the Nythari Tribe, tasked with bringing Lucas and Mina to the Watcher as part of a larger game involving the Elven Council. She is a key figure in the unfolding prophecy, and her mission's success is crucial for the balance of events. She is concerned about the risks involved and advocates for consulting the Watcher for guidance on the uncertain path ahead.

Personality Traits

Candice remains composed under pressure but is wary and insightful. She is often quiet, firm, and sincere, carrying the burden of her mission. Her personality also reflects a deep curiosity and openness, though she can become frustrated when others, like Altheris, dismiss the importance of the Watcher. Her determination and strong will make her a steadfast ally in the face of adversity.

Abilities/Magic

Candice’s abilities are tied to a chaotic presence she struggles to control, possibly linked to her Nythari heritage. She may also have knowledge of the Watcher and its importance, which is central to the unfolding events of the prophecy.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Candice is part of the Nythari Tribe, and although she is under the temporary protection of the Elven Council, her allegiance lies with the Watcher. She also belongs to a faction within the Nythari Tribe that values the Watcher's guidance in the unfolding prophecy.

Appearance

Candice has striking amber eyes and a graceful, elegant appearance, reflecting the pride she takes in her Nythari heritage. Her firm demeanor conveys her sense of purpose and determination in fulfilling her mission.

Character Arc/Goal

Her primary goal is to ensure Lucas and Mina’s safe return to the Watcher. Candice believes the Watcher holds the key to solving the mystery of the prophecy and wants to guide the group to it. She faces an internal conflict about her role in the prophecy and the uncertain future of the world.

Secret

Candice believes deeply in the prophecy concerning Lucas and Mina, and her mission is intrinsically tied to it. However, she harbors a chaotic presence within her that remains dormant, suggesting she might play an unexpected role in the events to come.

Combat Training

Candice has received some combat training due to the nature of her mission, preparing her to face both physical and magical challenges in the course of her journey.

Inventory

Candice carries a special pass granted by the Elven Council, which provides her with certain privileges and protections during her travels.
