Location Name

The Hidden Temple serves as the central point of worship and secrecy for Vermicalis and his tribe. Its name reflects its elusive nature, concealed from outsiders and protected by those who revere its sacred grounds.

Population

The Hidden Temple is inhabited by Vermicalis and his tribe's village. This close-knit community is dedicated to the temple's upkeep and the preservation of its ancient traditions. The villagers are skilled in various crafts and rituals essential to maintaining the temple's sanctity.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere surrounding the Hidden Temple is ominous and foreboding, creating a palpable sense of danger. The area feels charged with an ancient energy that warns intruders of the perils that lie within, making it a place both revered and feared.

Defenses

The Hidden Temple is well-protected, guarded by Vermicalis and his warriors. These defenders are trained in both combat and the use of ancient defensive mechanisms that deter and repel any potential threats seeking to uncover the temple's secrets.

Legends & Lore

The Hidden Temple plays a pivotal role in the Children of Hybris prophecy. It is believed to hold the secrets necessary to fulfill or prevent the foretold events. Legends speak of ancient rites performed here and the divine interventions that have shaped its history.

Hidden Dangers

The presence of a strange man with unclear intentions poses a significant threat to the Hidden Temple. His motives are unknown, and his actions could bring unforeseen dangers to the temple and its inhabitants, potentially disrupting the balance foretold by the prophecy.

Link to the Party

The Hidden Temple is a vital location tied to the Children of Hybris prophecy, making it a focal point for the party's quest. The stranger searching for the temple adds urgency, as his intentions could either aid or endanger the party's mission to uncover the prophecy's secrets.

Surrounding Terrain

The Hidden Temple is situated on the eastern slope of a mountain, enveloped by mountainous jungle terrain. The rugged landscape offers both natural protection and challenges, with dense foliage and steep inclines making access difficult for outsiders.
