The Watcher (Location Overview)

Attribute Details
Population
Appearance A sacred, mystical place in a tall mountain, with a mysterious, enigmatic presence.
Energy & Atmosphere The energy is heavy with ancient knowledge, yet filled with challenge, a sense of foreboding and gravity, tied to prophecies and fate.
Architecture
Symbolism & Carvings Inscribed with prophecy-related symbols, linked to the greater fate.
Defenses
Legends & Lore The Watcher is tied to the prophecy involving the Children of Hybris, and the true nature of the prophecy can be revealed here, tied to tests and visions.
Hidden Dangers May show truths that are difficult to bear.
Link to the Party Candice’s mission is to bring Lucas and Mina to the Watcher; the Queen has planned a journey there and insists it must happen soon.
Surrounding Terrain High mountain, harsh and difficult terrain.
Weather Cold, high-altitude climate.
Recent Developments Mentioned as part of Candice’s mission and the prophecy. The Queen has planned a journey there and insists it must happen soon.
