Population

The population of The Watcher consists mainly of the Nythari Tribe, a small and isolated elven community. Ceylan, an old man living in a cave high in the mountain, also resides here along with a few reclusive guardians and worshipers.

Appearance

The Watcher is a jagged, solitary mountain peak rising above lush jungles and valleys. Its steep and rugged slopes culminate in an exposed upper region, with the towering, jagged peak being surrounded at the base by elvish figures engaged in ceremonial and worshipful activities.

Energy & Atmosphere

The land around The Watcher exudes an ancient and spiritual energy, heavy with reverence and sacredness. It carries an almost foreboding atmosphere, filled with ancient knowledge, challenges, and a strong connection to prophecies and fate.

Architecture

The mountain's surrounding villages are small and scattered, with primitive huts made from natural materials. Caves are occasionally used by the tribe. The Watcher itself stands as a natural, towering mountain, with the land considered a place of pilgrimage.

Symbolism & Carvings

The caves surrounding The Watcher are adorned with Nythari-style paintings and carvings. These symbols, including those related to prophecy, speak of the mountain’s role as a spirit guardian and its connection to the fate of the Nythari people.

Defenses

The natural defenses of The Watcher come from its steep, rugged terrain, making it difficult to access. These rugged natural barriers protect the mountain from unwanted visitors.

Legends & Lore

The Nythari believe The Watcher is a guardian spirit, whose presence offers protection to those who understand its secrets. Legends also speak of an ancient watcher who guards the mountain against intruders or those seeking forbidden knowledge.

Hidden Dangers

Ceylan warns of unknown arcane threats, and there are rumors that the Empire might seek to interfere in the region. Hidden magical forces could be tied to forces seeking to corrupt the guardian of The Watcher.

Link to the Party

The Watcher plays a pivotal role in Candice’s quest. She must bring Lucas and Mina to the mountain as part of the Queen's planned journey. It holds a spiritual link for Candice, offering guidance and protection for her mission.

Surrounding Terrain

The Watcher is surrounded by dense jungles, rivers, and valleys. The harsh and difficult terrain is complemented by the towering mountain itself, making it both an asset and a challenge for those seeking to approach.

Weather

The weather is cold and misty, with occasional east winds. Storms are frequent, particularly during the colder months, with winds howling over the high-altitude regions.

Recent Developments

An explosion in Kingston, possibly tied to arcane forces, has raised tensions. Ceylan warns that the Empire could be involved, and The Watcher is seen as crucial for the protection against potential evil forces.
