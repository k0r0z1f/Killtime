Population

The Nythari Tribe's village is home to approximately 280 elves, forming a closely-knit community. This population includes Candice’s friends and fellow tribe members, all of whom share a strong bond and work together to maintain the harmony and safety of their village.

Appearance

The elves of the Nythari Tribe are resilient and deeply connected to nature, boasting an elegant and striking appearance. A dominant trait among them is their amber eyes, complemented by brown hair. Their attire features warm, earthy tones that blend seamlessly with their surrounding environment of dense jungles and large trees. The village is characterized by natural structures such as treehouses and humble huts, with lantern-lit interiors that enhance the natural beauty of their habitat.

Energy & Atmosphere

The village exudes a calm and grounded energy, reflecting their ancient connection to nature. While the overall atmosphere is peaceful, there is an underlying tension due to the threat of outside forces. The community is respectful and maintains a sense of ancient resilience, fostering a deeply rooted connection to their natural surroundings. The environment is harmonious and reverent, alive with communal focus and hope, often accompanied by traditional chanting by the elders.

Architecture

The architecture of the Nythari Tribe is simple yet highly integrated with nature. Their huts are constructed from wood, stone, and plants, ensuring they blend seamlessly into the dense jungle environment. The village features natural structures such as treehouses and sacred sites, all interwoven with the surrounding vegetation. These wooden and humble structures are designed to coexist harmoniously with the natural landscape, providing both functionality and aesthetic beauty.

Symbolism & Carvings

The village is adorned with emblems of the mountain hawk and other ancestral symbols that reflect their deep ties to nature, the land, and spirituality. Tribal carvings depict ancient tales of survival and resilience against the Empire's oppression. Sacred carvings linked to prophecy are prevalent, and villagers often carry woven charms, dried herbs, and small pouches as gifts, symbolizing their cultural heritage and spiritual beliefs.

Defenses

The Nythari Tribe relies on stealth-based traps and natural obstacles to defend their village. Guarded by skilled Nythari warriors, the village takes advantage of its remote jungle location and hidden pathways to deter intruders. Their strong connection to nature serves as a natural defense mechanism, eliminating the need for advanced fortifications while maintaining effective protection.

Legends & Lore

The village is rich with legends of the Watcher, revered as the protectors of the land, and tales of the Children of Hybris, which include ancient prophecies. These stories trace back to their earliest ancestors who survived the Empire's exile fifteen centuries ago, embedding a lasting spirit of resilience and defiance against oppression. Such legends play a crucial role in shaping the tribe's identity and collective memory.

Hidden Dangers

The jungle surrounding the village is both a guardian and a source of peril. While it offers protection through its density and natural barriers, it also harbors dangers such as mysterious strangers seeking to infiltrate the territory. Recently, a strange man has been spotted searching for a hidden temple, and natural threats like mountain tremors and avalanches have become more frequent, posing significant risks to the villagers.

Link to the Party

Candice’s home serves as the central point for the party's entry into the village. As a respected Nythari warrior, Candice guides her companions with a deep sense of responsibility towards her people. The village's reverence for Queen Aphyrosia, who makes rare visits, and the support of individuals like Aria and Eryndor are integral to the party's mission and objectives.

Surrounding Terrain

The village is nestled within a dense jungle, surrounded by steep slopes, high cliffs, rivers, and hidden valleys. The area is overgrown with thick vegetation and contains rare hidden ruins, adding an element of mystery. Towering trees and a thick jungle canopy dominate the landscape, making the terrain both beautiful and challenging to navigate.

Weather

The climate in the Nythari Tribe's region is warm and humid, with occasional storms that bring sporadic rain showers typical of the jungle. Higher elevations experience cooler temperatures, and the air is heavy with the scents of pine and damp earth. The weather contributes to the vibrant and lush environment, though it can also lead to challenging conditions for the villagers.

Recent Developments

Recent events have stirred the village, including discussions about the Kingston explosion and the potential involvement of the Children of Hybris. Candice has departed to fulfill her mission, and the arrival of the Children of Hybris has brought warnings about a strange man seeking a hidden temple. Additionally, recent mountain tremors have caused increased avalanches, heightening the sense of urgency and danger within the community.
