Population

The population of Haliriel is around 2 million, with a predominantly elven population living in the tree districts. Humans and a few dwarves are also present, particularly in the market district where trade and commerce thrive.

Appearance

Haliriel is an ancient city with a harmonious blend of nature and architecture. Towering columns of living wood, intricate designs, and natural elements like glowing Serran trees create an ethereal and majestic atmosphere. Sunlight filters through the thick canopy, giving the city a serene and timeless appearance.

Energy & Atmosphere

The city exudes a calm, serene, and ancient energy. It feels detached from the turmoil of the wider world. There's a mystical serenity in the air, but underlying this calm is a sense of tension and unspoken power, as though the city is holding its breath.

Architecture

Haliriel’s architecture is a perfect marriage of nature and elegant design. Organic elements like trees, vines, and crystal-like structures blend seamlessly with stonework. The city’s buildings are grown from the land, with smooth cobblestone streets in the market district and rope bridges connecting the higher tree districts.

Symbolism & Carvings

Throughout Haliriel, you’ll find carvings and symbols reflecting the city’s reverence for nature and its Elven heritage. Common motifs include leaves, nature symbols, and intricate depictions of the Elven crown, particularly on the Queen’s head, symbolizing strength and purity.

Defenses

The city is well-guarded, with strong defenses formed by natural barriers such as dense forests and rivers. The guards are stationed at key locations, particularly the stairways between districts. Outsiders need permits to access the higher levels of the city, and political intrigue may play a role in its defense strategy.

Legends & Lore

Haliriel is known for its deep connection to nature and magic, with legends of its involvement in the Eastern Continent’s affairs. It’s a city of immense power, entwined with prophecy and the flow of destiny. The Elves protect it fiercely, and outsiders tread carefully.

Hidden Dangers

Despite its serene appearance, Haliriel harbors hidden dangers. There are political forces at play, and tensions rise between factions. The prophecy hanging over the city adds layers of uncertainty, and there may be unseen dangers tied to the shifting political landscape and the party’s involvement in its future.

Link to the Party

Haliriel’s relationship with the party is politically significant. The presence of Candice, Lucas, Mina, and Jarek is part of a larger prophecy, and their involvement with the city has raised concerns among the High Elves. The city may offer sanctuary, but its atmosphere is charged with uncertainty regarding their future role.

Surrounding Terrain

The city is surrounded by lush forests, winding rivers, and mystical landscapes that offer natural protection. The verdant lands are intertwined with the Elven territories, giving the city an aura of secrecy and seclusion.

Weather

Haliriel enjoys a mild, temperate climate with occasional ethereal mists that drift through the trees. The gentle breeze and magical atmosphere create a peaceful environment, though there’s always a tension in the air, thick with the scent of flowers and earth.

Recent Developments

Haliriel remains independent and sovereign, resisting the control of outside forces like the World Police. However, recent discussions about the party’s role in the coming conflict and Candice’s arrival have marked a shift in the city’s fate. The Queen’s presence and her connection to the prophecy are stirring tensions among the High Elves.
