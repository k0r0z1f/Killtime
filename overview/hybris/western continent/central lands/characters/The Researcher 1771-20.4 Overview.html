Role/Background

The Researcher is a former scientist who once served the old regime. Now, with unclear intentions, they are helping Lucas, Mina, and Thomas with their escape. They modify equipment like the van and assist with solving problems, such as the tracking issue. The Researcher’s background and past experiments add layers of complexity to their current motivations, and they continue to navigate their own role within the group.

Personality Traits

The Researcher is earnest and determined, though often urgent and remorseful for past actions. Their desperation to control their fate leads to persistent behavior. They are conflicted, concerned, calm under pressure, but also manipulative when necessary. While composed, they frequently experience stress and irritation due to their complex situation.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Researcher was formerly part of the medi-laboratory in Kingston, where they were involved in the scientific experiments that now tie them to Lucas’s past. Their current allegiances remain unclear, but they are working with the group to help solve the problems they face.

Appearance

The Researcher has sharp features, a strong, intense gaze, and bloodshot eyes, indicating exhaustion. Their fatigued look is a reflection of their current state, caught between their past experiments and the pressure of aiding the group.

Character Arc/Goal

The Researcher’s main goal is to help Lucas unlock his full potential, reveal the truth behind the experiments, and control their fate. They aim to ensure their own safety while assisting Lucas and Mina in their escape and helping with the tracking problem.

Secret

The Researcher harbors a secret: they were responsible for embedding a chip in Lucas’s chest. This chip was later removed by an operation that was ordered by Thomas, adding another layer of mystery to their involvement with Lucas and the group's journey.
