Birth Year

Mina’s Father was born in the year 1712. As of the current date, 03-06-1771, he is 59 years old.

Role/Background

He is a powerful and influential figure in society, holding a high social status and possessing latent political power in the Central Lands. As a successful businessman and the founder of his own company, he has established a significant empire that supports not only his personal endeavors but also his daughter Mina’s athletic competitions.

Personality Traits

Mina’s Father is deeply protective of his adopted daughter, often displaying a controlling nature that stems from his concern for her well-being. Despite his protective instincts, he experiences internal conflict due to Mina’s strong desire for independence, creating a complex dynamic between them.

Abilities/Magic

No abilities or magic have been provided for Mina’s Father.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

He owns his own empire as a businessman and is the founder of a successful company. His wealth and influence have been instrumental in raising Mina, particularly supporting her participation in athletic competitions.

Appearance

Mina’s Father has short grey hair and a finely trimmed gray beard. He has a serious yet nonchalant attitude, standing tall with a commanding presence.

Character Arc/Goal

His main goal is to balance his protective instincts with Mina’s desire for independence. He is actively working with a team of lawyers and detectives to absolve Mina and her party from the incident in Kingston, aiming to remove their "enemy of the state" status and eliminate their threats.

Secret

Despite his public persona as a successful businessman, Mina’s Father is secretly an incognito underground criminal boss. His true intention is to bring balance and achieve real justice within the Central Lands.

Combat Training

Mina’s Father has basic training in martial arts and firearms, allowing him to handle himself in combat situations when necessary.

Inventory

He typically wears a bullet and taser-proof vest that complements his businessman suit. Additionally, he is equipped with the latest implant for communication, which allows him to bypass providers and gain full access to the network in the Central Lands, as well as some support in the Eastern Continent.

Mina

Mina is his beloved adopted daughter, who was taken in at birth. Her biological mother was his beloved sister, adding a layer of personal connection and responsibility to his protective nature.
