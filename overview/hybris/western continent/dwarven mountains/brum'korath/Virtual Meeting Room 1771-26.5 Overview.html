Population

The Virtual Meeting Room is devoid of any physical population. It serves as a sterile, functional environment, created for high-level communication and decision-making.

Appearance

The room is characterized by cold, clinical lighting that casts harsh shadows, giving it a sterile, courtroom-like ambiance. The walls are adorned with sleek digital screens displaying official data and holographic projections, emphasizing its utilitarian nature.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere is heavy with tension and oppressive silence, as though every word spoken carries immense weight. The space is designed for high-stakes confrontations, where every decision could have far-reaching consequences.

Architecture

The Virtual Meeting Room is a stone chamber, combining elements of traditional design with modern technology. Digital screens line the walls, displaying information and facilitating communication, while the minimalistic architecture is intended to keep the focus on the matter at hand.

Symbolism & Carvings

No physical carvings or symbols are present in the room, as it is designed purely for its functional purpose. Its stark, impersonal design reflects the clinical approach of the World Police.

Defenses

The Virtual Meeting Room does not have traditional physical defenses, but it is highly secure from a technological standpoint. The communication systems are encrypted, and only authorized personnel can access the room's functions.

Legends & Lore

There are no legends or lore attached to the room itself. It is purely a tool of the World Police, representing the organization’s power and authority over its enemies and allies alike.

Hidden Dangers

While there are no physical hidden dangers in the room, the true danger lies in the stakes of the decisions made here. The room’s high-stakes atmosphere is designed to pressure individuals into compliance, and the World Police’s power makes it a space of immense political peril.

Link to the Party

The Virtual Meeting Room plays a pivotal role in the party's confrontation with the World Police. It is where the World Police delivers its ultimatum, marking the party as enemies of the state and setting them on a dangerous path.

Surrounding Terrain

The room exists within a digital, artificial environment with no physical terrain surrounding it.

Weather

The Virtual Meeting Room operates under a controlled climate, unaffected by external weather conditions. The temperature and atmosphere are set to maintain a neutral, distraction-free environment.

Recent Developments

Recently, the room was used by the World Police to issue an ultimatum to the party, resulting in a major shift in the political landscape. This encounter marked the party as enemies of the state, and it foreshadows greater conflict to come.
