Population

Brum'korath is a large, bustling city primarily inhabited by dwarves. The city is filled with craftsmen, warriors, and political figures who shape the city's ongoing power struggles.

Appearance

The city features large, imposing stone buildings, with towering structures and heavy stone walls. Intricate tapestries are displayed throughout the city, depicting ancient history and the city’s significance in past conflicts.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere in Brum'korath is tense, filled with political unrest and uncertainty. While there is a festive energy in certain quarters, a constant undercurrent of unease pervades, especially among outsiders and visitors.

Architecture

The city's architecture features gilded, towering structures with high arches and stone pillars. The stone halls are grand and warm, with large hearths to fend off the cold mountain air.

Symbolism & Carvings

The city is adorned with tapestries that depict important historical events. These tapestries often include dwarven symbols and carvings that reflect Brum'korath’s role in past wars and its cultural heritage.

Defenses

Brum'korath is heavily guarded, with strong military presence, constant surveillance, and fortified stone walls. There is a focus on ensuring security and control within the city, especially as political tensions rise.

Legends & Lore

Brum'korath is renowned for its political intrigue. It is a city where the past and future collide, as ancient legends intertwine with modern-day power struggles. The city holds great significance in regional politics.

Hidden Dangers

The city harbors deep mistrust of outsiders, with lingering tensions from past events like the Disciple's attack. The political maneuvering here is dangerous, and powerful figures control many aspects of the city’s security.

Link to the Party

The party is deeply connected to Brum'korath’s political landscape. They are involved in Goldar’s trial, a pivotal event that determines the course of their freedom. The party has faced imprisonment here but was granted conditional release, though under constant surveillance.

Surrounding Terrain

Brum'korath is an urban center, surrounded by harsh mountain terrain. The city-state is nestled in a mountainous region, giving it natural defenses and isolating it from surrounding areas.

Weather

The weather in Brum'korath is typically cooler, with heavy, stone-laden air. The atmosphere often reflects the political tensions that hang over the city: thick with uncertainty, but with occasional moments of clarity and calm.

Recent Developments

Brum'korath has recently hosted Goldar's trial, which led to conditional freedom for the party. However, their movements are now under closer scrutiny, and the political dynamics surrounding their actions in the city remain uncertain.
