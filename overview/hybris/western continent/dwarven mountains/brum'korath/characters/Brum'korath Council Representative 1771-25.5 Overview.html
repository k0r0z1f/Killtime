Birth Year

The birth year of the Brum'korath Council Representative is unknown, as little personal history is shared, and his focus is primarily on his duties within the council.

Role/Background

The Brum'korath Council Representative is a stern, politically involved dwarf, tasked with representing the council in dealings with external parties. His primary role is to offer support to Lucas and his companions, ensuring the necessary resources and assistance for their investigation. His responsibilities also include facilitating important diplomatic meetings, such as the one with the elves, as well as supporting the broader goals of the Brum'korath city-state.

Personality Traits

The Council Representative has a number of defining personality traits:

  • Stern: His no-nonsense attitude makes him a serious figure in the political landscape of Brum'korath.
  • Firm: He stands by his decisions with conviction, reflecting his role in the council.
  • Urgent: Due to the nature of his political environment, he is often in a rush to resolve issues quickly and efficiently.
  • Respectful but with apprehension: While he shows respect for others, there is an underlying apprehension that colors his interactions, especially in tense situations.
  • Steady and Calm: He remains composed under pressure, often balancing the urgent nature of political dealings with a calm, steady demeanor.
  • Somewhat uncomfortable with tension: While he can handle pressure, there are times when the rising tension makes him uneasy.

Abilities/Magic

The Brum'korath Council Representative does not possess any magical abilities. His strength lies in his political acumen, knowledge of Brum'korath's internal workings, and his ability to navigate complex social dynamics.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

As a representative of the Brum'korath Council, his main alliance is with the Dwarven Council. His role involves representing their interests and ensuring that Brum'korath maintains its political influence within the region.

Appearance

The Council Representative has a stern, imposing presence. He has a long, braided beard, a symbol of his dwarf heritage, and his posture reflects his leadership role. Though his appearance is unassuming, his aura commands respect, and he is a figure that exudes authority and confidence.

Character Arc/Goal

Throughout the story, the Brum'korath Council Representative's goal is to assist the party in their investigation, helping them secure the resources necessary for their mission. He plays a crucial role in facilitating a meeting with the elves and supporting Lucas’s efforts. His larger role is to act as a political figure who can make things happen behind the scenes, all while navigating the ever-present tension of his responsibilities.

Secret
