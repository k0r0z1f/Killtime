Birth Year

The Militia Captain was born in 1681, a seasoned veteran of many conflicts, having seen decades of service in the defense of Brum'korath.

Role/Background

The Militia Captain is a high-ranking figure in the Brum'korath militia, charged with maintaining order and justice within the city. His role includes overseeing the arrest and interrogation of outsiders, especially those that pose a potential threat to the city's security, such as the party members.

Personality Traits

The Militia Captain is a stern and authoritative figure, known for his distrustful nature towards outsiders. His loyalty to Brum'korath and its traditions is unwavering, and he views his duty as paramount. He is resolute and uncompromising, often unwilling to entertain dissent or challenge to his orders.

Abilities/Magic

The Militia Captain possesses strong martial skills, particularly with the axe, a weapon he wields with great precision. He relies on his physical prowess and training to maintain authority and execute justice.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

As a captain in the Dwarven militia of Brum'korath, his primary allegiance is to the city and its people. He follows the orders of his superiors without question and ensures the militia remains loyal to the city’s traditions and laws.

Appearance

The Militia Captain is a male dwarf, stout and muscular, with a strong presence. His braided beard adds to his imposing appearance, and he is usually seen wearing steel armor, signifying his high rank within the militia. His overall demeanor is one of strength and authority.

Character Arc/Goal

Throughout the story, the Militia Captain's goal is to uphold justice for the city of Brum'korath. His primary objective is to bring the party to trial, ensuring that they face the consequences of their actions. His unwavering dedication to the city's laws and traditions shapes his actions throughout the narrative.

Secret

Currently, there is no known secret about the Militia Captain. His loyalty and sense of justice are clear, and there are no hidden motivations revealed so far.

Combat Training

The Militia Captain is highly skilled in axe combat, having undergone extensive training to serve as a leader and warrior. His martial prowess makes him a formidable opponent on the battlefield.

Inventory

The Militia Captain carries an ornate axe, a symbol of his rank and combat proficiency. He also wears sturdy armor, designed for both protection and intimidation. In addition, he possesses standard militia equipment to aid in his duties as a captain of the city’s forces.
