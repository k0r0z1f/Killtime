Population

The party is currently residing within *The Apartments*, where they are the sole occupants under observation.

Appearance

The apartments are luxurious, featuring marble floors, intricate tapestries hanging from the walls, and an overall atmosphere of opulence. Despite the lavish surroundings, there is an underlying tension that pervades the space, making it feel almost too perfect.

Energy & Atmosphere

The air feels tense, as if the party is constantly under the watchful eyes of unseen observers. While the apartments are comfortable and furnished with fine materials, the oppressive atmosphere makes it difficult for the party to fully relax or feel at ease.

Architecture

The architecture is ornate, blending grand marble features with gilded accents. The design showcases an air of wealth and power, with every detail crafted to display the significance of the occupants and their influence within Brum'korath.

Symbolism & Carvings

Throughout the apartments, there are subtle carvings and artwork depicting the ancient history of Brum'korath. These symbols hint at the city's long-standing traditions, its might, and the power structures that have governed it for centuries.

Defenses

The apartments are heavily guarded by constant surveillance, ensuring that no one enters or leaves without being watched. Security measures are subtle, yet thorough, contributing to the feeling that the party is never truly alone.

Legends & Lore

The apartments are new accommodations for the party, granted after their release from imprisonment. The historical significance of these quarters is tied to the city’s complex political system and the role the party is meant to play within it.

Hidden Dangers

Despite the seemingly secure and lavish nature of the apartments, the true danger lies in the heavy scrutiny and surveillance they are under. The party’s every move is observed, creating a false sense of security that may be used against them.

Link to the Party

The party’s presence in the apartments serves as a temporary shelter. However, their movements and interactions are closely monitored, as they are now considered "honored guests" but also subjects of political maneuvering.

Surrounding Terrain

The apartments are situated in the heart of Brum'korath, surrounded by the bustling city with its tall structures and labyrinthine streets. The environment reflects the city’s importance and its strategic position within the region.

Weather

The weather in Brum'korath is unpredictable and often harsh. The city is located in a region with frequent rainstorms and sudden shifts in temperature, which adds to the tension and unease of the city’s atmosphere.

Recent Developments

After their release from imprisonment, the party has been granted temporary shelter within *The Apartments*, but their every move is monitored. This recent change marks a new phase of their journey, as they try to navigate the complex political environment of Brum'korath.
