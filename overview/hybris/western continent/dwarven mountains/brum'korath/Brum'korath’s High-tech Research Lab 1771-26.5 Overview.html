Population

The research lab is populated by a small, select group of highly trained researchers and military personnel. These individuals are focused on their work with the relics and technology, with a deep commitment to uncovering ancient knowledge.

Appearance

The lab is designed with sleek, reflective metal walls, advanced technological interfaces, and holographic displays. Its sterile, high-tech environment is illuminated by glowing panels, giving it a futuristic and slightly ominous ambiance. The sterile atmosphere is punctuated by the hum of machinery and constant data flow.

Energy & Atmosphere

The lab is charged with energy, and the atmosphere is thick with static. It hums with an underlying force that seems to affect everyone within. A constant low-level buzz fills the air, making the atmosphere feel tense and volatile, as if something could spark at any moment.

Architecture

The architecture is ultra-modern and high-tech, combining advanced magi-tech with futuristic design elements. Metallic surfaces, glass walls, and glowing panels dominate the space, creating a sleek, almost clinical environment. Everything is designed to enhance the lab's efficiency and technological prowess.

Symbolism & Carvings

While the lab itself doesn’t feature traditional symbols, there are various high-tech, arcane interfaces that act as a symbolic representation of knowledge and power. These glowing panels and devices often display cryptic data related to the experiments taking place, with an eerie sense of purpose.

Defenses

The lab is equipped with state-of-the-art security systems, including automated defense drones, energy shields, and high-tech monitoring systems. The personnel are highly trained in handling dangerous experiments, and the entire facility is fortified against external threats.

Legends & Lore

As a cutting-edge research facility, the lab is tied to ongoing experiments aimed at unlocking the potential of the relics. While there are no ancient legends specifically tied to the lab, the work being done here is shrouded in mystery, and some whisper of hidden knowledge that could change the course of history.

Hidden Dangers

The lab holds various magi-tech dangers, some of which are unknown even to the researchers. Unstable relics and experimental technologies are housed within its walls, and there are rumors of dangerous side effects from the experiments, ranging from volatile energy surges to potential breaches in reality.

Link to the Party

The lab plays a pivotal role in the party's investigation of the relics. Lucas, in particular, becomes deeply involved in analyzing the relics and exploring their potential, often triggering dangerous reactions. His discoveries lead to dangerous encounters and revelations about the power of the relics and their connection to the Sent Ones.

Surrounding Terrain

The lab is located in a highly controlled zone within Brum'korath, surrounded by various security perimeters. The terrain surrounding the lab is relatively flat and urbanized, with few natural landmarks, making it easy to secure but difficult to escape if needed.

Weather

The lab is indoors, with artificial lighting and climate control maintaining a consistent environment. The weather outside is irrelevant to the lab's internal operations, which are entirely self-contained, though the surrounding area experiences the usual industrial smog and unpredictable weather patterns.

Recent Developments

Recently, Lucas's relic has begun to activate, revealing its dangerous potential. This has caused a series of unexpected events, as the lab’s experiments push the boundaries of both technology and arcane forces. The research on the relics has taken a darker turn, with new, potentially catastrophic discoveries being made.
