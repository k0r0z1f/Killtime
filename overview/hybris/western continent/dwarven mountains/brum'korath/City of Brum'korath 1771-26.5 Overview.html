Population

Brum'korath is a bustling city populated mostly by dwarves, with a population of around 1 million. It is the center of political and military power in the region, with many influential dwarven families and guilds residing here. The city is a hub of commerce, politics, and strategy.

Appearance

The city is a monumental feat of stone craftsmanship, with towering buildings made from durable stone and steel. The streets are lined with intricate tapestries depicting the city’s rich and turbulent history, some of which show signs of wear due to the ongoing conflict. The atmosphere feels both grand and oppressive.

Energy & Atmosphere

Brum'korath’s energy is tense and fraught with uncertainty. While a festive air sometimes pervades during public gatherings, there’s an undeniable undercurrent of unease. Political upheaval looms large, with the city’s rulers constantly monitoring and manipulating factions to maintain control.

Architecture

Brum'korath’s architecture is marked by towering structures with high arches and large stone pillars. Stone halls and grand chambers house the city’s noble houses and military command centers, with large hearths burning throughout. The city’s grandeur is matched only by its fortifications, built to withstand the toughest of sieges.

Symbolism & Carvings

The city is adorned with carvings and tapestries that depict significant events from its past. These include stories of war, alliances, and betrayals. Dwarven symbols are etched throughout the city, serving as reminders of their culture, power, and endurance in the face of conflict.

Defenses

The defenses of Brum'korath are legendary, with heavy stone walls, fortified gates, and constant surveillance. The city’s defenses have been tested during recent wars, and parts of the fortifications have been damaged. However, the dwarves have a strong military presence, and any threat to the city is met with swift retribution.

Legends & Lore

Brum'korath is steeped in legends, many of which concern the city’s strategic role in past wars. The city has always been a beacon of strength and resistance, and many of the region’s great leaders emerged from its halls. Its location has made it a key player in the politics of the region.

Hidden Dangers

Despite its grandeur, Brum'korath harbors many hidden dangers. Political intrigue runs rampant, and distrust of outsiders is common. The city has been torn by conflicts, and many scars remain from the wars that ravaged it. The elites pull the strings, often using the party as pawns in their power struggles.

Link to the Party

Brum'korath is where the party was held during Goldar’s trial. It remains a place of both respect and caution, with the party under close surveillance. Their parole conditions reflect the ongoing distrust the city’s rulers have toward them, though they are allowed to operate under strict scrutiny.

Surrounding Terrain

The city is surrounded by mountains, making it an ideal location for defense. The terrain is rocky, with ruins scattered around the outskirts of the city. Its location makes it a city-state surrounded by strategic high ground and fortified positions.

Weather

The weather around Brum'korath is typically cool and overcast, with thick clouds hanging over the city due to the constant political tension. The city’s stone-heavy environment adds to the cold, oppressive feel of the area.

Recent Developments

Recent developments have shifted the political landscape of Brum'korath. The trial of Goldar marked a significant turning point, and the party’s conditional freedom reflects the tense balance of power in the city. Surveillance has increased, and the city is now a more dangerous place to navigate as political forces vie for control.
