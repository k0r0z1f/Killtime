Population

The council is composed of 10–15 members, including prominent figures such as Elders Aria and Gorla. Each member brings centuries of experience, representing the pinnacle of dwarven leadership and wisdom.

Appearance

The chamber is grand, with intricate carvings of legendary dwarven judgments, battles, and lawgivers. The domed ceiling glimmers faintly with veins of gold and silver, evoking an ethereal and ancient beauty.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere is tense, foreboding, and charged with suspicion. The weight of centuries of governance is palpable, shifting from calm deliberation to alarm following recent revelations.

Architecture

The architecture showcases dwarven mastery, with towering columns, intricate stonework, and a dominating circular table. The dome above is a work of art, glowing with veins of precious metals.

Symbolism & Carvings

The carvings on the walls depict monumental battles, legendary judgments, and ancient dwarven lawgivers, emphasizing the chamber's role as a seat of justice and power.

Defenses

The chamber is heavily guarded, with potential enchantments and barriers to ensure security during critical meetings.

Legends & Lore

The chamber’s walls and carvings embody its rich history, including major decisions that shaped dwarven civilization. Elder Aria, in particular, is tied to tales of profound wisdom and leadership.

Hidden Dangers

Political tension and hidden agendas among council members pose significant risks. External threats, such as interference from other factions, add to the chamber’s vulnerabilities.

Link to the Party

The party stands at the center of the trial, accused of actions tied to the Disciple’s destruction. The judgment they face here could reshape their path and the city's future.

Surrounding Terrain

The chamber is located deep within Brum’korath, surrounded by sprawling mines and towering mountain peaks. The city itself is a dwarven stronghold, blending beauty and impenetrability.

Weather

The atmosphere inside mirrors the trial’s gravity, while outside, cold winds and the shadow of recent destruction create a somber mood.

Recent Developments

Political instability, the destruction caused by the Disciple, and the Empire’s growing influence all culminate in the party’s trial, making this chamber the focal point of unfolding events.
