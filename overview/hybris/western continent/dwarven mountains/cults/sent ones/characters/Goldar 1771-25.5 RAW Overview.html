Birth Year

The exact birth year of Goldar is unknown, but he was born into a position of leadership among the Sent Ones, and played a crucial role before his fall from grace.

Role/Background

Goldar was once the revered leader of the Sent Ones, a powerful and influential faction. However, after his downfall and subsequent arrest following a significant battle, he is now imprisoned. His downfall came after a trial that challenged his authority, and he now seeks to redeem himself and protect the world from the aftermath of his cult's influence.

Personality Traits

Goldar's personality is marked by a calm and deliberate demeanor, though this is tinged with an underlying tension, especially post-trial. He is authoritative, often keeping secrets and maintaining an air of mystery. Despite his fall from grace, his sense of responsibility and leadership still defines him, and he seeks redemption for the mistakes he made while leading the Sent Ones.

Abilities/Magic

Goldar does not possess magical abilities in the traditional sense, but his power lies in his experience as a tactician and leader. He has a deep understanding of strategy, manipulation, and leadership.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Goldar was the leader of the Sent Ones before their downfall. While he no longer holds official sway, his connections to the faction are still significant, and he may find ways to influence them from within his prison. He is also indirectly tied to the prophecy that he now seeks to fulfill.

Appearance

Once adorned in ceremonial robes as the leader of the Sent Ones, Goldar is now stripped of his status, wearing a plain tunic and shackled at the wrists. His current appearance reflects both his fall from power and his current imprisonment, yet there is a gravity to him that suggests his former role and the significance of his actions.

Character Arc/Goal

Goldar's character arc centers around his quest for redemption and his desire to protect the world from the fallout of his past actions. He aims to fulfill the prophecy that involves the use of sacred artifacts, even as he grapples with the legacy of his previous leadership. His story is one of seeking personal growth and confronting the consequences of his past decisions.

Secret

Goldar harbors deep secrets regarding his former leadership of the Sent Ones. He may have misjudged key figures within the cult and kept much vital knowledge to himself, which may have led to the downfall of his faction. His secretive nature, combined with his knowledge of the artifacts and prophecy, could hold the key to his redemption.

Combat Training

Goldar's combat abilities are largely tied to his leadership and tactical experience rather than personal martial prowess. His knowledge of battle strategies and manipulation made him a formidable leader and tactician during his time as the leader of the Sent Ones.
