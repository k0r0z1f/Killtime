Birth Year

Goldar was born in 1381, a time of great upheaval for the world, and his life has spanned many generations of political and spiritual conflict.

Role/Background

Once a powerful leader of the Cult of the Sent Ones, Goldar now finds himself imprisoned following a great battle. His influence over the cult is weakening, and many of his followers are questioning his decisions and leadership. Despite this, Goldar still harbors grand plans involving Lucas and Mina, although his ultimate goals remain shrouded in mystery.

Personality Traits

Goldar's personality is marked by a number of commanding traits, yet tinged with uncertainty in his current state:

  • He is commanding and authoritative, having led the Cult of the Sent Ones with an iron fist before his imprisonment.
  • Goldar is insightful, with deep spiritual knowledge and understanding of the world’s ancient forces.
  • Though often calm and deliberate in his decisions, there is an underlying tension in his demeanor, especially following his trial and fall from power.
  • He remains secretive, withholding much from his followers, even as his leadership begins to crumble.

Abilities/Magic

Goldar possesses spiritual influence and has access to ancient knowledge passed down through the ages, particularly related to the Cult of the Sent Ones. These abilities allow him to guide and manipulate spiritual forces, though much of his power is diminished since his fall from grace.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Goldar remains tied to the Cult of the Sent Ones, although his once-commanding position has been undermined by recent events. His influence is now limited, but the cult’s loyalty to him is still significant, though fractured.

Appearance

Goldar is a male dwarf with a tall, imposing presence. His long, white beard and intricate robes of gold and black once marked him as a leader of great stature. However, after his fall from power, he now wears a plain tunic, his ceremonial robes stripped away. His shackled hands reflect his current state of imprisonment and helplessness.

Character Arc/Goal

Goldar’s arc is one of falling from power and attempting to redeem his failures. As his leadership over the Cult of the Sent Ones weakens, he faces the challenge of maintaining control and achieving his long-term goals. His survival depends on the manipulation of Lucas and Mina’s powers and the use of ancient artifacts, though the full extent of his plans is unclear. Ultimately, Goldar seeks to fulfill the prophecy and protect the world from a greater threat, though his methods and the price of success remain uncertain.

Secret

Goldar harbors a deep secret: his long-term plans may involve not only survival against the Empire but also the manipulation of Lucas and Mina to further the Cult’s purposes. He may have misjudged key members of his cult and has kept much knowledge to himself, unsure of who he can trust in his current situation.

Combat Training

Details of Goldar’s combat training remain unclear. While he once commanded vast forces, his current state of imprisonment means he is unlikely to have the same physical prowess. His strength lies in his spiritual knowledge and manipulative tactics, rather than in physical combat.

Inventory

Shackles - A symbol of his imprisonment.
Ancient scrolls or artifacts - These items may contain crucial knowledge regarding the prophecy and the powers of Lucas and Mina.
