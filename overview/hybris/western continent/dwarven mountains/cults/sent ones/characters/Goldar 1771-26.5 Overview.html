Birth Year

Goldar was born in the year 1381.

Role/Background

Goldar is the leader of the Sent Ones, now liberated under parole. His trial is ongoing in Brum'korath as he faces the consequences of his actions and the responsibilities of his leadership.

Personality Traits

Goldar is a calm and cold leader, authoritative and insightful. He is unwavering in his actions but carries an underlying tension following the trial, with a secretive nature that hints at deeper motives.

Abilities/Magic

Goldar possesses spiritual influence and access to ancient knowledge, making him a formidable leader of the Sent Ones with deep insight into the world's secrets.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Goldar is aligned with the Sent Ones, guiding their actions and securing their place in the larger conflict unfolding around him.

Appearance

Goldar is a tall and imposing male dwarf with a long white beard and intricate robes of gold and black. Despite his commanding presence, he now carries a diminished appearance due to the ongoing trial and internal struggles.

Character Arc/Goal

Goldar’s arc revolves around surviving under supervision while plotting his next moves. His long-term plans for Lucas and Mina remain shrouded in mystery. As his leadership faces growing challenges, he seeks to redeem his failures, protect the world, and fulfill the prophecy through the use of the ancient artifacts.

Secret

Goldar may be secretly plotting not just for survival against the Empire, but also for manipulating Lucas and Mina’s powers for the Cult's larger purposes. He has kept much of his knowledge hidden, possibly misjudging some members of his cult.
