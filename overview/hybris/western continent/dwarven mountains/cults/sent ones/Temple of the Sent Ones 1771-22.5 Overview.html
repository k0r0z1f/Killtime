Defenses The temple is not just a physical structure; it feels as though it has a magical or spiritual defense. Secret Chambers/Rooms Hidden chambers within the temple, possibly filled with ancient knowledge. There may also be passages that lead to forgotten areas or areas that have not been accessed for centuries. The secrets of the Sent Ones are growing more tangled with Lucas’s fate.

Hidden Dangers Possible malevolent entities or traps designed to protect the secrets of the temple. These could range from physical monsters to magical wards. Now, it seems the temple may also be harboring enemies from within the Cult itself.
