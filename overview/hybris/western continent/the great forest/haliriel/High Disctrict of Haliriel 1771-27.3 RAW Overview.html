Population

The population is unknown, as it remains hidden in the delicate and peaceful district. It is likely made up of the Nythari people, deeply connected to the surrounding magical environment.

Appearance

The High District of Haliriel features delicate wooden architecture, with the soft glow of Serran trees illuminating the towering structures. Walkways in the treetops, shimmering pathways, and polished wooden streets create an ethereal and serene environment. The throne room is bathed in emerald-tinted lantern light, with blooming vines framing the area.

Energy & Atmosphere

The energy in this district hums with quiet magic, creating an ethereal glow. The air is cool and refreshing, carrying the scent of ancient wood and blooming vines. The atmosphere is both serene and bustling, as the people move quietly through their sacred, yet busy, spaces.

Architecture

The High District is an elegant blend of natural materials, with wood-based structures entwined with the surrounding nature. Timber walls, cedar floors, and oak beams create an intricate yet harmonious design. The throne room and chambers are crafted from living wood, with a rare use of stone in the architecture.

Symbolism & Carvings

Intricate carvings and engravings, often depicting symbols familiar to the Nythari people, are etched throughout the district. These symbols are steeped in ancient meaning, invoking a sense of wisdom and connection to the land.

Defenses

Guards stand at the gates, and a special pass is required for entry into the district. These measures ensure the security and secrecy of the area, guarding it from unwanted outside influence.

Legends & Lore

The High District is deeply tied to the Nythari tribe’s history and their connection to the land. The Watcher, part of ancient lore and prophecy, is a key figure in the district’s legends. It is said to be a place of great wisdom and spiritual significance, ruled by the Queen of Elves.

Hidden Dangers

While the district appears peaceful, there may be political tensions brewing or unseen factions lurking in the background, potentially leading to unseen dangers or power struggles.

Link to the Party

The party’s connection to the district grows when Candice and Jarek are granted special permission to stay. Candice's special pass provides them entry, and her quest to seek guidance from the Queen and the Watcher unfolds.

Surrounding Terrain

The High District is surrounded by lush, magical Serran trees and ancient forests. The environment blends nature with urban development, creating a serene yet vibrant atmosphere filled with the sounds of nature.

Weather

The weather is typically cool, with soft glowing light from the surrounding trees. The air smells faintly of pine and earth, with a refreshing evening breeze that adds to the district's tranquil ambiance.

Recent Developments

Recently, the Elven Council convened in the district, granting permission for Candice and her group to stay. Plans are also underway for a journey to the Watcher, a place where the true nature of an ancient prophecy may be revealed.
