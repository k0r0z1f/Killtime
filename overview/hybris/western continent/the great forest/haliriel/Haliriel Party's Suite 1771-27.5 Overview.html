There is a prophecy-related link to the Children of Hybris, and the location is connected to the Watcher.

The party is currently stationed here and preparing for the upcoming mission related to the Disciple of Air. Candice has revealed her true mission, which has influenced the decision to take a detour to the Watcher.

The surrounding terrain includes the Great Forest, with towering trees and distant towers visible in the distance.

The weather is characterized by pine-scented air and warm light emanating from the crystal heater.
