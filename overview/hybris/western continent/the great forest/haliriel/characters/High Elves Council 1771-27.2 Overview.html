Role/Background

The High Elves Council is a powerful governing body of elves who oversee the High Temple of Nature and the fate of Haliriel. The council consists of three members: the Elder Elf, the Regal Female Elf (Queen of Elves), and the Soft Yet Powerful Elf. They play an essential role in guiding the fate of the Elven realm and Haliriel itself, particularly in relation to prophecy and magic.

Personality Traits

The Elder Elf exudes wisdom and experience, always thinking carefully before making decisions. The Regal Female Elf is stern and authoritative, commanding respect and obedience from others. The Soft Yet Powerful Elf is more introspective, inquisitive, and cautious, bringing balance to the council's decision-making.

Abilities/Magic

The Regal Elf is skilled in air magic, divination, and diplomacy. Their magical abilities enable them to interpret prophecies and foresee future events, as well as maintain peace and unity within the Elven realm.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The High Elves Council is part of the Elven Nation and aligned with the Queen of Elves. Their influence is vast, particularly concerning the destiny of Haliriel and its people.

Appearance

The Elder Elf has long silver hair, braided intricately, embodying both elegance and wisdom. The Queen of Elves has a crown of leaves, exuding a regal presence and commanding respect. The Soft Yet Powerful Elf has deep, soft features with a powerful presence that emanates strength in a subtle manner.

Character Arc/Goal

The High Elves Council's primary goals are to preserve the sovereignty of the Elves, ensure the safety of Haliriel, protect the realm from external threats, and guide the Children of Hybris. They are deeply involved in the fulfillment of the prophecy that ties their fate to that of Lucas and Mina.

Secret

The High Elves Council holds secret knowledge of the prophecy, understanding its profound implications for Haliriel and its people. This secret is one of the most powerful and dangerous in the realm.
