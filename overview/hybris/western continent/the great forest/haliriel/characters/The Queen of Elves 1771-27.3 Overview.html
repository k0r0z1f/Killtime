Birth Year

Born in 929, the Queen of Elves is 842 years old in the current year of 1771. Her long lifespan is a testament to the ancient power and grace of her people.

Role/Background

The Queen of Elves, ruler of Haliriel, is the leader of the High Elves Council and commands the loyalty of her people. She is responsible for maintaining peace and prosperity within the Elven Realm, often balancing diplomacy and strength.

Personality Traits

Regal and commanding, the Queen is both a wise and diplomatic leader, fiercely protective of her people. Her calm demeanor hides a calculated mind and cryptic nature, always thinking several steps ahead.

Abilities/Magic

Mastery over Air Magic allows the Queen to heal, control plant life, enhance her senses, and communicate with nature. She also possesses ancient arcane knowledge, making her a formidable and wise leader.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Queen is deeply intertwined with Haliriel and the Elven Realm, leading her people and forming key alliances with other factions. She is also involved in ancient prophecies that shape the future of her world.

Appearance

The Queen is tall and regal, with long silver hair intricately braided and adorned with a crown of leaves and jewels. Her circlet is made of entwined leaves and gemstones, symbolizing her connection to the natural world. She wears a robe woven from the essence of the forest, and her piercing green eyes convey both strength and wisdom.

Character Arc/Goal

The Queen's goal is to lead her people through times of political tension, ensuring the prosperity of the Elven realm. She also plays a crucial role in guiding key individuals, including Lucas, Mina, and Candice, towards uncovering the deeper truths of a looming prophecy.

Secret

Unknown to many, the Queen harbors secret knowledge of an ancient prophecy, and she has made hidden alliances to secure the future of her people. Her actions are often shrouded in mystery, as she deceives even those closest to her for the greater good.

Combat Training

Highly trained in elven warfare, archery, and both defensive and offensive magic, the Queen is a formidable opponent in battle. She is as skilled with a bow as she is with her magical abilities, making her a leader in both peace and war.

Inventory

The Queen carries elaborate ceremonial robes, an enchanted staff that enhances her magical abilities, a collection of ancient elven relics, and a small pouch of rare healing herbs used for both magical and physical restoration.
