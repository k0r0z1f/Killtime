Birth Year

Role/Background

Aranel is the lead researcher at a magi-tech laboratory, deeply involved in the study of arcane artifacts and magical technologies. Her work involves understanding the fusion of magic and technology to uncover lost knowledge and enhance current capabilities.

Personality Traits

Aranel is known for her calm and professional demeanor. She is analytical, respectful, and always focused on the task at hand. She maintains a sense of calm even in stressful situations, which helps her in her role as a researcher.

Abilities/Magic

Aranel has extensive knowledge of magi-tech and specializes in the study of arcane artifacts. Her deep understanding of both ancient magic and technology allows her to bridge the gap between the two, often uncovering secrets from relics and devices that others cannot comprehend.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Aranel is affiliated with the Elven faction, known for their long-lived wisdom and connection to arcane energies. Her role as a researcher places her in contact with many scholars, but her primary allegiance lies with her elven heritage.

Appearance

Aranel is tall and possesses sharp, elegant features. Her piercing blue eyes are often the first thing others notice about her, reflecting a deep, analytical mind. She carries herself with grace and confidence, and her appearance reflects the refinement of her elven heritage.

Character Arc/Goal

Aranel's primary goal is to study and understand the relics that Lucas carries. She believes these artifacts could hold the key to unlocking new magical technologies or ancient powers that could change the course of history. Her research is driven by a deep curiosity and a sense of responsibility to protect the knowledge she uncovers.
