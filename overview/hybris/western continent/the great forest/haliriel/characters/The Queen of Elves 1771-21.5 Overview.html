Birth Year

The Queen of Elves was born in 929, making her over 800 years old and one of the longest-living individuals in Hybris.

Role/Background

As the ruler of Haliriel and the leader of the elves across the world of Hybris, the Queen holds significant influence, ensuring the prosperity and safety of her people. She is a well-respected figure in elven society and beyond, with a long lineage of leadership and wisdom.

Personality Traits

The Queen possesses a commanding presence, characterized by her regal bearing and authoritative demeanor. She is deeply wise, diplomatic, and always seeks to protect her people while navigating the complexities of elven politics. Her leadership is driven by a strong sense of duty to ensure the survival and prosperity of the elves.

Abilities/Magic

The Queen's abilities are deeply tied to nature. She can control plant life, heal with nature-based magic, and enhance her senses to perceive the world in extraordinary detail. She also possesses knowledge of ancient elven arcane magic, passed down through generations, which she uses sparingly due to its dangerous potential.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

She is closely allied with the Elven council and other High Elves across Hybris, guiding their actions and policies with her wisdom. The Queen is also seen as a central figure in the broader balance of power within the world, especially with the growing political tensions.

Appearance

The Queen is a striking figure, adorned with a crown made from leaves and jewels. She has long, silver hair that flows elegantly and green eyes that reflect her deep connection to nature. Her regal presence commands respect, and she carries herself with a grace that befits her station.

Character Arc/Goal

Her main goal is to protect her people and maintain the prosperity of Haliriel. However, she faces growing internal political strife and external threats. She must navigate these challenges while ensuring that the elves remain strong and unified, all while confronting the mystery of the prophecy and its potential consequences for her people.

Secret

There are whispers that the Queen harbors secret knowledge about the ancient prophecy and may be hiding alliances that could change the course of elven history. This secret knowledge could be the key to understanding the unfolding events across Hybris.

Combat Training

The Queen has undergone extensive training in elven warfare, mastering the use of the bow and defensive magic. She is a skilled combatant and tactician, capable of leading her people both in battle and in diplomacy. Her elven magic also enhances her ability to protect and defend her realm.

Inventory

The Queen carries elaborate ceremonial robes that signify her station, an enchanted staff that serves as both a symbol of her authority and a powerful tool for magic, and a collection of elven relics that hold both historical and magical significance. She also keeps a pouch of rare healing herbs for emergencies.
