Character

Queen of Elves, known as Aphyrosia, is the sovereign of Haliriel and the entire Elven Realm. As the leader of the High Elves Council, she embodies ancient traditions and the weight of timeless prophecies. Her identity remains mysterious to most, a secret kept even as she guides her people with firm authority.

Birth Year

Born in the year 929, Aphyrosia has witnessed over eight centuries of history, now standing at 842 years old. Her long life is a testament to the mystical nature of the elves and their enduring legacy.

Role/Background

Aphyrosia rules Haliriel and the entire Elven Realm with wisdom and strength. As the leader of the High Elves Council, she not only governs her people but also follows a mysterious group ascending the Watcher, monitoring the bond between Lucas and Mina, and intervening during critical confrontations such as that with the Minulican. Her role as a battlefield controller, adept in wind magic, complements her deep knowledge of ancient prophecies and the subtle energies that permeate her realm.

Personality Traits

Aphyrosia is both commanding and regal, exuding authority and wisdom. Her diplomatic approach, combined with a protective nature, ensures the well-being of her people. Calm, composed, and observant, she balances grace with determination and remains cryptic about her inner thoughts, fostering an air of quiet reverence.

Abilities/Magic

Aphyrosia wields a profound mastery over air magic, which she uses for healing, manipulating wind, and controlling plant life. Her abilities extend to creating stabilizing barriers, mending wounds with a golden light, and restraining formidable foes. In addition, she has intermediate command over fire, water, and earth magic, and her acute senses allow her to perceive lingering energies that others cannot.
