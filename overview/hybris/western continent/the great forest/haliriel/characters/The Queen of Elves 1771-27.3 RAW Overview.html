Role/Background

The Queen of Elves is the ruler of the Elven people, head of the High Elves Council, and the sovereign of the Elven Realm. Her leadership is deeply entwined with the fate of the Elven kind and their ancient ways.

Personality Traits

She is calm and authoritative, exuding an air of wisdom and serenity. Despite her regal bearing, she is warm and compassionate to those she deems worthy. However, her wisdom often gives way to a calculated, cryptic demeanor that keeps others guessing about her true intentions.

Abilities/Magic

The Queen possesses mastery over air magic, a gift that is deeply connected to the natural world. Her understanding of nature allows her to command elemental forces and communicate with the forces of the forest itself.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

The Queen is aligned with the High Elves, ruling over the Elven Realm. She is also involved in ancient prophecies and holds a significant role within Haliriel’s political structure, acting as a guide to other key figures in the ongoing struggles for power.

Appearance

Her appearance is dignified and regal. She has silver hair, intricately braided, adorned with a circlet made of entwined leaves and gemstones. Her robes are woven from the essence of the forest itself, shimmering with ethereal magic, and her piercing green eyes reflect the depths of the ancient world she governs.

Character Arc/Goal

The Queen's arc revolves around navigating an ancient prophecy and guiding others, especially Lucas and Mina, to fulfill their roles in the greater plan. She works to establish alliances for future challenges and plays a crucial role in testing Candice’s resolve, all while revealing hidden truths about the prophecy.

Secret

The Queen has deceived Candice, Jarek, and others to further the Elven cause. She is privy to deeper knowledge regarding the prophecy and the Watcher, a secret she keeps hidden for the greater good of her people.
