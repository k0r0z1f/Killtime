Role/Background

Altheris is an envoy of Haliriel, tasked with persuading the party to join a crucial mission to the Eastern Continent. Additionally, Altheris serves as an elven council member, representing Haliriel in high-stakes negotiations with the dwarves. Later, Altheris becomes the Commandant overseeing the Eastern Continent mission and leads the party's operations in the region.

Personality Traits

Altheris is calm and composed, exuding a commanding presence. Persuasive and diplomatic, he approaches situations with calculated precision. However, there are moments where his cold, slightly amused demeanor reveals a determined leader who can be frustrated by the reluctance of others. Tall, elegant, and purposeful, Altheris is someone who commands respect effortlessly.

Abilities/Magic

Altheris is highly skilled in high-level strategy and leadership, making him a natural at directing operations and overseeing complex missions. His understanding of tactics and diplomacy is a key asset in his role.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Altheris is an ally to the elves, particularly Haliriel, where he holds a leadership role. His alliances extend beyond his own kin, as he works to ensure successful diplomacy with other factions, such as the dwarves.

Appearance

Altheris has silver hair and pale green eyes, embodying the elegance of his elven heritage. His attire consists of an intricate tunic embroidered with vines and stars, emphasizing his connection to nature and magic. His angular features and composed demeanor make him appear both noble and distant, while his tall, elegant form is typically draped in dark elven robes.

Character Arc/Goal

Altheris’s main goal is to uncover the truth behind the Disciple of Air and the prophecy associated with it. He is determined to protect Haliriel from any threats and to ensure smooth negotiations and cooperation with Brum'korath, a significant ally. Throughout his journey, Altheris must guide the party in understanding the Disciple of Air, leading them through the mission to the Eastern Continent and ensuring their success in the process.
