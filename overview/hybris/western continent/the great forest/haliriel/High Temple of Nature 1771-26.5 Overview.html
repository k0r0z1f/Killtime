Population

The High Temple of Nature is home to the High Elves, a secluded and noble race known for their deep connection to nature and their long life spans. Visitors from various factions are allowed entry, but they are carefully selected based on their purpose.

Appearance

The temple stands tall with towering columns of living wood, each intertwined with delicate vines that bloom with soft flowers throughout the seasons. Sunlight filters through the thick canopy of ancient trees above, casting warm rays on the lush greenery below. The entire temple is an organic structure, blending seamlessly with its surroundings in a natural, serene environment.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere within the High Temple of Nature is serene, yet thick with a sense of deliberation and ancient wisdom. Every moment feels heavy with the importance of what occurs within the temple. The High Elves often gather here to make decisions that affect the course of nature itself, which gives the air an almost foreboding tension.

Architecture

The architecture of the High Temple blends seamlessly with the natural world. It is constructed from living wood, grown over centuries by the hands of skilled elven artisans, forming majestic columns and spires that appear to grow organically from the earth itself. This temple is a perfect harmony of nature and the elven race's sophisticated craftsmanship.

Symbolism & Carvings

The High Temple is rich in symbolism, with intricate carvings depicting the natural cycles of life, death, and rebirth. These motifs, intertwined with the temple’s wood and stonework, serve as a constant reminder of the High Elves’ deep respect for the forces of nature and their role in maintaining balance.

Defenses

The High Temple of Nature is protected by powerful ancient magics that have been woven into the fabric of the temple’s construction. The High Elves also maintain a dedicated group of guardians who are capable of calling upon nature’s wrath to defend the temple from intruders.

Legends & Lore

Legends of the High Temple are tied to the ancient pact between the High Elves and the forces of nature. It is said that the temple was created with the help of ancient spirits who still watch over it, guiding the High Elves’ decisions. The temple is also believed to house powerful artifacts that could alter the very balance of nature itself.

Hidden Dangers

Although serene, the High Temple of Nature is not without its hidden dangers. There are whispers of ancient, forgotten powers buried deep beneath the temple, capable of disrupting the natural order if they were ever unleashed. The High Elves are wary of these forces, but they continue to guard the secrets within.

Link to the Party

The party becomes involved in the High Elves' deliberations, as their role in the world’s balance begins to intertwine with the High Temple's goals. The decision of the High Elves regarding Candice and the party could have far-reaching consequences for both the group and the world at large.

Surrounding Terrain

The High Temple is located deep within an ancient forest, where towering trees stretch high into the sky, their roots tangled in the rich, fertile soil. The surrounding terrain is dense with life, from small creatures scurrying along the forest floor to birds singing above in the branches. The environment feels alive, as though the very land itself is watching over the temple.

Weather

The weather within the High Temple of Nature is often mild, with soft beams of sunlight filtering through the canopy above. The air is fresh, with a slight breeze carrying the scent of pine and fresh leaves. Occasionally, the weather becomes more intense, as if reflecting the emotional tone of the High Elves' deliberations—storms are rare but can be powerful when they occur.

Recent Developments

Recently, tensions have risen within the High Elves as they deliberate on the role of Candice and the party in the larger balance of the world. Some factions wish to support the party, while others are more cautious, wary of what such an alliance might mean for the natural order. The party’s actions in the coming days could influence the temple’s future decisions.
