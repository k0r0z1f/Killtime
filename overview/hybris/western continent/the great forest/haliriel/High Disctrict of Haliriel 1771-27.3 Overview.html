Population

The High District of Haliriel is home to Elves, primarily high-ranking members of Haliriel's society. The district is a place of nobility and sophistication, where the most influential and esteemed individuals of the realm reside.

Appearance

The High District is a stunning blend of nature and grandeur. Towering structures rise from the treetops, delicately crafted from wood. The glow from Serran trees bathes the district in an ethereal light, while shimmering pathways and grand chambers create a sense of serenity.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere is serene yet lively, with the gentle hum of magic filling the air. The cool evening breeze adds a refreshing feel, and there is a sacred silence, heavy with the weight of ancient knowledge and tradition.

Architecture

The architecture of the High District blends nature and elegance. Delicate wooden structures are woven into the natural landscape, with intricate timber walls, smooth cedar floors, and ancient oak beams supporting majestic buildings.

Symbolism & Carvings

Throughout the High District, intricate carvings and ancient symbols can be found, each one deeply connected to the Nythari people's history and spirituality. These symbols convey wisdom and power, reflecting the district’s deep cultural roots.

Defenses

The district is well-protected, with guards stationed at every entrance and special passes required for entry. The elite are afforded safe passage, ensuring that only those deemed worthy may access its secrets.

Legends & Lore

The High District is deeply steeped in lore, with the Watcher playing a central role in its ancient prophecies. Legends tell of a great destiny tied to the Queen of Elves, who rules the district with wisdom and authority.

Hidden Dangers

The district holds potential for political strife. While outwardly peaceful, underlying tensions brew, and the balance of power is fragile. Factions vie for influence, and the true nature of some of these conflicts remains hidden.

Link to the Party

The party finds themselves in the High District, with Candice and Jarek granted a reprieve to stay. Candice holds a special pass, allowing the group access to the Queen and her counsel. Here, they will seek guidance and uncover the truths hidden within the district.

Surrounding Terrain

The High District is surrounded by lush Serran trees, with the city built among the natural environment. The district feels as though it is one with nature, yet its wooden architecture contrasts with the ancient forests that encircle it.

Weather

The weather in the High District is mild but often cool, with soft glowing light emanating from the surrounding trees. The air is refreshing, with a hint of pine and earth, carrying the essence of ancient woods and nature’s breath.

Recent Developments

Recent developments include the Elven Council convening to discuss the arrival of the party. The Queen of Elves plans a journey to the Watcher, and the true nature of the prophecy may soon be revealed.
