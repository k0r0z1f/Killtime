Population

The city is home to 2 million inhabitants, predominantly elves living in tree districts. There is a mix of elves, humans, and occasional dwarves present in the bustling market district, reflecting the city's trade and cultural exchanges.

Appearance

Haliriel is a breathtaking city where nature and architecture seamlessly blend. Towering columns of living wood rise up from the earth, interwoven with delicate vines. The glowing Serran trees illuminate the city at night, creating a tranquil atmosphere that is both magical and serene.

Energy & Atmosphere

Haliriel carries a serene and mystical atmosphere, as if suspended in time. Yet, beneath the tranquility, there is an underlying tension, tied to the ancient prophecies and the forces stirring in the world. The air is cool and refreshing, but charged with an unsettling energy.

Architecture

The city's architecture is a masterpiece of elven design, blending the natural world with elegant craftsmanship. Organic, flowing structures made from living wood tower above, while the High District showcases grand citadels, large windows, and smooth cedar floors.

Symbolism & Carvings

Throughout Haliriel, intricate carvings and symbols etched into wood and stone tell the city's history. Nature motifs like leaves and flowers are common, representing the elves' deep reverence for nature. The High Temple of Nature contains special carvings tied to ancient prophecies.

Defenses

The city is well-protected by natural barriers, such as the surrounding forests and the Great Lake. In addition, strong defenses are in place, including magi-tech barriers, guards stationed at district entrances, and secretive, well-hidden passageways for strategic movements.

Legends & Lore

Haliriel's history is deeply intertwined with prophecies, ancient relics, and the fate of the world. The city is said to hold key secrets that could shape the future. The elves' connection to the Children of Hybris and the Nythari Tribe adds further depth to the city's ancient lore.

Hidden Dangers

There are rumors of sealed chambers beneath the city, containing powerful artifacts long forgotten. Political intrigue and the possibility of secret factions working against the city's interests add to the tension, with the prophecy casting a shadow over all.

Link to the Party

The party plays a pivotal role in uncovering the city's hidden truths. Their presence is connected to ancient prophecies, as they search for relics and navigate complex political landscapes. Their involvement in the city's future may shape the outcome of the prophecy.

Surrounding Terrain

Haliriel is surrounded by lush forests and mystical landscapes, offering both beauty and protection. The terrain is rugged and difficult to traverse, adding to the city's secrecy and its isolation from the outside world.

The city's political landscape is in flux, with growing tensions among the various factions. The prophecy is beginning to take shape, and the arrival of Candice, a key figure in the unfolding story, has added to the city's sense of uncertainty and change.
