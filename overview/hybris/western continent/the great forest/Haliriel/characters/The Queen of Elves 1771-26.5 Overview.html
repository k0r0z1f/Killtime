Birth Year

Born in 929, nearly a millennium ago, the Queen of Elves stands as a figure of immense wisdom and authority, having witnessed countless changes throughout the ages.

Role/Background

As the ruler of Haliriel, the City of Elves, the Queen governs with a deep sense of duty, blending tradition and progress. She serves as the embodiment of elven ideals, bridging the various factions within her realm and representing her people to the wider world.

Personality Traits

The Queen is commanding and regal, traits befitting her leadership. Though she is authoritative, she is also wise and diplomatic, balancing the needs of her people with the delicate intricacies of politics. Above all, she is protective of her realm and its future.

Abilities/Magic

Her magical abilities include a mastery over Air Magic, focused on nature-based powers like plant control and wind manipulation. She is also skilled in healing magic and has access to ancient arcane knowledge, making her a powerful figure within the elven world.

Alliances/Clans/Factions

Her primary allegiance lies with Haliriel, but her diplomacy extends to other realms. She may also have undisclosed connections with secretive elven factions, and her influence within these groups could be pivotal in navigating the political landscape.

Appearance

The Queen’s appearance reflects her ancient lineage. She is tall, with silver hair that symbolizes her long reign. Her piercing green eyes are both commanding and compassionate, and she wears a crown made of leaves and jewels, reflecting her deep connection to nature.

Character Arc/Goal

Her primary goal is the protection and prosperity of Haliriel, but her reign faces political challenges. Internal strife and external threats test her abilities, especially as the prophecy of the Sent Ones looms. She must navigate these threats while safeguarding her people’s future.

Secret

The Queen may know hidden aspects of the Sent Ones prophecy, including secret alliances and strategies. These could influence Haliriel's fate and her people’s role in the greater conflict unfolding across the world.

Combat Training

While the Queen rarely engages directly in battle, she is highly skilled in elven combat, particularly archery and defensive magic. She is a master strategist, relying on her forces and magic to defend her realm.

Inventory

Her ceremonial attire includes fine robes, enchanted for protection. She wields an enchanted staff, symbolizing her authority and magical prowess. Additionally, she carries elven relics and a pouch of rare healing herbs, reflecting her deep commitment to her people’s well-being.
