Population

The population of Haliriel is around 2 million, with the majority being Elven inhabitants living in tree districts. The city also hosts a mix of humans and the occasional dwarf, mostly in the bustling market district.

Appearance

Haliriel is a breathtaking sight, with towering spires and living wood structures seamlessly integrated into the surrounding natural environment. The Serran trees, which glow softly at night, add to the ethereal ambiance. Sunlight filters through leaves, casting dappled light across the delicate woodwork of the city.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere in Haliriel is serene, with a timeless, ancient feel. It is detached from the world’s turmoil, but there is a quiet tension beneath the surface, likely connected to the city’s complex politics and the prophecy that looms over it.

Architecture

The city’s architecture is a stunning blend of nature and elegance, with buildings grown directly from the land. The structures are carved from living trees and stone, and the design incorporates graceful, organic curves and crystal-like buildings that reflect the elven reverence for nature.

Symbolism & Carvings

The city’s carvings and symbols reflect the Elven connection to nature. Intricate motifs of leaves, trees, and jewels are prevalent, with special emphasis on the crown of jewels worn by the Elven Queen. These symbols embody the Elven respect for nature and their legacy of magic.

Defenses

Haliriel is protected by both natural and magical defenses. The surrounding forests and rivers act as natural barriers, and within the city, powerful arcane wards guard against intrusion. However, there are whispers of hidden political forces operating from the shadows.

Legends & Lore

As one of the most powerful cities in the Eastern Continent, Haliriel is steeped in ancient lore and magic. The city is integral to the Elven connection to the world’s natural flow, and its political and military affairs often influence broader events.

Hidden Dangers

While Haliriel appears peaceful, there are undercurrents of tension—particularly related to the prophecy that connects the city to the greater world. The Elves are resistant to outside influence, and the complexity of politics within the city could present unforeseen dangers for outsiders.

Link to the Party

The party’s visit to Haliriel is politically significant, with their destinies tied to the city. They are connected through the World Police’s jurisdiction and represent sanctuary for the group. Candice's presence has further complicated the city's role in the unfolding prophecy.

Surrounding Terrain

Haliriel is surrounded by lush forests, mystical rivers, and protected landscapes. These natural barriers offer the city secrecy and protection, keeping it hidden from external threats. The nearby verdant lands are rich in magical and natural resources.

Weather

The climate in Haliriel is mild and temperate, with occasional ethereal mist hanging in the air. The gentle breeze carries the scent of flowers and earth, and the weather seems to shift with the presence of the Elven Queen, adding to the city’s mystical atmosphere.

Recent Developments

Haliriel has remained sovereign, resisting the influence of the World Police. Recently, the capture of the Disciple of Air and the mission to the Eastern Continent have brought new tensions. Candice’s arrival marks the beginning of a new chapter for the city, with many uncertain about her connection to the prophecy.
