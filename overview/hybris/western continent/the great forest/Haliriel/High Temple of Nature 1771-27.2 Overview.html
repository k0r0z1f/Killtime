Population

The High Temple of Nature is primarily inhabited by the High Elves, a select group of visitors also attending important deliberations. This exclusive location is a site for both ritual and council.

Appearance

The High Temple's structure is formed by towering columns of living wood, with delicate vines wrapping around them, creating a natural yet refined appearance. The sunlight filters through the canopy of Serran trees, bathing the structure in a soft, golden glow.

Energy & Atmosphere

The atmosphere at the High Temple is serene, yet there is a palpable tension in the air. It is thick with the scent of blooming flowers and the hum of magic, as if the place itself is infused with fate and deliberation.

Architecture

The High Temple's architecture blends seamlessly with the natural surroundings, with elegant wooden structures that echo the surrounding forest. It stands as a testament to the Elves' reverence for nature, with its natural wood materials and delicate design.

Symbolism & Carvings

The temple is subtly adorned with natural motifs, representing the balance between nature and Elven culture. The intricate carvings are a reminder of the divine connection the Elves maintain with the world around them.

Defenses

There are few overt defenses within the temple, as it is primarily a place of peace. However, subtle magic may be woven into the structure, ensuring that only those with noble intent can enter freely.

Legends & Lore

The High Temple of Nature holds great significance in Elven culture, with stories surrounding the council and deliberations that have taken place there for centuries. It is said that the temple is a place where the fate of the Elven people is decided.

Hidden Dangers

The true dangers of the temple are not physical, but rather the political and magical forces at play within. The fate of the High Elves' people is often debated, and it is rumored that not all deliberations are as peaceful as they seem.

Link to the Party

Candice and Jarek are involved in the High Elves' council, and the party's role in these deliberations is pivotal. As the party unravels the mysteries of the High Temple, their actions could tip the balance of power.

Surrounding Terrain

The temple is surrounded by towering Serran trees and other lush natural features, creating a serene and beautiful environment. The area is teeming with life, from mystical creatures to abundant flora.

Weather

The weather in the area is temperate, with soft beams of sunlight filtering through the canopy of leaves. The atmosphere is calm and pleasant, reflecting the peaceful nature of the High Temple.

Recent Developments

The High Elves have been deliberating on the role of Candice and the party in their future, with recent revelations potentially altering the course of their ancient traditions.
